## §2 WHAT LIANA BANYAN COOPERATIVE IS

The cooperative is a member-owned platform that keeps 83.3% of every
transaction with the person who did the work. We charge Cost+20% margins.
We do not take 30% the way delivery apps do. We do not sell member data.
The members own the infrastructure.

The first application is food. We call it Let's Make Dinner. Restaurants
and home cooks publish menus at Cost+20% pricing. Members search and order.
The cook or restaurant keeps 83.3% of every transaction. The cooperative
keeps its margin. No DoorDash extraction. No advertising. No sponsored
placement.

The deeper layer is what we call the Cooperative Substrate: a persistent,
member-owned AI-memory and coordination layer that fixes the problem of
AI systems forgetting everything at the end of every conversation. The
substrate travels with the member across platforms and vendors.
Continuity-lift across vendor churn. It is the infrastructure that
makes everything else possible.

The cooperative is structured to run for fifty years, not to be sold in
five. It is not a startup seeking exit. It is a cooperative institution
being built from the ground up.

## §3 WHAT I AM ASKING

An Endorsement Crown. Here is what that means.

**A small honorary patent stake.** 0.05% of my international patent
portfolio (300+ countries under international patent treaty). This is
a meaningful acknowledgment, not a token. It vests over twelve months
with no cliff. It represents the Cooperative's commitment to you, not
the other way around.

**Your name and voice, available.** When we launch Let's Make Dinner
in your city or region, we may ask to reference your endorsement in
press materials. When a journalist asks about the cooperative food
model, we may ask if you are willing to speak to it. This is not a
contractual obligation -- it is a relationship. If you say no to a
specific request, we hear no.

**One to two hours per month, on average.** Not scheduled standing
calls. Not mandatory attendance at anything. When we need a voice
of credibility on a specific matter that falls in your lane, we ask.
You decide whether to engage. Most months, we will not ask for anything
at all.

That is the whole ask. No salary (because there is no salary). No
ongoing operational commitment. No exclusivity that would affect your
current work.
