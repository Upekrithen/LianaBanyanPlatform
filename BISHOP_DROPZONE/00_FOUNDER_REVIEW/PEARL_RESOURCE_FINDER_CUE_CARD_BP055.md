# Pearl Resource Finder™ — Cue Card
## Member-Facing Cooperative Resource Discovery Tool
**BP055 · May 24, 2026 · Knight**

---

## What It Is

Pearl Resource Finder is a member-facing tool that surfaces cooperative resources available in a member's geographic area and economic context. "Pearl" because cooperative resources are often hidden — each one a small, valuable thing formed through accumulated community effort, not obvious on the surface.

The tool answers the question every new member has but rarely knows how to ask: *"Okay, I've joined. What can actually help me right now, in my situation, in my zip code?"*

---

## What It Does

| Function | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Local Resource Discovery** | Surfaces cooperative resources within a configurable radius: credit unions, food co-ops, tool libraries, mutual aid networks, cooperative employers, community health resources |
| **Initiative Matching** | Maps the member's stated needs to the relevant Sweet Sixteen Initiative (MSA for healthcare, Let's Get Groceries for food, VSL for short-term cash needs, etc.) |
| **Referral Routing** | Routes the member to the correct onboarding path for each resource (Cephas article, external cooperative link, platform feature) |
| **Gap Flagging** | When a resource category has no local cooperative equivalent, Pearl flags it as a gap — contributing to the cooperative's understanding of where new initiatives are most needed |

---

## How to Use It

1. **Member opens Pearl Resource Finder** from the Mnemosyne left-nav ribbon (future) or via the platform dashboard
2. **Member selects their primary need** from a simple category list: Food / Health / Childcare / Housing / Financial / Legal / Transportation / Other
3. **Member confirms their location** (zip code or city — no precise address required)
4. **Pearl returns a results card**: cooperative resources nearby + platform initiatives that apply + a direct "join this" or "learn more" CTA
5. **Member can save results** to their Phoebe™ Idea Storage for later reference

---

## Cooperative Economics Tied to It

Pearl Resource Finder is not just a directory — it is the onboarding mechanism for cooperative surplus creation. When a member finds and joins a cooperative resource through Pearl:

- The member saves money or gains access to a service they couldn't afford alone (direct member benefit)
- The cooperative resource gains a new participant (network compounding)
- The gap data Pearl collects feeds platform planning for which Sweet Sixteen Initiative to incubate next (substrate intelligence)
- Every successful referral is a Banyan Metric row — a unit of cooperative economic infrastructure installed

**The platform keeps 83.3% logic applies to Pearl indirectly**: every member Pearl helps retain is a member generating activity at 83.3% creator payout. Pearl increases retention by proving value immediately, which increases creator economics, which increases platform surplus, which funds more cooperative infrastructure.
