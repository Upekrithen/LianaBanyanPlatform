- Members retain unconditional right to use the substrate via any other vendor or via local inference
- Vendor's Branded Fork shall not technically or contractually impede member portability
- Vendor shall not condition any commercial offering on member-substrate exclusivity

This covenant runs perpetually and survives any other termination.

### 4.2 Pledge Compliance

Vendor signs Pledge #2260 as a condition precedent to this agreement. Vendor shall not pursue patent litigation against any other Pledge signer regarding substrate-architecture innovations covered by the Pledge.

Vendor shall not represent the Branded Fork as the sole source of substrate-grounded retrieval. Public materials shall acknowledge: *"Powered by Liana Banyan Cathedral architecture. Substrate available via any vendor."* (or equivalent counsel-approved language).
