**Measurement.** Three empirical metrics, cooperative-class peer-witness real: (a) gross sales at Cost+20%, (b) operator take-home vs typical food-truck day, (c) QR enrollments. No vanity metrics. Variance band published Honest-Alpha.

**Why this matters.** The Greek experiment is the smallest possible legible proof that Anglerfish (Profit-Armada extractive vendor) and Krill (cooperative-class operator) produce different lived outcomes for the same labor. ## SAGA 13.13 — Cooperative-Class Peer-Class Peer-Witness Testimonials Harvest

**Discipline supreme.** **Never paraphrase the Founder.** Never paraphrase any peer-witness member. Quote in full, in their words, or do not quote at all. This applies to testimonials *more strictly* than to any other artifact in the LB corpus.

**Harvest mechanics:**
- Testimonials are *given*, never *solicited with prompts*. Members write their own, in their own words, on their own initiative.
- Bishop attests the testimonial is real (peer-witness real → coroner ratify).
- Member retains full edit rights, including deletion, in perpetuity.
- LB publishes testimonials with member's full chosen attribution (real name, handle, or anonymous-with-attestation).
- No A/B testing of testimonial copy. No testimonial templates. No "tell us about your experience" forms.

**What we do not publish:**
- Founder-drafted testimonials attributed to others (zero tolerance).
- Edited-for-marketing testimonials.
- Composite or "representative" testimonials.
- Testimonials from members who later asked for deletion (purged within 24h, peer-witnessed).

**Why this discipline matters.** Profit-Armada-class platforms manufacture testimonials industrially. LB's structural difference is empirical: the testimonials we publish are the testimonials members wrote, full stop. If we have 3 testimonials in Year 1, we publish 3. If we have 30, we publish 30. **"may earn" never "may earn."**

**Deliverable:** testimonial harvest log (peer-witness real attestations) + deletion-request audit trail.
