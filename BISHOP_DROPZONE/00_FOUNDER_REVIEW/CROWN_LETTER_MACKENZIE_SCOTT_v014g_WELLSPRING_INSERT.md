## Proposed new paragraph (insert between v014f lines 32 and 34)

The Keystone canonical text is *"A rising tide lifts all boats. And I think I've built a system of wells."* Bishop added two extending clauses:

1. **"but not evenly, and never reliably for the people furthest from the shore"** — acknowledges that the rising-tide claim has been falsified in practice for exactly the population Scott funds. This is the pivot from metaphor-as-platitude to metaphor-as-diagnosis.
2. **"and the means do not depend on any foundation's continued patronage, or any government's continued permission"** — closes back to Scott's self-stated principles (grantee autonomy) AND to the implicit Canada 40K concern (government patronage can be withdrawn). Founder-prose welcome; this is Bishop scaffolding.
