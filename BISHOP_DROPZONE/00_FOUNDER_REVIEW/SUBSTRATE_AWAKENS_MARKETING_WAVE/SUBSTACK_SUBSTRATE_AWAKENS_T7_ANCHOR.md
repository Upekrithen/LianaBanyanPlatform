## Body

We've been building toward this since 1989. Thirty-seven years of iteration compressed into a cooperative AI architecture that runs on your hardware, never touches a paid API, and earns 83.3% for the people doing the work.

Saturday — approximately — we run it live.

### What "Substrate Awakens" is

The first live cooperative mesh benchmark. Five LAN nodes plus WAN plus open replication from anyone who wants to join. Real questions. Real answers. Andon-Cord quarantines when the mesh catches itself being wrong. No fabrication. No polished lie.

The dashboard will be at **[mnemosynec.ai/live/SubstrateAwakens/](https://mnemosynec.ai/live/SubstrateAwakens/)**. Watch the cooperative compute in real time.

### Why event-driven, not time-driven

"Saturday" is the target. It slips one day if any readiness sharp comes up RED Saturday morning. The announcement will say "Saturday-ish — she's not awake yet — back Sunday" if we slip. This is Truth-Always. A polished lie about a launch time costs more than a one-day slip ever could.

### What this proves

The Substrate Awakens benchmark uses fresh questions — not the 1,400 already in the distributed eval that produced our 68/70 MMLU-Pro proof. Fresh questions, live stream, Andon-Cord active. Every quarantine shows on the dashboard. Every dropout shows as "handled." The mesh doesn't hide its failures — it surfaces them and continues.

### What watchers get

A live dashboard showing:
- The constellation of connected peers
- Per-peer progress tickers
- Aggregate accuracy of non-quarantined nodes
- Every quarantine event in real time
- Failure banners if the relay degrades, aggregate stalls, or peer count drops

### What replicators get

Join the event. Register. Receive your one-time heartbeat token. Launch MnemosyneC v0.5.0. Contribute your results. Earn:

- **Crow Feather** — "First Live Mesh · Substrate Awakens" (permanent, non-transferable)
- **100 Marks** — the cooperative's contingent accounts payable
- **Founding Replicator status** — first 100 contributors get a numbered slot in the permanent record

### What you need to replicate

- Windows PC with 8+ GB RAM
- [MnemosyneC v0.5.0](https://mnemosynec.ai/download/) installed
- [Ollama](https://ollama.com/download) running with gemma2:2b or gemma4:12b pulled

That's it. The setup-helper.ps1 in the [mesh bundle](https://mnemosynec.ai/tools/#mnemosynec-plow-cli-lan-mesh-bundle) checks everything automatically.
