> The structure has two doors. Liana Banyan Corporation is the cooperative -- AGPL-licensed, member-owned, the substrate on which the eighty-six-percentage-point measurement runs. Upekrithen LLC is its sister entity, also filed in Wyoming, also under our stewardship -- it holds the integration layer under Apache 2.0 for any company that wants to adopt the same architecture without taking on the cooperative's copyleft. Big enterprises walk through the Upekrithen door. Members and grantees walk through the LB Corp door. Both pay into the same distribution structure: sixty percent to the patent commons, twenty percent to the founder-creator share, ten percent to a global sponsor pool, ten percent to individual member Pedestals. We are not asking the foundation to seed something speculative; we are asking the foundation to amplify the cooperative side of a structure where the commercial side is already designed to feed it.

## The quiet structural shift

With the dual-entity paragraph added, the letter makes a different claim about sustainability. v014f says *"we have a cooperative that needs introductions."* v014i says *"we have a structure where commercial revenue and cooperative revenue feed the same distribution split; introductions amplify the cooperative side, but the structure already self-sustains via the commercial side."* For Scott specifically, who funds organizations she identifies as already doing their best work, this matters: it transforms the grant case from philanthropy-dependent to amplification-class.

It also handles the natural Scott-side concern about giving large gifts to small organizations: the dual-entity structure shows that the recipient organization is not staffed-up beyond the philanthropic capital, because the operational layer (Upekrithen) is funded separately. Large gifts amplify cooperative-tier work without creating organizational dependence on philanthropy.

Pairs with the Pawn 46 finding (Yield Giving / OIN-style "Cooperative AI Infrastructure Grant" framing): the dual-entity structure IS the OIN-style pattern at the legal layer.
