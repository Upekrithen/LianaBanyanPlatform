The architecture isn't a cloud-API artifact. **The substrate does the work, not the vendor.** If every cloud AI vendor disappeared tomorrow, the cooperative still has the architecture.

The architecture composes recursively without bound — the same primitive at one rule on one machine, at a guild, at a cooperative, at a planet, at every scale.

We just patented the architecture. The first piece — the personal discipline rules — ships in the next month. The federation builds out across the quarter. Members participate at whatever depth they choose. **No vendor controls the protocol. No member is forced into any tier. Sovereignty is structural, not a feature.**
