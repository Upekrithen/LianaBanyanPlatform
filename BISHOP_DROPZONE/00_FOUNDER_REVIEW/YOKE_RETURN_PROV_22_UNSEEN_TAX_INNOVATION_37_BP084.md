## Bedside Read (1 paragraph)

Innovation 37 — Unseen Tax — is the financial layer of the same Truth-Always discipline that powers the TIC and Code Breakers innovations. The industry extracts a structural tax by hiding per-call AI inference cost from users: default to the most expensive model, show nothing in real time, surface only an aggregate at month-end. MnemosyneC's inverse binding is structural, not aspirational: every call shows its cost as it happens, every session has a running total, every escalation shows a cost delta with a machine-generated reason, the default is always the cheapest capable model, and the month-end bill is itemized — not just totaled.
