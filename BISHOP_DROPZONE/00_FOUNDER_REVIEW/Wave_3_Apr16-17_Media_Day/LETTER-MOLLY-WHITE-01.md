**Why I'm Writing to a Web3 Skeptic**

Because you should be skeptical of us too.

Here's why we're different, and I want you to tell me if I'm wrong:

**1. The blockchain is for verification, not speculation.**

Medallions track ownership. That's it. They're not tradeable tokens designed to pump. They're receipts that prove you own part of something—like stock certificates, except immutable and auditable.

**2. The economics are locked.**

Platform margin is **Cost + 20%**, fixed in the operating agreement. Not "our policy" or "our commitment"—architecture. Changing it would require dissolving the corporation. Creators keep **83.3%** of every transaction.

This matters because most Web3 projects can change their tokenomics whenever insiders want an exit. We can't.

**3. There's no token sale.**

We're not raising money by selling tokens to speculators. The $5 membership gets you in. Medallions are earned through participation, not purchased for speculation.
