## What I've Built

Liana Banyan is a cooperative commerce platform where creators keep 83.3% of every transaction. Fixed margin — Cost + 20% — locked permanently in the bylaws. No board can change it. No investor can override it.

Three commercial portals generate revenue. That revenue permanently funds **fifteen** cooperative initiatives across food security, healthcare access, education, financial inclusion, family management, and crisis response.

This isn't charity TO the people. It's infrastructure BY the people, FOR the people.

## Why It Matters for Women

- **$5 barrier to entry** — Anyone can start a business for the cost of a coffee. No bank account required. No credit history. No husband's signature. And you can REALLY start for free with Marks.
- **83.3% to creators** — On platforms that extract 30-50%, women lose first. On ours, they keep what they make.
- **Distributed manufacturing** — A woman with a 3D printer in her home can produce precision components. Or test them. Or design them. Or sell them. Or cook meals for neighbors for money. Or start a business that leads a group that does all that and more — for mutual benefit. No factory floor. No commute. No boss.
- **Cooperative ownership** — Every participant earns equity proportional to contribution. Not charity. Ownership.

And when things go wrong: Defense Klaus for self-defense products and legal help. VSL for microloans without collateral or a co-signer. Rally Group for crisis response. The safety net is built in. To every. Single. Component. And Harpers (HR) that work for Liana Banyan in every Member business.

The economics don't care about gender. That's the point. When the system is fair by design, the people who've been excluded by unfair systems benefit most.

## The Hard Part

Making it work everywhere. Not just Louisville. Lagos. Lima. Lahore.

A maker in Nigeria and a maker in Kentucky getting equal purchasing power for their $100 equivalent — automatically, without charity, without arbitrage, without excluding anyone.

The three-gear currency system solves this:

- **Credits:** Primary currency, stable internal value
- **Marks:** Effort-debt for weak-currency economies, clearable through participation
- **Joules:** Stored surplus for strong-currency economies, locked at acquisition rate

Equal footing for disparate economies. No arbitrage. No regional segregation. No weak-currency exclusion. The system adjusts through mechanism design, not charity.
