## Universal Innovation Pipeline (BP094)

Layered on top of the Tier 1 and Tier 2 licensing structure is the Universal Innovation Pipeline -- open to every cooperative member who develops an innovation worth disclosing. All eight licensed AI companies participate in the same competitive marketplace for member IP disclosures. No exclusivity in either direction. Members route disclosures to their preferred receiver at the moment of disclosure -- a per-disclosure choice, not a per-relationship lock-in. Companies compete for innovator attention by their reputation, prior pickup rate, bounty rates paid, and how they treat cooperative members. The cooperative publishes aggregate routing stats annually as competitive discipline.

The IP Ledger records every pickup: member ID, disclosure hash, receiving company ID, tier at pickup, timestamp, disclosure category. If a company declines or ignores a disclosure within 30 calendar days, the member may re-route to any other licensed company with the original stamp date preserved as prior-art anchor.
