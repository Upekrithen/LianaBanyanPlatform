## The hook (proposed copy, Founder ratify)

**Title:** *The $213 Ghost Subscription -- or, Why I Built MnemosyneC*

**Lede:**

I paid Google $213 this year for Google One Pro. I cannot use it.

Not "I forgot to use it." Not "I decided I did not need it." I am charged for it. I am locked out of it. Both at the same time. By the same company.

**The Setup:**

Google decided to merge my private gmail with my business email. Without my consent. Without a path back. The features I bought sit behind an identity wall I cannot cross from either side.

Then I open Antigravity, Google's AI tool. It runs on Gemini. The same model my $213 supposedly covers. Antigravity asks me to pay AGAIN. Separate credits. Different meter. Same model.

Pay twice for one thing. Use it zero times. Welcome to the modern AI economy.

**The Pattern:**

This is not an accident. This is the architecture.

1. Lock the user into one vendor's identity system
2. Bundle the features so the user pays a single big number
3. Quietly silo the AI compute behind a SECOND meter the bundle does not cover
4. Merge accounts without consent so the user cannot escape the lock without losing access to other things they paid for

The vendor wins twice. Once on the bundle. Once on the meter. The user pays twice. Uses it less than once.

**The Cooperative Answer:**

I am building MnemosyneC because of this exact pattern.

MnemosyneC runs on YOUR computer. The AI is bundled into the download. The bigger model upgrade is in-app and free. No second meter. No identity wall. No account-merge ambush. You pay for the cooperative membership ($5 a year) and the rest of the value lives where it should: in the work you do, the substrate you contribute to, and the cooperative members who hire each other for real work at fair rates.

The Liana Banyan cooperative is built on three internal currencies: Credits, Marks, and Joules. None of them convert to fiat. EVER. Members get fiat through actual hiring and payment. Substitution makes the work you already did MORE valuable. So get started today.

No bundle. No merge. No second meter. No $213 ghost subscription.

**The Math (specific, Truth-Always):**

- Google One Pro charge to Founder: $213 / year
- Effective use after account merge: $0 worth of features accessed
- Antigravity Gemini meter: separate paid credits, denominated in a different unit, billed independently
- Total paid to use Gemini once: $213 + per-token Antigravity rate
- Total paid to use Gemini under MnemosyneC FULL upgrade: $0 (already paid the $5 cooperative membership; FULL upgrade is the in-app pull of an open-weights model that runs on your own machine)

**Closing line:**

You pay Google. Google decides whether you get to use what you paid for.

You install MnemosyneC. You decide.

For America. And for the World.
