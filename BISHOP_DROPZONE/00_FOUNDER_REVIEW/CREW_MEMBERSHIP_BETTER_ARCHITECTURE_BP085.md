## §1 · The CREW Pattern

**Anatomy**
A CREW is the cooperative's operational work unit. It forms around a Bounty Poster and dissolves (or persists) when the Bounty closes.

| Element | Value |
|---|---|
| Minimum size | 1 (solo PM) |
| Maximum size | No limit |
| Formation trigger | Bounty Poster on CerosTechnology.com (or organic among NOIDs) |
| Formation lead | PM (Become-the-Boss canon) — the PM IS the first crew member |
| Membership gate | Required — non-members cannot join or earn |
| Effort ledger | Marks earned during the Bounty work period |
| Verify layer | Code Breakers Guild (adversarial verify = valid CREW role) |
| Dispute resolution | BP082 reputation-weight: `1 + log10(Marks+1)` |

**CREW Formation paths**
1. PM hires CREW via Become-the-Boss hiring canon — PM is first member, builds team
2. NOIDs self-organize organically within a Guild Chapter for a Bounty
3. Solo: PM alone works the Bounty — CREW of 1, earns 100% of pool

**Payout Math (explicit · Truth-Always)**
```
Member share = (Member's Marks earned during this Bounty / Total CREW Marks earned during this Bounty) × Bounty pool
```

No black-box. Proportional to effort. Not Marxist-equal (ignores intensity). Not extractive-capitalist (winner-takes-most). Third path: oar-effort earns oar-share.
