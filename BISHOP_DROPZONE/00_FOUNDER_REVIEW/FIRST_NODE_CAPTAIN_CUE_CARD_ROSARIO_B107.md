## THE ASK (plain language)

Rosario,

The first Liana Banyan Node launches in San Antonio on April 30, 2026. A Node is a local cooperative — a group of neighbors who use the platform to help each other: share resources, buy together, start businesses, get things at cost.

I am asking you to be the **Captain** of that Node.

Not because you owe me anything. Because I can't think of a person in San Antonio who has more standing to say *"this works"* than someone who:

- Came to this country and earned citizenship
- Put herself through college at San Antonio College
- Taught Spanish to people who needed it
- Has paid out of pocket for medications that should cost a fraction of what they charge

You're not a charity case and I'm not offering charity. I'm asking you to run a cooperative. Captains choose their own initiative. The platform handles the billing, the bookkeeping, the rules. You handle the neighbors.

The Node needs 500 members in 90 days to break even. La Capital del Sabor is the anchor. Your son [AMBASSADOR NAME] will be your Ambassador. I'll be at INDL-NA in Washington, D.C. when it goes live — announcing it from the stage.

## WHAT A CAPTAIN DOES

- Welcomes the first 50 members personally
- Hosts one community event in the first 30 days (can be at La Capital del Sabor)
- Is the face of the Node — not the manager, the neighbor
- Reports to no one above the Founder and the cooperative's bylaws
- Earns **Captain Marks** for every member who joins through your invitation (one level — not MLM, just attribution)
- Has a permanent seat in the Node's governance after 500 members

**Time required:** 5–10 hours the first month. Then as much or as little as the community needs from you.
