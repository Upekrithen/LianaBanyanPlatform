# 🦋 LETTER TO DOLLY PARTON
## A Recognition Letter — Not a Crown Offer
## The Woman Who Wrote the Anthem
### To Dolly Parton — and to anyone positioned to help
## Liana Banyan Corporation

---

Dear Ms. Parton,

I listen to "9 to 5" every single day while I work.

The original. The Pitbull remix. Both. On rotation.

Not as background noise — as fuel. As a reminder of why I'm doing what I'm doing.

*"Tumble outta bed and stumble to the kitchen / Pour myself a cup of ambition..."*

And in the Pitbull version — when you talk about your mother — it gets me every single time:

*"My mama, she worked her fingers to the bone... She scrubbed other people's floors and she cleaned their homes... And she did it all with dignity..."*

That part moves me to tears. Every. Single. Time.

Because that's who I'm building this for. The people who do the work with dignity, and deserve to keep more of what they earn.

I've been pouring cups of ambition for nine years now, building something I hope would make you — and your mama — proud.

---

## WHAT I'VE BUILT

My name is Jonathan Jones. I'm a {{founderAge}}-year-old father of eight, ARNG veteran, and the founder of Liana Banyan — a cooperative platform where workers keep 83.3% of what they earn.

You wrote "9 to 5" about workers getting squeezed:
*"It's all taking and no giving / They just use your mind, and they never give you credit..."*

I built a platform where that doesn't happen. **Cost + 20%.** Workers keep 83.3%. No middlemen extracting value. No algorithms pushing wages down. No "gig economy" that's really just exploitation with an app.

When someone cooks a meal for their neighbor, they keep $4 of every $5.
When someone makes a craft, they keep 83.3%.
When someone provides a service, they keep 83.3%.

The platform takes 20% to operate — and that's it. Transparent. Fair. The way it should be.

---

## WHY I'M WRITING TO YOU

I'm not asking you to run anything. You've got the Imagination Library to grow and a thousand other ways you're already changing the world. I know you funded the Moderna vaccine research without telling anyone. I know you've given away more books than most libraries own.

I'm writing because you wrote the anthem, and I wanted you to know someone's been listening.

Really listening.

## THE IMAGINATION LIBRARY CONNECTION

You give kids books so they can imagine a better future.
I'm trying to give adults a platform so they can build one.

Same philosophy: **Give people the tools. Trust them to do the rest.**

Your books don't come with strings attached. Neither does my platform. Join for $5 a year. Keep 80% of what you earn. No gotchas.

You've said: *"If you see someone without a smile, give them one of yours."*

I'm trying to build a system that gives people more than smiles — it gives them a fair shake. A real path. A way to work hard AND get ahead, not just work hard and barely survive.

I wrote to Pitbull too. Told him the same thing: your remix of "9 to 5" is on my daily playlist. Both of you — country royalty and Mr. Worldwide — saying the same thing about workers and dignity and not giving up.
