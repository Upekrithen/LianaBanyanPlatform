### Innovation Area 35: Truth Integrity Chain (TIC) — Claim Group 28

Opens with the Africa anecdote — when the Founder was 3, a mother dog attacked him protecting her puppies. She was a perfect reasoner with incomplete data. She had no ELIMINATED category. This is the same structural problem as every current LLM: confident sentences without representing what has been definitively ruled out.

The section specifies the five-field TIC eblet record: KNOWN (verified propositions with evidence trails), THEORIES_OPEN (competing hypotheses with survival scores), ELIMINATED (definitively falsified hypotheses with contradiction trails), DEPENDENCIES_UPSTREAM, and APPLICATIONS_DOWNSTREAM. A Bob-is-a-dog formalization shows the foundational-claim collapse trap in logic. A hasty-generalization structural guard prevents universal-quantified claims from achieving KNOWN status without minimum sample size plus counter-evidence search. Three new plow loops (10/11/12) implement the mechanism: Loop 10 tests open theories by probing their consequences; Loop 11 searches the substrate for facts that contradict open theories and eliminates them; Loop 12 propagates upstream knowledge updates downstream through the dependency graph. A full gravity-domain worked example demonstrates all five fields and all three loops with realistic values.

### Innovation Area 36: Code Breakers Guild and Gold Refined by Fire — Claim Group 29

Opens with the Founder's principle: *"What survives the smashing is Immutable. Code Breakers smash. Marks pay them. Gold Refined by Fire."* The cooperative pays its critics. This is what peer review was supposed to do before it became unpaid, anonymous, and conflicted.

The section specifies the four-tier claim progression (UNTESTED → TESTED [K=20 verifications] → FORGED [M=10 adversarial challenges OR 90 days] → GOLD_REFINED_BY_FIRE [Immutable]). Code Breaker member tiers progress from Initiate (1 success) through Journeyman (10) and Master (100 + 10 FORGED-tier reversals) to Refiner of Gold — the highest honor, awarded for honest hardest attacks on claims that subsequently became Immutable. This inverts the conventional recognition structure: the highest honor goes to the person who tried hardest to break the truth and couldn't. A reversal-of-Immutable process is specified: new empirical data plus Guild majority vote plus new challenge cycle, structurally high-bar but occasionally crossed.

Negative-Knowledge Tokens are specified as Marks tagged with a negative-knowledge denomination at the cooperative ledger level — same currency, different denomination label, 1.0x redemption parity. A game-theoretic analysis shows that paying Code Breakers inverts the perverse incentives of unpaid peer review, citing Ioannidis 2005 and bug bounty program empirical results.

**Patent claims:** 29.1 (four-tier progression), 29.2 (Code Breaker member tiers), 29.3 (Negative-Knowledge Marks denomination and parity).
