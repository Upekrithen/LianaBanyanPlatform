## Proposed ToS Section (Scaffold)

### § X.Y — Anti-Money-Laundering Cooperation

*[Counsel to determine section numbering and integrate with existing ToS structure.]*

**X.Y.1 — Monitoring.** Members agree that Liana Banyan Corporation ("LB Corporation") may monitor transaction patterns on the platform for indicators of money laundering, fraud, or other prohibited financial activity. This monitoring is conducted using automated systems and is reviewed by trained community curators and, where applicable, LB Corporation's compliance function.

**X.Y.2 — Cooperation.** Members agree to provide source-of-funds documentation upon reasonable written request from LB Corporation's compliance function. High-volume platform participants (as determined by LB Corporation from time to time) may be required to provide enhanced identity verification, including but not limited to government-issued identification and documentation of source of funds, as a condition of continued platform participation at elevated transaction volumes.

**X.Y.3 — Regulatory Reporting.** Members acknowledge and agree that LB Corporation may be required by applicable law to file Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) or other regulatory reports with relevant authorities, including but not limited to the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN). Members agree not to take any action to impede or circumvent lawful regulatory reporting.

**X.Y.4 — Consequences of Non-Cooperation.** Failure to cooperate with a lawful request under this Section may result in:  
(a) suspension of account activity pending review;  
(b) termination of membership in accordance with § [Termination Section]; and/or  
(c) referral to appropriate regulatory or law enforcement authorities as required by applicable law.

**X.Y.5 — Privacy.** LB Corporation's handling of information obtained under this Section is subject to the Privacy Policy at [lianabanyan.com/privacy]. Internal transaction monitoring data is accessible only to designated compliance personnel and authorized community curators who have signed confidentiality agreements.

**X.Y.6 — Good-Faith Operation.** This Section exists to protect the integrity of the Liana Banyan cooperative and its members from financial crime. It does not authorize LB Corporation to monitor or report on member activity unrelated to financial integrity on the platform. AML monitoring is scoped to platform Credit, Mark, and Joule transactions and does not extend to members' off-platform financial activity.
