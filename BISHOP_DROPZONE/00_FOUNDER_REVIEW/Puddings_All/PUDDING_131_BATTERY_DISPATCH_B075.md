## How It Works: Three Systems, One Button

### 1. Plugs — Connect Once

Connect each social account one time via OAuth (the same "Sign in with..." button you've used a thousand times). That's it. You're plugged in.

**12 platforms supported:**
TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, YouTube, Bluesky, Threads, Mastodon, Discord, Substack, Imgur

Each Plug remembers your connection. You don't re-authenticate every time. You don't copy API keys. You don't manage tokens. Connect once, and your Plug stays live until you unplug it.

Think of it like plugging in appliances around your house. Plug in the TV once. Plug in the lamp once. They work until you unplug them. Same thing — but for your social media accounts.

### 2. Circle in a Square Hole — Auto-Adapt

Every platform is a different-shaped hole.

Twitter is a tiny square: 280 characters. TikTok is a vertical rectangle: video required, no clickable links. LinkedIn is a wide rectangle: 3,000 characters, professional tone expected. Instagram is a visual circle: image or video required, no clickable links in captions.

Your content is a circle. These platforms are different-shaped holes. What do you do?

You use an adapter.

The Circle in a Square Hole adapter does this automatically:

- **Your 500-word post going to Twitter?** Auto-truncated to 280 characters with a "Full version at lianabanyan.com" link.
- **Your text-only post going to TikTok?** Flagged — TikTok needs video. You can attach one or skip that platform.
- **Your casual post going to LinkedIn?** You can toggle "professional tone" mode for that platform's version.
- **Your long post going to Discord?** Formatted as a rich embed with your image as thumbnail.
- **Your video going to Instagram?** Auto-trimmed to 90 seconds for Reels if it's longer.

You write once. The adapter fits it everywhere.

### 3. Battery Dispatch — One Tap

The Dispatch button is the payoff. After your Plugs are connected and your content is adapted, you see a panel:

- ✅ Twitter/X — "I'm a Patriotic Interde..." [280 chars, truncated]
- ✅ TikTok — [Video + caption]
- ✅ Instagram — [Video as Reel + caption]
- ✅ LinkedIn — [Full text + link preview]
- ✅ Facebook — [Full text + video + link]
- ☐ YouTube — [Skipped — no video attached]

Toggle any platform on or off. Edit any platform's version individually if you want. Or just hit **DISPATCH** and let it fly.

Real-time status shows each platform posting: ✅ ✅ ✅ ⏳ ✅ — done.

## Stamp-to-Send: Nothing Leaves Without Your Approval

Battery Dispatch is NOT a fire-and-forget blast tool. Every dispatch is **staged**:

1. **Stage:** You compose your content. The adapter generates per-platform previews.
2. **Review:** You see EXACTLY what will post on EACH platform.
3. **Stamp:** You explicitly approve each platform's version (toggle per platform).
4. **Send:** Only stamped platforms receive the dispatch.

Every Stamp is logged in a verified ledger — timestamp, platform, content snapshot. Full dispatch history viewable in your dashboard.

Why this matters:

- **Reputation protection:** You can't accidentally post something wrong on LinkedIn that was meant for TikTok.
- **Trust differentiator:** "We don't blast your content. We stage it, you review it, you stamp it, we send it."
- **Undo window:** Between Stamp and Send, there's a configurable delay (default 30 seconds) where you can cancel.

Every other tool optimizes for speed. We optimize for trust.

## For the Skeptics

**"How can this cost $0.42/month?"**
Because we're a cooperative. No shareholders, no advertising budget, no executive bonuses. The tool costs what the servers cost, divided by the membership. That's cooperative economics.

**"What about analytics?"**
Battery Dispatch tracks clicks and conversions per platform per dispatch. You see what works where. No extra charge.

**"Can I schedule posts?"**
Yes. The Universal Remote supports scheduling through the same dispatch interface. Set it and forget it.

**"What if a platform changes its API?"**
The adapter layer is modular. When Twitter changes something (and they will), we update the Twitter adapter. Your dispatch workflow doesn't change.

**"What about my existing followers on each platform?"**
Nothing changes. Your followers stay your followers. Battery Dispatch posts to your accounts. It's your content, your voice, your audience. We just make the posting part take one tap instead of twelve.

**"Does this use blockchain?"**
No. Liana Banyan does not use blockchain technology. Dispatch records are maintained in a standard verified database ledger. Simple, reliable, auditable.
