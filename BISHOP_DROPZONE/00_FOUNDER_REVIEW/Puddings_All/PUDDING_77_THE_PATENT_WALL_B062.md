# Pudding #77 — The Patent Wall

*How one person filed 11 provisional patents for $715 total — and why the system is designed so that anyone can.*

---

Most people think patents are for corporations with legal departments. The filing fees alone seem designed to discourage individuals — $1,600 for a non-provisional utility patent at full rate, plus attorney fees that routinely exceed $10,000 per application.

But there is another path. The USPTO micro-entity designation reduces filing fees by 75%. A provisional patent application costs $65. You get 12 months of protection while you build, test, and refine. And you can file it yourself — no attorney required for the provisional stage.

Liana Banyan Corporation has filed 11 provisional patent applications. Total cost: $715. Total innovations covered: over 2,100. Total formal claims: 2,103. The first was filed on November 26, 2025. The most recent — Application 64/025,635, covering approximately 150 innovations across 17 specification groups — was filed on April 2, 2026.

This is not a flex about volume. This is a demonstration of a principle: **intellectual property protection should be accessible to individual creators, not just funded startups.**

The cooperative is built on this principle. When a member creates something on the platform — a product design, a recipe, a teaching method, a manufacturing process — the IP Ledger tracks it. The member owns their creation. The platform's job is to help them protect it, monetize it, and build on it.

The Canister System (our desktop manufacturing unit) is covered by these patents. So is the Star Chamber dispute resolution system, the three-currency economic model, the Red Carpet demand-signal recruitment mechanism, and 164 other Crown Jewel innovations with zero or minimal prior art.

None of this required a law firm. It required systematic documentation, clear descriptions, and $65 per filing.

The patent wall is not a moat. It is a demonstration that one person — with discipline, AI assistance, and $65 at a time — can build a defensible intellectual property position that would cost a traditional startup millions to replicate.

The system is designed so that every member can do the same with their own innovations. The IP Ledger, the A&A formal process, the Crown Jewel classification — these are not internal tools. They are the infrastructure for a cooperative where every creator's ideas are protected by default.

$65. That is the price of protecting an idea. The cooperative makes sure the idea has somewhere to grow.
