## The Pudding

Traditional journalism has two economic problems: advertising revenue collapsed, and the credentialing path is closed. You get to be a journalist by being hired by someone who already decided you are one. If the institutions collapse — and they are — the pipeline closes with them.

Harper Guild solves both problems.

**The economic fix:** Cost + 20%. When a member fact-checks a claim, writes an explainer, reports on a local business, or audits an initiative's ethics — they keep 83.3% of the bounty. No advertising. No platform extraction. The Harper does the work; the Harper gets paid.

**The credentialing fix:** Qualification is stacked, transparent, and earned through verified work. Not through who hired you. Not through which school you attended. Not through which institution credentialed you. Through the specific verifiable tasks you have completed, stamped by peer review.

## The five criteria

To be a Harper, a member demonstrates:

**Verifiability** — every factual claim cites a primary source, names its sources when safe, links the data, and stores receipts.

**Correction discipline** — errors get public, timestamped corrections. Your correction history lives on your profile. A Harper with no corrections is suspicious. A Harper who never updates is not one.

**Independence declaration** — conflicts of interest, funding, prior involvement all disclosed up front. Disguised advertising disqualifies.

**Peer review participation** — Harpers stamp each other's work. False stamps affect your own standing. Your attestations become part of your record.

**Subject-matter transparency** — state your expertise, state your limits, bring in experts where you're out of your depth.

These are operational disciplines, not credentials. A member without a journalism degree can meet them. A journalism PhD who fails them doesn't qualify.

## The ladder

A bus driver who fact-checks articles for $15 per check accumulates a verifiable journalism record. Five entry-level bounties with 100% verification passes = **Cub Harper**. Fifteen bounties with 90%+ verification + peer review participation = **Stamped Harper**. Fifty bounties + sustained correction discipline + declared beat = **Harper**. Two hundred bounties + mentor status + ethics audit privileges = **Master Harper**.

Four stages. Each earned. Each visible on-ledger. Each can be lost if the discipline fails.

## Why this matters

Because the truth-telling function does not belong to the people who can pay to credential it. It belongs to the people who do the work well enough to have it verified.

Because the collapse of advertising-funded journalism left a vacuum that gets filled by content-farm slop and billionaire vanity outlets. Neither serves the community. Neither audits power. Neither fact-checks the lie you will hear tomorrow.

Because a cooperative platform that says "member ownership" but leaves journalism to external institutions is cooperative-adjacent, not cooperative. The information the cooperative runs on — the fact-checks, the ethics audits, the stories that hold initiatives accountable — must live inside the cooperative's own bylaws.

Harper Guild is that infrastructure. Brené Brown holds the Harper Prime seat because trust infrastructure is not soft. Anne McCaffrey's Pern Harpers carried conscience across the network; Harper Lee named the truth the town wanted buried. Both references live in the name. Both jobs live in the role.

## This is NOT Pudding

This is not a credential-replacement scheme. A Harper's verification record is not a degree; it's a working reputation that can decay as fast as it accumulates.

This is not open-season for partisan content. Independence declaration is structural. Harpers disclose funding, conflicts, and biases on every piece. Partisan framing without disclosure fails the criteria.

This is not a finished system. The bounty criteria are drafted; the platform infrastructure is queued; the first Cub Harpers haven't been stamped yet. But the design is explicit: anyone can qualify, the path is public, and the economics reward the work.

The bus driver is coming.
