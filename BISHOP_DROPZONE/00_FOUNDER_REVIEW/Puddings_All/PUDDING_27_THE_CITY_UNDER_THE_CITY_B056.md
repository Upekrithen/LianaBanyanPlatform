## What the Deep Audit Found

When we sent 9 parallel AI agents to audit every domain — reading every file, tracing every import, mapping every component — the real numbers emerged:

**Gaming domain**: The Librarian said 16 pages. The audit found 31 pages and 43 components. A full 3D world renderer. Overworld navigation with gondolas and pipe transit. Diceless combat. Root-lock mechanics. Twelve cities. An island builder. A dual economy with crow feathers and fray leagues.

**Social domain**: The Librarian said 6 pages. The audit found 8 pages, 12 edge functions, and 6 business logic modules. MoneyPenny — the AI command center — has its own email classifier, morning briefing generator, SMS gateway, social media auto-poster, and a dual AI brain that falls back from Claude to Perplexity. It manages 12 social platforms with per-platform rate limits.

**Currency domain**: Three currencies (Credits, Marks, Joules) plus Medallions, LB Card, escrow, and 9 checkout functions that share 700 lines of duplicated Stripe boilerplate. Fifteen database tables. Twenty-seven edge functions.

**Commerce domain**: The Librarian said 3 pages. The audit found 23. Marketplace, product catalog, pre-order flow, production queue, manufacturing pipeline, storefront templates, Printful integration, Google Calendar sync.

Every domain was deeper than it looked.
