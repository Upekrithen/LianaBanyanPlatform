**The Surveillance Capitalism Alternative**

Liana Banyan operates on a different business model: **Cost + 20%**.

| Platform | Revenue Model | Incentive |
|----------|---------------|-----------|

| Liana Banyan | Transaction margin (fixed) | Maximize transaction value (for creators) |

- Acquisition (the operating agreement survives)
- Leadership change (the structure persists)
- Investor pressure (no VC funding, no IPO)

We cannot pivot to surveillance capitalism because the legal structure prevents it.
