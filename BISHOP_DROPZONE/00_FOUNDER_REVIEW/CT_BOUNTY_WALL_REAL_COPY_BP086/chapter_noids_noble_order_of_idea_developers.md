## BACK FACE

### What NOIDs Are
**NOIDs = Noble Order of Idea Developers** — the cooperative-class for builders who originate ideas on Liana Banyan and the projects that branch from it (MnemosyneC · Ceros Technology · Cephas · HexIsle).

Self-identification, not diagnosis. The NOID accepts the Order.
You do not need to be labeled NOID by anyone else. You name it yourself.

### What Idea-Originators Get
- **Public Provisional #1 rails:** Contribute an innovation to the cooperative's public-contribution patent stream. Bring your own innovation. Your name goes on the filing. Pledge #2260 binds every entry — irrevocable cooperative commons dedication.
- **Marks:** Earn cooperative participation Marks for every verified contribution to the substrate — adversarial testing · edge-case documentation · Plow blade refinement · math · claim drafting.
- **Authorship credit:** Named in the Public Provisional filing you contributed to. Permanent attribution in the substrate. Not a thank-you in the notes — named authorship on the I.P.
- **Ground-floor inclusion:** Early access to working-state releases · Crow Feather tracking.
- **Code Breakers sister relationship:** NOIDs who also join Code Breakers earn Negative-Knowledge Marks for adversarial falsification — the cooperative pays its critics, not just its choir.

### Marks Earning Structure
- Contribution Marks: per verified substrate contribution (testing · claim draft · Plow refinement)
- Negative-Knowledge Marks: per successful falsification of a THEORIES_OPEN claim (Code Breaker cross-role)
- Proportional CREW split: if working within a CREW Bounty, your share = your Marks / total CREW Marks × Bounty pool
