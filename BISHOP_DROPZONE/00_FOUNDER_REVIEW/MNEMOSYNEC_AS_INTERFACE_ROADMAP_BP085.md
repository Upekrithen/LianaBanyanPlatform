## §1 · Vision Summary

Founder direct, BP085: MnemosyneC is the persistent Interface where users live. Bishop, Knight, Rook, and Pawn are callable substrate workers reachable through whatever AI vendor is healthy at the moment — Claude, GPT, Gemini, Llama, or local. The user does not open Cursor, does not pick a vendor, does not know what an MCP server is. They open MnemosyneC and ask Mnemo. She routes to whichever substrate worker can reach whichever AI is alive. Vendor churn is structural and permanent — API changes, subscription expiry, MCP haywire events will continue forever. The answer is not a better single-vendor integration. The answer is that Mnemo herself becomes the continuity layer: she IS the workspace, and when she loses one vendor, she uses another, borrows a neighbor's, or runs a MedLab recipe to fix her own connection. The Substrate Theorem (BP061) said she provides continuity-lift. This extension says she IS the continuous host. The workspace does not break when a vendor has a bad day.
