## §4 · SUBSTRATE ARCHITECTURE (Pawn must understand to explain on CT)

The Substrate is MnemosyneC's core innovation. CT needs to explain it to non-technical visitors in a way that is accurate, not dumbed-down, and grounded in the receipts. Here is the architectural vocabulary — use these terms correctly.

### Core Primitives

**Eblets** — atomic knowledge units. Each eblet carries:
- KNOWN (definitive truths with receipts)
- THEORIZED / THEORIES_OPEN (enumerated unknowns with consequence chains and survival scores)
- ELIMINATED (provably false with contradiction trails)
- DEPENDENCIES_UPSTREAM (chronos-linked priors)
- APPLICATIONS_DOWNSTREAM (what builds on this)

**Eblits** — snapshot-at-access fragments. They freeze substrate state per moment of access. Distinct from eblets (ongoing) vs. eblits (snapshots).

**Pheromone Trails** — stigmergic retrieval routing. Access strengthens a trail; decay clears unused ones. The substrate routes to relevant knowledge the way ant colonies route to food — emergently, without central coordination.

**Eblit Emitter** — emission primitive with 4 circuits: contingency_operator · oracle_circuit · prophet_circuit · thorax.

**Wrasse Quartermaster** — pre-session auto-injection. Before a session begins, the Wrasse loads relevant substrate context automatically.

**Pearls** — substrate-enrichment / audit / transmission / milestone / drift-receipt / peer-witness events. A Pearl is emitted when something notable happens in the substrate — a milestone, a reconciliation, a peer verification.

**Thorax Heartbeat** — cooperative-access authentication. Forks, derivatives, and third-party implementations that want to reach the Cooperative Node Frontier MUST route through the Thorax heartbeat. Open-source posture intact: forks are fine. Gated frontier access is the value.

**Scrambler** — A&A Formal #2259 deterministic sync layer. Reads canonical substrate state at session start; reconciles at session end. No AI inside the Scrambler — it is a deterministic protocol, not a model.

### The 12-Loop Plow

The Plow is the synergistic activator of the Substrate. It runs 12 loops in sequence:

- Loops 1–9: base reasoning loops (established prior to BP084)
- Loop 10: CONSEQUENCE_TRACE — traces downstream effects of any claim
- Loop 11: ELIMINATION_VERIFICATION — adversarial falsification (Code Breakers operationalize this)
- Loop 12: DEPENDENCY_PROPAGATION — propagates changes to all downstream eblets when an upstream eblet is updated

Loops 10–12 were added in BP084 (days ago as of this briefing). The Plow is what converts a language model from a token predictor into a reasoning substrate. The 97.1% receipt is the Plow's empirical output.

### Key Concepts for CT Explanation

**Substrace Theorem** — the wake/dispatch protocol. When a session begins, the Substrate re-weaves shared state from the stored substrate. It does not re-transmit data — it reconstructs coherent context from atomic units. This is how 6 simultaneous SEGs can operate at 40% context without collision.

**Staggered Single Domains** — the test methodology for the 68/70 run. 14 MMLU-Pro domains ran independently in stagger, then connected. This prevents cross-contamination between domains and ensures each domain score is clean. The 14-of-14 GREEN is the stagger result.

**Ascending (Andon)** — when the Substrate is uncertain, it ASCENDS (escalates) rather than guessing. The 2 Andon ascensions in the 68/70 run are NOT failures. They are the substrate's visible self-correction — it knew it didn't know, and it said so. This is the epistemic opposite of hallucination.

**Gold Refined by Fire Principle** — what survives adversarial Code Breaker attack becomes Immutable. The Substrate is not static knowledge — it is knowledge that has passed through fire.
