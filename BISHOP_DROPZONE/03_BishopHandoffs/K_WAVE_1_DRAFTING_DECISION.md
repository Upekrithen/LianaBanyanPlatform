## D.1 — Drafting depth (Founder pre-ratified γ Hybrid)

**Option γ (RATIFIED):** Substack + LinkedIn at β (full narrative, ~90% finished length); Reddit + Discord at α (skeleton + anchors only).

**Rationale:**

Substack long-form and LinkedIn professional posts require narrative voice and tonal coherence that benefits from β treatment — Founder's prose-pass rewrites 60-80% per `feedback_drafts_as_scaffolding.md`, but the frame, evidence ordering, and structural argument need to be fully staged. A bare skeleton would leave Founder doing structural work rather than voice work. β is the right investment for 15-channel posts.

Reddit and Discord formats are shorter, community-specific, and inherently informal. α (skeleton + anchors + tailored pitch angle) gives Founder the frame and receipts; voice is trivially adjusted at fire-time in these formats. The community-shaped openings are the critical scaffolding; prose is easy for Founder to supply in 200-600 words.
