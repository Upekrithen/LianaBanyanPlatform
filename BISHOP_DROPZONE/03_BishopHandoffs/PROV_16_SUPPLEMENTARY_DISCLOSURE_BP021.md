**File**: `BISHOP_DROPZONE/12_Innovations_AA/AA_FORMAL_2327_MARK_BACKING_ONE_WAY_VALVE_BP021_DRAFT.md`

Cooperative currency anti-extraction architecture combining (a) the Mark backing ratchet — all Marks are backed by cooperative surplus; backing only goes up, never down — and (b) the Credit one-way valve — Credits never cash out to fiat currency. **File**: `BISHOP_DROPZONE/12_Innovations_AA/AA_FORMAL_2329_TRUST_MATCH_BP021_DRAFT.md`

**File**: `BISHOP_DROPZONE/12_Innovations_AA/AA_FORMAL_2332_BAGS_OF_HOLDING_BP021_DRAFT.md`

Three-class session-context-vs-persistent-substrate architecture with cross-session continuity. Three bags: session bag (Ephemeral), member bag (Personal-Permanent), cooperative bag (Shared-Permanent). Inside structurally larger than outside: context window capacity ≪ substrate capacity. Prior art (RAG, vector databases) retrieves content reactively; Bags of Holding proactively preloads from the Bedrock bag at session start and captures all session work product to permanent storage at session end.
