## Section 1 — Innovation Summary

### What this paper discloses

*"A Considered Approach to Health Care"* is the clinical healthcare sovereignty paper — Paper 7 of the 12-paper Save-the-World Series.

**Thesis**: US healthcare is broken not because medicine is broken, but because the financial-extraction layer wrapped around medicine is broken. Restoring patient-direct + cooperative-volume + transparent-pricing fixes most of it without changing the practice of medicine.

The paper assembles composing primitives (Detective-confirmed where possible):

1. **Medical Savings Accounts** — member-owned health-spending vehicle within LB cooperative-economics; portable across employers; non-extractive; funds remain in the cooperative economy
2. **The Swoop** — emergency-medical-coordination primitive (Founder canonical naming; Decisions_63 confirmed; mechanism details Founder confirms at fire-time)
3. **Volume Discounted Medications** — cooperative buying-power against pharmaceutical pricing; same Architecture-Beats-More logic as manufacturing pre-orders applied to medication supply chain
4. **Cost+20% healthcare services** — canonical platform pricing rule applied to clinical services; transparent margin; non-extractive
5. **Providing food (food-is-medicine composition)** — Health Care paper composes with Paper 4 (Abolishing World Hunger); Family Table / Let's Make Dinner / Let's Get Groceries directly contribute to clinical outcomes
6. **Network of Experience** (canonical name TBD — Founder confirms) — crowdsource-expert-network for hard-diagnosis cases; NYT Diagnosis-column-referenced; connects specialists + patients globally to solve real diagnostic cases

**Composing Claim — A Considered Approach to Health Care via Cooperative Healthcare Sovereignty Architecture**

Method + system for cooperative-platform community-and-member-scale clinical healthcare sovereignty where:

(i) **Medical Savings Accounts** — member-owned health-spending vehicles operating within the LB cooperative-economics architecture — provide portable, employer-independent, non-extractive healthcare funding that remains within the cooperative economy rather than flowing to insurance-extraction intermediaries;

(ii) **The Swoop** — emergency-medical-coordination primitive — provides rapid-response coordination for acute medical situations at cooperative-platform tier (canonical mechanism details to be elaborated by Founder at fire-time per Decisions_63 substrate receipt);

(iii) **Volume Discounted Medications** — cooperative buying-power aggregation against pharmaceutical pricing — applies the pre-order volume-discount architecture (established in Paper 5 manufacturing) to medication procurement: cooperative members pre-commit to medication demand; pharmaceutical volume discounts pass through at Cost+20% cooperative margin;

(iv) **Cost+20% healthcare services** applies the canonical platform pricing rule to clinical service coordination: transparent margin, non-extractive, with member-share-back for cooperative-participant healthcare practitioners;

(v) the **food-is-medicine composition** connects Paper 7 (Health Care) with Paper 4 (Abolishing World Hunger): the Family Table / Let's Make Dinner / Let's Get Groceries food-sovereignty architecture directly contributes to clinical outcomes by ensuring cooperative members have reliable access to nutrition, shifting the healthcare architecture from treatment-of-disease-caused-by-deprivation to prevention-via-food-sovereignty;

(vi) a **Network of Experience** (canonical name TBD — Founder confirms) — crowdsource-expert-network for hard-diagnosis cases — connects specialists and patients globally to solve real diagnostic cases at civilization scale: using the Apiarist Librarian Hive (cohort-class collaborative intelligence) and Thirteenth Warrior (civilization-scale concert layer) primitives to assemble the right experts for rare-disease and hard-diagnosis cases without geographic or institutional gatekeeping;

(vii) the clean water + cooking heat + electricity substrate from Paper 3 (Universal Abundant Low Cost Energy) reduces a substantial fraction of healthcare-driving illness by addressing water-borne disease, malnutrition from inadequate cooking heat, and cold-exposure vectors;

(viii) the cooperative-platform layer (Liana Banyan) productizes medical-savings coordination + emergency-response dispatch + medication-volume aggregation + clinical-service coordination at Cost+20% structural margin, making healthcare a cooperative infrastructure service rather than an extractive insurance architecture;

(ix) the paper composes as the clinical-healthcare-sovereignty leg of the 12-paper Save-the-World Series, forming the Life vector's clinical component alongside Papers 4 (nutrition), 9 (learning), and 11 (caregiving) while remaining distinct from Paper 11 (caregiving = daily-life) — Paper 7 is clinical; Paper 11 is daily-life labor recognition.
