### Innovation #2177: Commitment Receipts

**Category**: Member Engagement / Accountability
**Crown Jewel**: NO
**Patent Relevance**: Yes

**Description**: A system for issuing verifiable proof-of-commitment tokens when a member commits to a specific project task within the Liana Banyan cooperative platform. Each receipt records the commitment made, the deadline, and whether the commitment was ultimately honored. These are accountability instruments, not financial instruments.

**Mechanics**: When a member accepts a task on a Bridge (project control panel), the platform generates a Commitment Receipt containing the task description, acceptance timestamp, projected completion window, and the member's Helm ID. Upon task completion or expiration, the receipt is updated with an honored/broken status. Honored receipts contribute positively to the member's ADAPT Score and Cue Card reputation. Broken receipts trigger a cooldown period before the member can accept similar commitments. The receipt ledger is visible to project captains when evaluating Crew Call applicants, creating a transparent reliability history that operates entirely within the platform's Credit/Mark/Joule economy at Cost+20% margin.

**Prior Art Distinction**: Existing project management tools (Jira, Asana, Trello) track task assignment and completion but do not generate portable, verifiable commitment records that persist across projects and influence future access. No existing platform ties commitment history to a cooperative reputation score that gates future participation opportunities within a closed-loop credit economy.

**Claims**:
1. A cooperative platform system comprising: a commitment receipt generator that creates verifiable proof-of-commitment tokens upon member acceptance of project tasks; a receipt ledger that records commitment status including honored and broken outcomes; and a reputation integration module that adjusts member standing based on cumulative commitment history.
2. A method for managing cooperative task accountability, comprising: receiving a member's acceptance of a project task; generating a commitment receipt containing task metadata, member identifier, and temporal bounds; monitoring task completion status; updating the receipt with an honored or broken designation; and propagating the updated status to the member's platform-wide reputation score.
3. The system of claim 1, wherein the commitment receipt ledger is accessible to project captains during crew formation, enabling reliability-based member selection for future cooperative projects.

**SEC-Safe Language**: This innovation operates within the Liana Banyan closed-loop credit economy. It does not create securities or investment instruments.
