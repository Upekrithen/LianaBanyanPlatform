### 4.2 Tier 1 — Cathedral (Foundation Tier — Lone Wolf cohort default)

**What member gets at Cathedral tier:**

- **Per-member venue** — the canonical "Cathedral" substrate venue (#2270 Scribes Cathedral) personalized for the member; the member's name, work, history, and substrate are housed here
- **Own Scribes** — Architecture / Decisions / Handoffs / Queue / BRIDLEMemory / R11 scribe-class persistent memory, all member-scoped

- **Cathedral Effect baseline retrieval** (#2278 cross-vendor receipt) — floor lift over default-AI even at Lone Wolf tier; LB Frame demonstrates lift even at floor-class
- **Public AGPL substrate baseline** — Cathedral architecture preserved as cohort-class default; long-haul stable

**What member does NOT get at Cathedral tier:**

- **Orchestra access** — no SHARED Scribes + Eblets; no cross-Cathedral provenance; the member's Symphony plays only their own Scribes
- **Federation Scribe-sharing** — per BP016 Federation-vs-Non-Federation structural canon, Federation membership IS the bright line for Scribe-sharing
- **Apiarist Hive participation** — requires Federation cohort-class minimum
- **Excalibur Class subscription** — orthogonal commercial-purchase tier; Cathedral members may purchase Excalibur slices but membership-tier remains Cathedral
- **Pedestal Forum cross-cohort surfaces** — Cathedral-tier Pedestals are per-member-venue-scoped (member can author Pedestals on their own work; cannot compose alongside other-member work without tier upgrade)

**Cathedral-tier Pedestal Forum behavior:**

Cathedral-tier members host Pedestals on their own published artifacts (e.g., personal-permanent essays member shares to their own Cathedral). Per the Mordecai-Esther canon, Cathedral-tier Pedestals admit member's own decree-compositions (member's later additions to their own original) but do NOT admit cross-member decree-composition without tier-upgrade boundary crossing. Pedestal stamping at Cathedral tier is local-Cathedral-ledger.
