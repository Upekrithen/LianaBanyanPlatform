## Innovation #2037 — Sponsored Business Starter Kit
**Category:** Onboarding / Sponsorship / Economic Development
**Impact:** HIGH
**Crown Jewel Candidate:** YES

### The Copy

> **"Every member who starts a project becomes a BUSINESS OWNER. Even at sole prop level. This is the 'save the world' part — you're not just giving people jobs, you're giving them businesses."**

### Description

Fund 10,000 Business Starter Kits at $100 each ($1,000,000 total), backed by the Patent Portfolio (10 provisionals, ~1,511 formal claims). Each kit includes:

- **$5/year LB membership** (first year paid)
- **Business formation guide** (sole prop → LLC pathway)
- **Cue Card with QR code** linking to first bounty board
- **500 starter Marks** (enough to claim first bounties immediately)
- **LB Card** (prepaid with $50 in cash — enough to buy materials for first prototype)
- **"DO THE WORK = GET THE STATUS" physical card** (the recruitment Cue Card)

**Distribution:** Handed out at maker spaces, FabLabs, community colleges, veterans organizations, workforce development programs. Each kit turns a person into a business owner on the spot.

**Funding:** Sponsored Marks backed by Patent Portfolio. The $1M investment is collateralized by 10 provisionals with 1,511 claims across 2,077+ innovations. As products sell, the sponsorship pays for itself through C+20% margin on every unit produced by every business started from a kit.
