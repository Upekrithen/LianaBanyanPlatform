## UPDATE 1: ALL FOUR CURRENCIES ACCEPTED

Subscriptions accept ANY of the three cooperative currencies PLUS dollars:

| Payment Method | How It Works | Fees |
|---------------|-------------|------|
| **Marks** | Internal ledger transfer. Subscriber's Marks → Creator's Marks. | 16.7% platform facilitation (Cost+20%) |
| **Credits** | Internal ledger transfer. Subscriber's Credits → Creator's Credits. | 16.7% platform facilitation (Cost+20%) |
| **Joules** | Internal ledger transfer. Subscriber's Joules → Creator (as Marks or Credits, creator's choice). | 16.7% platform facilitation (Cost+20%) |
| **Dollars (Stripe)** | Stripe recurring billing. External payment → Commerce Engine → Creator's Credits. | 16.7% (Cost+20%) + Stripe processing fees |

**All at permanent parity:** 1 Credit = 1 Mark = 1 Joule = $1.

**Subscriber chooses at checkout.** The creator sets ONE price (e.g., "10/month"). The subscriber picks which currency. The platform handles the rest.

```
Subscription: "Diana's Deal Drops" — 10/month

Subscriber A: Pays 10 Marks  → Diana receives 8.33 Marks
Subscriber B: Pays 10 Credits → Diana receives 8.33 Credits
Subscriber C: Pays 10 Joules  → Diana receives 8.33 Credits (Joules convert)
Subscriber D: Pays $10 Stripe → Diana receives 8.33 Credits (after Cost+20% + Stripe fees)
```

**Why this matters:**
- **New members** who bought Credits can subscribe immediately (don't need to earn Marks first)
- **Active members** can spend Marks they earned through contribution
- **Long-term members** sitting on Joules can activate stored value
- **Non-members** can pay dollars (Ghost mode subscribers, potentially)
- **The creator doesn't care.** All currencies have equal value. Revenue is revenue.

**The internal economy advantage:** Marks, Credits, and Joules subscriptions are ALL internal ledger transfers. No Stripe fees. No payment processing delay. Instant. The only time real money moves is when a subscriber chooses the Stripe/dollar option. This means the cooperative's internal economy runs at LOWER cost than external payment rails — an inherent advantage of the three-currency system.

## UPDATE 2: TASTEMAKER INTEGRATION

### The Existing System

The BandWagon Taste Ranger system already has a tiered progression:

| Tier | SAA Score | Title | Meaning |
|------|----------|-------|---------|
| Scout | 0+ | 🔍 Scout | New to project evaluation |
| Ranger | 50+ | 🎯 Ranger | Active project backer |
| Curator | 200+ | 📋 Curator | Consistent quality judgment |
| **TasteMaker** | **500+** | **✨ TasteMaker** | **Proven taste — picks winners consistently** |
| Patron | 1,500+ | 🏛️ Patron | Major project sponsor |
| Luminary | 5,000+ | 🌟 Luminary | Legendary sponsor with portfolio track record |

A TasteMaker (SAA 500+) has **demonstrated good judgment**. Their project backings have a high success rate. Their taste is verified by outcomes, not self-proclamation.

### The Subscription Connection

**A TasteMaker's recommendations are worth subscribing to.**

When someone reaches TasteMaker tier, the platform suggests: "Your taste is proven. Want to share it? Create a subscription channel."

| TasteMaker Subscription Type | Content | Who Subscribes |
|------------------------------|---------|---------------|
| **"What I'm Backing"** | Weekly picks of projects the TasteMaker is backing + why | Members who want to follow smart money |
| **"Hidden Gems"** | Early-stage projects the TasteMaker spotted before anyone else | Project hunters, early adopters |
| **"The Taste Report"** | Monthly analysis of project trends, success patterns, category insights | Captains, Guild leads, serious backers |
| **"My Portfolio"** | Real-time feed of every project backed, with outcomes tracked | Members building their own backing strategy |

### The Trust Chain

```
TasteMaker backs Project X
    │
    ├── Subscriber sees: "Diana (TasteMaker ✨, 87% success rate) just backed Project X"
    ├── Subscriber trusts Diana's judgment (proven by SAA score + track record)
    ├── Subscriber investigates Project X → decides to back it too
    │
    └── Result: TasteMaker's subscription acts as a TRUST AMPLIFIER
        Projects recommended by high-SAA TasteMakers get funded faster
        But it's ONE LEVEL — Diana earns subscription revenue, NOT a cut of the project
```

**ONE LEVEL ONLY.** Diana earns Marks/Credits from her subscription. She does NOT earn a commission from projects her subscribers back. No MLM. No second-degree attribution. The subscription is for her TASTE, not for downstream revenue.

The TasteMaker badge is EARNED (SAA 500+), not claimed. It signals to potential subscribers that this person's judgment has been validated by the system. Subscribers know they're paying for proven taste, not marketing.

### Non-TasteMaker Subscriptions

**Important:** You do NOT need TasteMaker status to create a subscription. Any member can. The TasteMaker badge is a trust signal, not a gate. A Pearl Diver with 30 verified tips can offer deal subscriptions without being a TasteMaker. A Photographer with great work can offer photo subscriptions.

TasteMaker status just adds a layer of verified credibility — especially valuable for project-recommendation channels where trust in judgment is the core product.
