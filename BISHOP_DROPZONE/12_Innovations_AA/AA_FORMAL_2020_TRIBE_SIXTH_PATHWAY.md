## Innovation #2020 — Tribe Formation as Sixth Cold Start Pathway: Six Paths, Six Levels, Many Tribes

### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform wherein:

(a) The Cold Start Hub presents SIX pathways — matching the platform's 6 Production Levels — completing the symmetry:

| # | Pathway | Color | Destination |
|---|---------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Food | Orange | Food Captain |
| 2 | Manufacturing | Slate | Production Captain |
| 3 | Service | Blue | Service Captain |
| 4 | Local Business | Emerald | Business Captain |
| 5 | Guild | Purple | Guild Leader (Captain) |
| 6 | **Tribe** | **Gold** | **Tribe Elder (Captain)** |

"Six paths. Six levels. All lead to Captain."

(b) **Tribe Formation Wizard:**
  - Step 1: **Name Your Tribe** — Name, type (Family, Neighborhood, Interest, Cultural, Hybrid), description
  - Step 2: **Invite Your People** — Share link, add family members, connect to existing contacts
  - Step 3: **Set Your Table** — If Family type: auto-connect to Family Table (K130). If not: set a meeting cadence or skip.
  - Step 4: **Seed Your Treasury** — Optional first contribution to the Tribe's collective Credit pool
  - Step 5: **Your First Banner** — Launch a banner contest or upload a starting image

(c) **CORRECTION from #2019: A member can belong to ONE Guild but MANY Tribes.** Tribes are not exclusive:
  - Your **Family** is a Tribe (connects to Family Table, meal planning, shared grocery orders)
  - Your **Neighborhood** is a Tribe (geographic, local business support, Beacon Runs)
  - Your **Interest Group** is a Tribe (board gamers, gardeners, book club)
  - Your **Veterans Group** is a Tribe (shared identity, Defense Klaus priority)
  - Your **Language Community** is a Tribe (maps to hexislo.com for Spanish, etc.)
  - You can be in ALL of these simultaneously

(d) **Theme governance with many Tribes:** When a member belongs to multiple Tribes, each with its own theme, the resolution is:
  - Personal Override (costs Credits) > **Active Tribe** (whichever Tribe page you're currently viewing) > Guild > Community Default
  - On your Helm (personal space), you choose which Tribe theme applies as your default, or let the Guild theme take precedence, or accept the community default

(e) **Family Table as Tribe Feature:** The Family Tribe type auto-creates a Family Table (K130) connection:
  - Shared meal planning and grocery aggregation
  - Family Treasury (pool for shared expenses — NOT LB Card funding, which is separate real money)
  - Family Beacon Runs (discover family-friendly content)
  - Family Banner (kids can submit designs — everyone votes)

### The Six-Path Symmetry

```
6 Pathways    ←→    6 Production Levels
─────────           ──────────────────
Food                Level 0: Concept
Manufacturing       Level 1: Pre-Mint (5x)
Service             Level 2: Minted (3x)
Local Business      Level 3: Production (2x)
Guild               Level 4: Distribution (1.5x)
Tribe               Level 5: Established (1x)
```

Six is the platform's number. Six paths to enter. Six levels to progress. The symmetry is intentional.

### Patent Relevance: HIGH

A cooperative commerce platform wherein six onboarding pathways (food, manufacturing, service, local business, professional guild, community tribe) mirror six production levels in a governance hierarchy — with Tribe formation enabling multi-membership across family, geographic, interest, cultural, and hybrid groups, each with collective treasury, visual identity contests, and autonomous governance — and Family-type Tribes auto-connecting to shared meal planning and grocery aggregation infrastructure.
