## #2114 — Creator & Press Red Carpet Outreach Pipeline
**Session**: K191
**Category**: Commerce / Outreach
**Crown Jewel**: No
**Prior Art**: CRM + outreach tools exist (HubSpot, Mailchimp). But a cooperative-native pipeline that seeds Instagram creators as "undrafted" prospects, generates personalized cue card QR codes per recipient, creates universal invite codes resolving to role-specific Red Carpet experiences across 9 walkthrough types, and wires a MoneyPenny outbound queue directly to a Founder content review dashboard — that's ours.

### Description
Complete outreach pipeline connecting the Content Command Center (K190) to every person in the Opening Gambit. Components: (1) 28 Instagram creators seeded as undrafted picks in creator_draft_picks, (2) CreatorRedCarpet enhanced with cue card QR preview and attribution tracking, (3) PressJunket page at /press with guided tours (15/45/90 min), (4) Universal invite code system at /welcome/:code resolving DB codes to Red Carpet slugs across 9 walkthrough types for 100+ recipients, (5) MoneyPenny outbound queue (moneypenny_outbound table) with full lifecycle (queued → approved → sent) wired to Content Command Center "Queue for Outbound" button, (6) auto-generated personalized cue cards for all recipients in helm_content_queue.

### Innovation Markers
- Cooperative-native creator scouting (undrafted → drafted → active, no paid acquisition)
- Universal invite codes mapping to 9 distinct walkthrough types
- MoneyPenny outbound queue integrated with Founder content review
- Auto-generated cue cards for every Opening Gambit recipient

### Formal Claim
A cooperative platform outreach system comprising: a creator scouting pipeline that identifies and seeds social media creators as prospective members with attribution-tracked referral links; a universal invite code resolution system that maps alphanumeric codes to recipient-specific onboarding experiences across multiple walkthrough categories; an outbound communication queue integrated with a content review dashboard wherein approved items are automatically staged for delivery; and an automated credential card generation system that produces personalized digital cards for each outreach recipient.
