## ABSTRACT

A discipline-substrate primitive that solves Bishop AI cold-start session-open friction via a single canonical-path file (`bishop_coffee.md`) overwritten at every session-close, providing one-Read-call full-prime payload for the next session. The primitive composes with existing Bishop's Breakfast (Knight-side-written prep file) and Hand-Crank Card (BP016-canonized procedural-recovery sheet) to provide guaranteed cold-start ZEST via a single Founder-paste line.
