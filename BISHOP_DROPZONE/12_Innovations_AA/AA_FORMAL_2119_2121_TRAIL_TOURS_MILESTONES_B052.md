## #2119 — Cooperative Trail Map with Customizable Trail Markers
**Category**: User Experience / Gamification
**Crown Jewel**: No
**Prior Art**: Progress trackers exist (LinkedIn profile strength, Duolingo XP paths). But a cooperative platform where the member's journey is visualized as a treasure-map-style dotted trail with customizable Trail Markers (defaulting to Ghost icon, upgradeable to community-designed icons via bounty system or member's own Brand), where completed stops are filled circles connected by solid lines and upcoming stops are outlined circles connected by dotted lines — no prior art for this specific combination in cooperative commerce.

### Description
Visual treasure-map progress tracker for the Crow's Nest page showing a member's journey through the platform as a vertical dotted-path trail. Completed milestones (joined, explored pages, dropped beacons, earned Marks) show as filled circles with solid connecting lines. The member's current position shows their **Trail Marker** — a customizable icon defaulting to a Ghost. Members can choose from preset icons (Ant, Hen, Acorn, Compass, etc.), use their Brand from the Design Crew system, or earn new markers designed by other members through the Brand Bounty system. Trail Markers are the cooperative's version of game tokens/meeples/avatars.

### Formal Claim
A cooperative platform navigation system comprising a visual progress tracker rendered as a vertical trail map with sequential milestone stops, wherein completed stops display as filled indicators connected by solid path lines, upcoming stops as outlined indicators connected by dotted path lines, and the member's current position is marked by a customizable icon (Trail Marker) selectable from preset options, importable from the member's existing brand identity, or acquirable through a community design bounty system where other members create and submit new marker designs.

## #2120 — Grand Tour Packages with Marks-Earning Completion
**Category**: Content / Engagement
**Crown Jewel**: No
**Prior Art**: Online course platforms have sequential content completion. Guided tours exist. But themed tour "packages" within a cooperative knowledge base — structured like theme park rides where completing a package earns cooperative currency (Marks) — no prior art.

### Description
Themed tour packages built on the Guided Tour Engine (#2115). Four starter packages: The Fable Trail (intro, 10 Marks), The Maker's Walk (manufacturing, 15 Marks), The Money Map (economics, 15 Marks), The Founder's Path (full deep dive, 25 Marks). Each package is an ordered sequence of Cephas content stops. Completing a package awards Marks and appears as a filled stop on the Crow's Nest Trail Map.

### Formal Claim
A cooperative platform content engagement system comprising themed sequential content packages operating within a knowledge base guided tour engine, wherein each package defines an ordered series of content stops, member progress is tracked per-package with persistence across sessions, package completion triggers an award of cooperative currency recorded on the member's ledger, and completed packages appear as milestones in a visual progress tracker.

## #2121 — Marks Milestone Prize Panel with Three-Tab Business Launch
**Category**: User Experience / Commerce / Onboarding
**Crown Jewel**: YES — No prior art for a cooperative platform that celebrates earning milestones in real time AND at a threshold presents a THREE-TAB Prize Panel showing (1) what you can GET (spend on goods/services/branding), (2) what you can DO (accept payments, set up subscriptions, work from home), and (3) how you can MAKE MONEY (all earning paths), with contextual card rotation based on earning category — making the path from first Mark to launching a business visible in a single interface.

**Prior Art**: Achievement popups exist (Xbox, Duolingo). Business setup wizards exist (Shopify, Square). Reward systems exist (cashback). But NO platform combines: real-time milestone celebration + earning category breakdown + a three-tab prize interface that simultaneously shows spending options, platform capabilities, AND money-making paths — all within a cooperative commerce context where every spend funds another member's work. The tagline captures it: "Don't Wait for Your Ship to Come In — Launch Your Ship Yourself TODAY."

### Description
Real-time celebration system triggering on Marks milestones (1, 10, 25, 50, 75, 100, 250, 500, 1000). At 100 Marks, transforms into a **Three-Tab Prize Panel**:

**Tab 1 — "What Can I Get"** (5+ cards): PreOrder a Meal, PreOrder a Slotted Top, PreOrder a Service Near You, Get Your Logo Made (Brand Bounty), Get Your Brand Package (logo + colors + business card). Contextual rotation based on earning category.

**Tab 2 — "What Can I Do"** (5+ cards): Accept Payments Today (member payment profile), Set Up Subscriptions to Your Work (4-currency subscriptions), Work From Home (services board), Launch a Campaign (business campaign), Join a Guild (professional network).

**Tab 3 — "How Can I Make Money"** (6+ cards): Photograph Businesses (Bounty Photography), Scout Deals (Pearl Diver), Teach From Home (Cooperative Classroom), Prep & Sell Meals (Freezer Node), Back Projects Early (Plant Seeds 5x Joules), Give Feedback (press N anywhere).

Total: 16+ actionable cards across 3 tabs. Every card navigates to a real page. Every path leads to earning OR spending within the cooperative. The closed loop: earn Marks → see what's possible → launch your business → earn more Marks.

### Formal Claim
A cooperative commerce milestone celebration and business launch system comprising: (1) a real-time listener detecting when accumulated cooperative currency crosses predefined thresholds; (2) progressive popup interfaces at each threshold displaying current total, visual progress, and category-specific earning breakdowns; (3) at a designated threshold, a multi-tab prize interface with three distinct panels — a spending panel presenting goods and branding services available for purchase with earned currency, a capability panel showing platform actions the member can now perform including payment acceptance and subscription creation, and an earning panel displaying all available income-generating activities with descriptions and navigation links — where spending panel items are algorithmically rotated based on the member's primary earning categories; and (4) direct navigation from each panel item to the relevant platform page, creating a closed economic loop from earning to business launch within the cooperative.
