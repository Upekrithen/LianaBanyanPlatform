## TRACK 3: PEARL DIVER SUBSCRIPTIONS (The Recurring Revenue Model)

### The Insight

A Pearl Diver with a reputation for finding great deals has something more valuable than individual tips — they have a **consistently reliable eye**. Members would pay to get THEIR alerts first, filtered to THEIR interests, delivered to THEIR phone.

This is the subscription model: Pearl Divers can offer premium alert subscriptions to members who want curated, personalized, early-access deal intelligence.

### How It Works

```
PEARL DIVER (established, verified, rated)
    │
    ├── Creates subscription offering:
    │   "Diana's Deal Drops — San Antonio"
    │   • 5 Credits/month
    │   • Early alerts (subscribers get tips 2 hours before Resource Board)
    │   • Filtered to YOUR preferences (grocery, thrift, kids, military discounts)
    │   • Weekly digest: top 10 deals of the week
    │   • Flash alerts: time-sensitive deals pushed immediately
    │
    ├── Members subscribe via Helm:
    │   └── 5 Credits/month → Diana keeps 4.17 (83.3%) │ Platform keeps 0.83 (16.7%)
    │
    └── Subscription management:
        ├── Auto-renew monthly
        ├── Cancel anytime
        ├── Pearl Diver maintains quality or loses subscribers (market discipline)
        └── Platform handles billing, delivery, and dispute resolution
```

### Subscription Tier Examples

| Tier | Price/Month | What Subscribers Get | Pearl Diver Keeps |
|------|-----------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Free** | 0 Credits | Resource Board tips (public, delayed) | Marks only |
| **Basic** | 3 Credits | Early alerts + weekly digest | 2.50 Credits |
| **Premium** | 5 Credits | Early + filtered + flash alerts + personal recommendations | 4.17 Credits |
| **VIP** | 10 Credits | Everything + direct message line to Pearl Diver for specific requests | 8.33 Credits |

### The Subscription Math

A Pearl Diver with 100 Premium subscribers:
```
100 subscribers × 5 Credits/month = 500 Credits/month revenue
Pearl Diver keeps: 500 × 83.3% = 416.50 Credits/month
Platform: 500 × 16.7% = 83.50 Credits/month

PLUS: Resource Board Marks (free tips still earn)
PLUS: Influencer Marks (social media posts still earn)
PLUS: Let's Get Groceries integration Marks (bulk buys triggered by tips)

Total monthly income for a Pearl Diver with 100 subscribers + active posting:
  Subscriptions:  416 Credits
  Business tips:  200-400 Marks
  Social media:   100-300 Marks
  ─────────────────────────────
  TOTAL: 416 Credits + 300-700 Marks/month
```

At 500 subscribers: **2,082 Credits + Marks/month** — genuine full-time income from deal intelligence alone.

### Why Members Would Subscribe

1. **Time is money.** A working parent doesn't have time to check 15 stores. Diana already did. Her 5-Credit subscription saves them HOURS.
2. **Insider knowledge.** "This cashier applies veteran's discount without checking" is worth the subscription by itself.
3. **Flash alerts.** "Target started clearance 2 hours early — GO NOW" is only valuable if you get it BEFORE everyone else.
4. **Personalization.** "Show me only: kids' clothes, groceries, school supplies" — filtered to what matters to THIS family.
5. **Track record.** A Pearl Diver with 95% tip accuracy and 200 verified finds is worth 5 Credits/month. The reputation is visible.

### Quality Control

Subscriptions require minimum Pearl Diver qualifications:
- **Minimum 30 verified tips** on Resource Board before offering subscriptions
- **Minimum 85% accuracy rate** (community-verified tips ÷ total tips)
- **Active for 30+ days** (no instant subscription offers)
- **ADAPT Score Silver+** on Pearl Diver dimensions (Effectiveness, Durability)

If quality drops:
- Below 80% accuracy → warning + 14-day improvement window
- Below 70% accuracy → subscriptions paused, subscribers notified, can cancel
- Below 60% accuracy → subscriptions terminated, refunds issued for current month

Market discipline + platform quality floor = subscribers can trust what they're paying for.
