## Innovation #1995 — Ambassador Guru Bounty: Paid Expert Responders Across External Platforms (Discord, Reddit, etc.)

### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform for recruiting, organizing, and compensating expert members ("Gurus") who answer questions about the platform on external social platforms (Discord, Reddit, Twitter/X, Facebook groups, etc.), wherein:

(a) The platform posts "Ambassador Guru" bounties — paid positions for members who have deep knowledge of the LB system and are willing to answer questions on their chosen external platforms. (b) Gurus choose WHERE they answer: their preferred Discord server, a specific subreddit, their Twitter feed, etc. The Guru picks their territory. (c) Gurus are compensated in Marks for verified responses — tracked through the platform's unified messaging system (#1998). (d) The bounty is tiered: more questions answered with higher quality ratings = more Marks. Reputation builds over time. (e) ALL members get ONE-STOP ACCESS to reach a Guru: from their portfolio, they can send a single message or Cue Card through the platform's authenticator (the Furnace), which routes it to the appropriate external platform where a Guru is active.

### Patent Relevance: HIGH

A cooperative expert-responder bounty system where community experts are compensated for answering questions about a platform across self-selected external social platforms, with unified inbound routing from platform members through an authenticator gateway and compensation tracked via platform reputation currency.

## Innovation #1996 — The Furnace: Authenticated Cross-Platform Posting Gateway with Shirley Temple Content Policy

### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform for providing members authenticated posting access to external platforms (Discord, Reddit, Twitter/X, email, etc.) through a single gateway ("the Furnace"), wherein:

(a) Members connect their external platform accounts to the Furnace (OAuth, API keys, or platform-specific auth). (b) From their LB portfolio, a member can compose a message, Cue Card, or response and the Furnace posts it to the appropriate external platform on their behalf. (c) The Furnace includes a **Shirley Temple Policy** — a content moderation layer that: if content is flagged as potentially objectionable, ASKS THE MEMBER FIRST before sending ("What level do you want?"), and requires an email verification to continue. This protects both the member and the platform from unintended content distribution. (d) The Furnace also enables legal compliance: if the platform ever needs to restrict certain destinations (sanctions, legal orders), the Furnace is the single point of control. But this is a LAST RESORT governed by the Shirley Temple Policy — ask first, block only when legally required.

### The Shirley Temple Policy

Named for the most wholesome thing you can order: the default is "send it through, no questions asked." But IF flagged:
1. System asks: "This content may not be appropriate for [destination]. What level would you like?"
2. Member can adjust or confirm
3. If confirmed, member enters email to continue (identity verification)
4. Content sent (or not, if member backs off)

This is NOT censorship. It's a seatbelt. The member always has the final say. But the platform records the decision for compliance.

### Patent Relevance: HIGH

An authenticated cross-platform posting gateway for cooperative commerce, with a "Shirley Temple" content moderation policy that prompts users for confirmation rather than blocking, combined with email verification for flagged content and legal compliance capabilities at the gateway level.

## Innovation #1997 — Guru Unified Inbox: Receive Messages from All Connected Platforms into One Destination

### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform for aggregating messages from multiple external platforms into a single unified inbox for Guru responders, wherein:

(a) A Guru connects their external platform accounts to the Furnace. (b) The Guru specifies WHERE they want all incoming messages delivered: a specific email, phone number, Discord channel, or their LB portfolio inbox. (c) Messages from Discord, Reddit, Twitter/X, email, etc. all flow INTO the Guru's chosen destination. They answer in ONE place. (d) The response is routed BACK to the platform it came from: if a question came in on Discord, the answer goes back to Discord. If it came from Reddit, back to Reddit. The Guru doesn't need to switch between apps. (e) If the Guru wants to BROADCAST a response to all connected platforms, they can — but by default, responses go back to the originating platform only.

### Patent Relevance: HIGH

A unified messaging inbox for cooperative commerce expert responders that aggregates incoming messages from multiple external platforms into a single destination, routes responses back to the originating platform automatically, and optionally supports broadcast mode for cross-platform distribution.

## Innovation #1998 — Screenshot Broadcasting: Share Social Media Thread Screenshots Across Connected Platforms

### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform for enabling members to broadcast screenshots of social media interactions across their connected platforms, wherein:

(a) When a Guru or member has a notable exchange on one platform (e.g., a helpful Discord thread, a Reddit answer that went well), they can capture it as a screenshot from within their LB portfolio. (b) The system packages the screenshot with proper attribution and formatting for each target platform. (c) The member selects which of their connected platforms to broadcast to. (d) The Furnace posts the screenshot to all selected platforms, with platform-appropriate formatting (image post on Twitter, embedded image on Discord, etc.). (e) Multiple screenshots can be sent in sequence (e.g., a 15-screenshot thread) as a gallery or sequential posts.

This enables members to show proof of engagement: "Here's the conversation I just had helping someone on Discord" → broadcast to Reddit, Twitter, LB portfolio. Social proof that compounds across platforms.

### Patent Relevance: HIGH

A cross-platform screenshot broadcasting system integrated with a cooperative commerce gateway, allowing members to capture, package, and distribute social media interaction screenshots across multiple connected platforms with platform-appropriate formatting and sequential gallery support.
