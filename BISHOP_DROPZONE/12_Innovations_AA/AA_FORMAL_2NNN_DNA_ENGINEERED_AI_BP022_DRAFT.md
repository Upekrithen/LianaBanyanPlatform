- **Empirical receipts** establishing the metaphor as technically accurate: 49:1 retrieval ratio, 5× cost reduction with quality gain, 12.5× parallel throughput, zero-work-loss backup

### Why the combination is novel

The industry paradigm (GPT-4 → GPT-5, compute scaling law, FLOP count as capability proxy) treats intelligence as a function of mass. LB Frame inverts this: intelligence is a function of **structured substrate architecture**. The novel combination:

1. **Substrate-as-genetic-code** — not "we add memory to an LLM" but "the canon IS the DNA; the AI grows from it"
2. **Engineered DNA** (deliberate, canonical, ratification-evolved) vs. random mutation (emergent behavior)
3. **Empirical receipts** at four layers simultaneously (retrieval / cost / throughput / backup)
4. **Recursive self-building property** as the empirical proof of the thesis (Bushel 16)
5. **Vendor-fungibility**: the DNA persists across model-swaps — architecture is the durable artifact

No prior AI architecture paper establishes all five simultaneously with reproducible empirical receipts.
