### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform wherein the first members to adopt a new Cue Card role (Photographer, Pearl Diver, Home Teacher, etc.) receive diminishing-tier pioneer rewards for **opting in** to let the platform use their real experience as showcase examples for recruiting the next wave. Pioneer rewards taper from generous (first 10) to standard (after 1,000) as the role becomes established and self-evidently viable.

### The Core Insight

**Every new Cue Card role is unproven until someone does it.** The first person to teach Spanish from home via LB has no case study to follow. The first Pearl Diver has no proof that deal-logging earns real income. The first Business Photographer has no portfolio of completed bounties.

These pioneers take the RISK of being first. They validate the role for everyone who comes after. That risk deserves reward — and that validation is the platform's most valuable marketing asset.

### The TasteTester Pattern (Existing Architecture)

The platform already has this pattern for recipes:

```
TasteTester Rewards (existing — tasteTesterService.ts):
  First 100 orders:  5 Marks + 10 Reputation
  101-500:           3 Marks + 5 Reputation
  501-2,000:         2 Marks + 3 Reputation
  2,001-5,000:       1 Mark + 1 Reputation
  5,001+:            0 (recipe is vetted — rewards stop)
```

The Cue Card Pioneer Program applies the SAME pattern to cooperative ROLES:

### Pioneer Tiers (Per Cue Card Role)

| Tier | Pioneers # | Marks Reward | Bonus | Badge | Opt-In Requirement |
|------|-----------|-------------|-------|-------|-------------------|
| **Founders' Circle** | 1-10 | **50 Marks/month** (for 12 months) | Pioneer Medallion (physical) + permanent "Founders' Circle" badge | 🏅 | Agree to: real name, real photo, real numbers used in platform marketing |
| **Trailblazer** | 11-100 | **25 Marks/month** (for 6 months) | "Trailblazer" badge | 🔥 | Agree to: anonymized case study + optional real name |
| **Pathfinder** | 101-500 | **10 Marks/month** (for 3 months) | "Pathfinder" badge | 🧭 | Agree to: anonymized stats only |
| **Early Adopter** | 501-1,000 | **5 Marks** (one-time) | "Early Adopter" badge | ⭐ | Agree to: aggregate stats (counted, not named) |
| **Standard** | 1,001+ | 0 bonus | — | — | Role is established. Standard Cue Card rewards only. |

### The Opt-In: What Pioneers Agree To

**Founders' Circle (1-10):**
- Platform can use their REAL NAME, REAL PHOTO, and REAL INCOME NUMBERS in marketing
- "Rosario earns $1,200/month teaching Spanish from her living room" — on the website, in Cue Cards, in letters, in pitches
- They become the FACE of the role
- They can withdraw consent anytime (numbers stay, name/photo removed)
- They get a physical Pioneer Medallion (brass, serial-numbered: PIONEER-TEACHER-001)

**Trailblazer (11-100):**
- Platform can use anonymized case study: "A Spanish teacher in San Antonio earns $1,200/month"
- Real name/photo OPTIONAL (their choice)
- They get a digital Trailblazer badge on their Helm profile

**Pathfinder (101-500):**
- Platform can use anonymized stats: "Average Home Teacher earns $X/month after 3 months"
- No individual identification
- Digital badge only

**Early Adopter (501-1,000):**
- Counted in aggregate only: "1,000 Home Teachers on the platform"
- One-time Marks bonus for being in the first thousand

### Why 1,000 Is the Threshold

At 1,000 examples of a role:
- The role is **statistically validated** — enough data for real averages, real income ranges, real success rates
- The role is **self-marketing** — 1,000 people doing it means 1,000 people telling their friends
- The pioneer rewards have served their purpose — the role doesn't need subsidized early adopters anymore
- The ADAPT Score has enough data to show real quality distributions

After 1,000, the role runs on its own economics. No more pioneer subsidies needed.

### SEC Notes

- Pioneer rewards are Marks (cooperative currency), NOT investment returns
- The opt-in is for MARKETING consent, not for financial participation
- "Real numbers" means actual income from services rendered — this is reporting, not projecting
- No income guarantees implied — showcase includes ranges, averages, and explicit "your results will vary"
- Physical medallions are recognition tokens, not securities

### Patent Relevance: HIGH

A cooperative commerce platform with a diminishing-reward pioneer program for new cooperative roles wherein:

(a) The first 10 adopters of a new role receive premium rewards + physical medallions for opting in as named case studies,

(b) Rewards taper across 4 tiers (10 → 100 → 500 → 1,000) following the TasteTester diminishing-return pattern,

(c) Pioneer stories become platform marketing assets (with explicit consent) that recruit the next wave of adopters,

(d) The program self-extends to every new Cue Card role template created on the platform,

(e) Physical Pioneer Medallions with QR codes function as recruitment tools linking to the pioneer's verified success story,

represents a novel role-validation incentive system for cooperative commerce career paths.
