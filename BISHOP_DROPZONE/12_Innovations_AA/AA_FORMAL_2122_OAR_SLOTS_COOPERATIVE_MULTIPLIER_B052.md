## #2122 — Oar Slots: Visual Cooperative Crew Formation with Earning Comparison
**Category**: User Experience / Commerce / Cooperative Economics
**Crown Jewel**: YES — No prior art for a visual interface that shows a member their current solo earning potential side-by-side with their earning potential in a full cooperative crew, using an oar/ship metaphor where empty "oar slots" represent unfilled roles in a cooperative venture, each slot displaying the specific role, earning potential, and multiplier explanation, with direct integration to invitation and recruitment systems.

**Prior Art**: Team formation UIs exist in gaming (MMORPG party finders, raid group builders). Revenue calculators exist (Uber driver earnings estimators). Cooperative education materials exist (ICA resources). But NO platform provides: (1) a visual side-by-side comparison of solo vs. cooperative earnings using real dollar amounts, (2) empty role slots that map to actual cooperative positions with specific earning potential per slot, (3) a physical metaphor (canoe → ship) that makes the cooperative advantage viscerally understandable, (4) integrated invitation CTAs that let members recruit others into specific empty slots using the platform's cue card system, and (5) the ability to fill multiple slots yourself to compound your own earnings. This combination — visual proof of cooperative advantage with integrated recruitment — has no prior art.

### Description
After a member selects any earning path from the Prize Panel, they see an Oar Slots visualization showing their role as a filled oar (green) alongside empty oar slots for the remaining crew positions needed to launch a full cooperative venture. Each slot displays: role name, description, solo earning estimate, crew earning estimate, and a "Why More?" explanation of the cooperative multiplier.

A side-by-side comparison shows "Your Canoe" (solo: ~$100/month) vs. "The Ship" (crew: ~$500-800/month each), using the same member doing the same work. The difference is the cooperative infrastructure — steady demand, no platform fees beyond Cost+20%, cross-role synergy.

Ship Templates are predefined per initiative: Let's Make Dinner (6 oars), Bounty Photography (3 oars), Cooperative Classroom (4 oars), Freezer Node (5 oars). Templates include real earning projections based on the platform's Cost+20% economics.

CTAs: "Invite Someone to Fill a Slot" (generates a pre-filled Cue Card) and "Fill Another Slot Yourself" (shows compatible roles the member could also hold).

### Innovation Markers
- Canoe-to-ship visual metaphor for solo vs. cooperative earnings
- Per-oar earning projections with real dollar amounts
- Ship Templates mapped to actual cooperative initiatives
- Integrated cue card recruitment for empty slots
- Multi-role self-enrollment (photographer + Pearl Diver on same walk)
- "Why More?" per-slot multiplier explanations

### Formal Claim
A cooperative commerce visualization system comprising: (1) a visual crew formation interface displaying a member's selected role as a filled position within a cooperative venture template, alongside unfilled role positions required to activate the full venture; (2) per-position earning projections showing both solo and cooperative earning estimates with explanatory multiplier rationale; (3) a comparative visualization contrasting individual earning capacity against cooperative earning capacity for the same member performing the same work; (4) venture templates defining the specific roles, headcount, and earning projections for each cooperative initiative type; (5) integrated recruitment actions that generate pre-filled invitation communications targeting specific unfilled positions; and (6) multi-role enrollment enabling a single member to occupy multiple compatible positions within the same venture, compounding their earning potential.
