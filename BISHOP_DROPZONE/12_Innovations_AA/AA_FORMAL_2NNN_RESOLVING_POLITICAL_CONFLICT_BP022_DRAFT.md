## Section 1 — Innovation Summary

### What this paper discloses

*"A Considered Approach to Resolving Political Conflict"* is the civic-coordination sovereignty paper — Paper 6 of the 12-paper Save-the-World Series.

**Thesis**: Political conflict is a coordination + information + listening problem, not an ideological problem. Most political conflict is structurally manufactured by attention-extractive media. Removing the extraction architecture removes most of the conflict.

The paper assembles 9 composing primitives (all pre-ratified and Detective-confirmed in Founder's existing writings):

1. **Political Expedition** — civic-discourse innovation; Listen-First architectural form; Behemoth Bag-1 #23
2. **Listen Before Talk** — architectural form for civic-discourse non-misusability; the platform makes attention-extraction structurally impossible
3. **People Informed** — tracking representative votes; transparency as civic-class infrastructure; members know what their representatives actually voted
4. **Having a Voice** — member-agency through Pedestal initiatives + dual-currency voting
5. **Pedestal initiatives** — 10% IP allocation; individual contribution recognition with visibility + ownership + agency
6. **Funding through voting with credits AND attention (Marks)** — dual-vector funding: Credits = direct economic allocation; Marks = attention-as-economic-vote
7. **Grassroots project creation** — members spawn projects themselves; not gated; not extracted from
8. **Speak Friend and Enter** (Tolkien Doors-of-Durin reference) — international-cooperation INTERDEPENDENCE primitive; friendship-recognition password as structural mechanism
9. **Canada 40K + Jimmy Kimmel letter** — specific international-cooperation instances and civic-influencer-class outreach exemplar

### Why the combination is novel

Prior art (voting platforms, representative tracking apps, civic tech, international cooperation forums) achieves partial political-coordination. The novel combination:

1. **Listen Before Talk as platform-structural form** — not a guideline but architectural: the platform structure makes listening-first the default, not an opt-in behavior
2. **Dual-currency voting** — Credits (economic vote) + Marks (attention-as-economic-vote) — money AND attention as democratic-class signals in one platform
3. **Grassroots project creation without gating** — member can spawn a civic project without platform approval; no editorial gatekeeping
4. **Speak Friend and Enter INTERDEPENDENCE** — international cooperation via friendship-recognition structural mechanism (not treaty negotiation, not diplomatic protocol)
5. **Complete civic-class architecture** — local (tracking representatives) + grassroots (project creation) + international (Speak Friend and Enter) in one cooperative substrate

## Section 2 — Composing Claim (Scaffold)

*Constructed from `paper_a_considered_approach_to_resolving_political_conflict_canon_bp021.eblet.md`. Founder + counsel refine at fire-time.*

**Composing Claim — Resolving Political Conflict via Cooperative Civic-Coordination Architecture**

Method + system for cooperative-platform civic-discourse-and-international-cooperation sovereignty where:

(i) a **Political Expedition** primitive provides civic-discourse coordination with a Listen-First architectural form: the platform's structure ensures listening precedes speaking, making attention-extraction and manufactured-conflict architecturally impossible within the platform's cooperative civic-coordination surfaces;

(ii) a **People Informed** primitive provides transparent tracking of representative votes: members can see what their elected representatives actually voted, when, and with what stated rationale — civic transparency as platform infrastructure rather than journalistic investigation;

(iii) a **Having a Voice** primitive provides member civic-agency through three composing mechanisms: (a) Pedestal initiatives (10% IP allocation per `project_ip_load_balancing_v2.md` giving individual contributors visibility + ownership + agency over their civic contributions), (b) funding through voting with Credits (direct economic allocation), and (c) funding through voting with attention via Marks (attention-as-economic-vote — time + engagement = funding signal without capital requirement);

(iv) member-originated **grassroots project creation** is ungated: any member can spawn a civic coordination project without platform editorial approval, allocating Credits and/or Marks to the project via the cooperative-platform funding architecture;

(v) a **Speak Friend and Enter** (INTERDEPENDENCE) primitive provides international-cooperation coordination: a friendship-recognition structural mechanism (referencing Tolkien's Doors-of-Durin password-of-entry pattern) enables cooperative-platform members across national jurisdictions to establish cross-border cooperative projects via mutual recognition without requiring diplomatic-protocol infrastructure;

(vi) the dual-currency voting architecture (Credits + Marks) separates economic-class participation from attention-class participation in civic decision-making: holders of economic capital use Credits; holders of time and engagement use Marks; both signals are structurally co-equal in the governance architecture;

(vii) the **Listen Before Talk** architectural constraint is implemented at the platform-design layer, not as a community guideline or moderation policy: the interaction architecture requires engagement with prior contributions before adding new ones, structurally reducing reactive conflict generation;

(viii) the cooperative-platform layer (Liana Banyan) provides the substrate on which all civic-coordination primitives compose: the same three currencies, Pedestal allocation, and Hive primitive that run the cooperative economy run the civic-coordination architecture;

(ix) the paper composes as the political-conflict-resolution sovereignty leg of the 12-paper Save-the-World Series, providing the civic-class architecture on which Paper 12 (Earth Stewardship, cross-jurisdictional coordination) depends.
