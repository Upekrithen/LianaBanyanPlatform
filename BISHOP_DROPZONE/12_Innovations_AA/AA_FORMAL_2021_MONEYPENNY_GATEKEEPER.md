## Innovation #2021 — MoneyPenny Gatekeeper: AI Receptionist for Inbound Unknown Contact Screening

### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform wherein the AI executive assistant (MoneyPenny) serves as a gatekeeper for inbound communications from people NOT on the Founder's invite/contact list:

(a) **The Problem:** The Founder needs to be reachable by people who matter — potential partners, press, collaborators, even the occasional Robert Herjavec — but can't afford to wade through spam, cold pitches, and noise. A traditional "contact us" form goes nowhere. A public email gets buried.

(b) **The Solution:** MoneyPenny sits at the gate. She receives ALL inbound contact attempts and performs intelligent triage:

  **Tier 1 — Known Contacts (Invite List):**
  - Passes through immediately to the Founder
  - SMS notification via MoneyPenny SMS: "Message from [Name] — [subject]"
  - No screening needed

  **Tier 2 — Recognized Names (Public Figure Detection):**
  - MoneyPenny identifies the sender against a known-figures database (investors, press, industry leaders, government, academics)
  - Routes to Founder with PRIORITY tag + context: "Robert Herjavec reached out. Shark Tank investor. Cybersecurity background. Message: [summary]"
  - Sends polite holding response: "Thank you for reaching out. I've flagged your message for priority review. — MP"

  **Tier 3 — Unknown but Relevant:**
  - MoneyPenny analyzes the message content for relevance to Liana Banyan's mission
  - Scores on: cooperative commerce relevance, business potential, partnership opportunity, press inquiry, member issue
  - Routes to Founder with context + relevance score
  - Sends holding response: "Your message has been received and will be reviewed. — MP"

  **Tier 4 — Spam / Irrelevant:**
  - Silently logged (audit trail) but not forwarded
  - No response sent (doesn't confirm the contact address is active)
  - Periodic summary to Founder: "Blocked 47 messages this week. 3 flagged for your review."

(c) **The Interface:**
  - Public-facing: Contact form on the platform (all portals), email address (support@lianabanyan.com), and potentially a Cephas "Contact" page
  - MoneyPenny-facing: All inbound messages land in `moneypenny_inbox` (existing table)
  - Founder-facing: SMS summary via MoneyPenny SMS (now deployed!), plus inbox in the Helm
  - MoneyPenny uses Claude API to analyze, summarize, and score each message

(d) **Founder Controls:**
  - Whitelist: add contacts to always-pass-through list
  - Blacklist: add contacts/domains to always-block list
  - Priority keywords: specific topics that always get flagged (e.g., "patent", "cooperative", "partnership")
  - Quiet hours: MoneyPenny holds non-urgent messages until morning

### Integration Points

- **MoneyPenny SMS** (now DEPLOYED): Priority messages trigger SMS notification
- **MoneyPenny Inbox** (existing): All messages stored and categorized
- **MoneyPenny Daily Digest** (existing): Summary of gatekeeper activity
- **MoneyPenny Signal** (existing): Threshold alerts for unusual patterns
- **Crown Letters**: If a Crown Letter recipient responds, MoneyPenny recognizes them as Tier 1

### The Pitch

"You can reach us anytime. MoneyPenny will make sure your message gets to the right person. If it's urgent or important, she'll flag it immediately. If you're someone we've been hoping to hear from, she'll know."

### Patent Relevance: HIGH

A cooperative commerce platform wherein an AI executive assistant serves as an intelligent gatekeeper for inbound communications — performing multi-tier triage (known contacts, recognized public figures, relevance-scored unknowns, and spam filtering) with Claude API-powered message analysis, SMS escalation for priority contacts, and Founder-configurable controls — represents a novel application of AI-mediated communication management for cooperative platform founders.
