## Claim shape (method + system)

Method + system for cooperative-platform commercial revenue where:

1. **Members opt-in** to allow their data-contribution into curated-and-tested Scribe slices
2. **Non-Federation users purchase** per-topic OR per-category access to distilled Scribe slices via subscription OR one-time payment
3. **Pricing computed** as `(member-data-licensing-share-back-pay × N_contributing_members) × Cost+20%-platform-margin`; per-category bundles apply 0.70 discount factor; subscription-annual ≈ 5× one-time (5-year amortization favoring loyalty)
4. **Member-share-back computed** via per-user data-stamping provenance proportional to data-contribution
5. **Excalibur tag-assignment gated** by 4 gates: Cathedral Effect cross-vendor verification (#2278 — lift ≥+30pp HOT) + Furnace gear-tooth-fit (KN044) + Adversarial Fence Testing (BP015 protocol) + Federation member-vote (configurable quorum + threshold)
6. **Anti-extraction by structural form**: Cost+20% binds platform itself; Member-share-back loops value to contributors; subscription is to UPEKRITHEN LLC commercial path (Apache 2.0); LB Corp stays AGPL cooperative-baseline (no commercial paywall on Federation)
7. **Post-hoc enforcement** via Marked Exception in Cooperative Defensive Patent Pledge (#2260) framework — no preemptive viewpoint-based vetting; Federation-member annual subscriber-conduct-review enforces; per-user data stamping makes misuse traceable
8. **Naming "Excalibur"** earns through structural test — only Scribe slices that pass all 4 tag-assignment gates earn the Excalibur tag; un-tested slices stay as "raw Federation Library" cooperative-knowledge-store
