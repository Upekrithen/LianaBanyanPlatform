## #2123 — Elbow Grease: Visual Effort Scale with Flippable Badge
**Category**: User Experience / Gamification / Onboarding
**Crown Jewel**: YES — No prior art for a cooperative platform effort-scaling system using a physical-metaphor icon (oil can) that assigns numbered difficulty levels to all platform activities, displays as a clickable badge that flips to reveal process steps and cooperative earnings comparisons, and integrates with the X-Ray Goggles system to show effort requirements on every discoverable element.

**Prior Art**: Difficulty indicators exist (game ratings E/T/M, recipe difficulty stars, Yelp price ranges $$$$). But NO platform combines: (1) a 10-level effort scale mapped to specific cooperative activities with Marks earning ranges, (2) a flippable badge UI that shows front (level + description) and back (process + solo vs. crew earnings), (3) integration with an X-Ray inspection system so every element shows its effort requirement, and (4) a community design bounty (Lark) for creating the icon itself.

### Description
A 10-level effort scale called "Elbow Grease" using an old-timey oil can icon (Wizard of Oz Tin Man reference). Every activity on the platform gets an Elbow Grease level:

- Level 1 (Browse): Read articles, explore pages, take tours — 1-10 Marks
- Level 2 (Engage): Give feedback, complete 100 Marks, drop beacons — 10-25 Marks
- Level 3 (Research): Find businesses online, send Cue Cards, play Golden Key Codebreakers — 25-50 Marks
- Level 4 (Create): Design icons/Larks, write content, make Brand packages — 50-100 Marks
- Level 5 (Scout): Bounty Photography in person, Pearl Diver deal logging — 100-200 Marks
- Level 6 (Pitch): Walk into business with Red Carpet card, make the pitch — 200-350 Marks
- Level 7 (Launch): Start project/campaign, set up storefront, accept payments — 350-500 Marks
- Level 8 (Captain): Build crew (fill Oar Slots), manage Node — 500-750 Marks
- Level 9 (Forge): Cold Start initiative — 10 businesses, crew, revenue flowing — 750-1000 Marks
- Level 10 (Founder): Multiple Nodes, train Captains, expand cities — 1000+ Marks

The badge is a clickable UI element:
- **Front**: Oil can icon + "Elbow Grease Level X" + one-line description
- **Back** (on click/flip): Process steps + reward comparison showing solo vs. crew earnings at that level

Integrated with X-Ray Goggles: every element in X-Ray mode shows its Elbow Grease level badge. The Lark for designing the oil can icon is itself a Level 4 Elbow Grease activity.

### Formal Claim
A cooperative platform effort-scaling system comprising: (1) a multi-level difficulty classification assigning numeric effort levels to all platform activities based on required investment of time, skill, and physical action; (2) a visual badge interface using a physical-metaphor icon that displays the activity's effort level and, when activated, transitions to reveal a detailed process description with comparative earnings data showing individual versus cooperative team income; (3) integration with a platform inspection overlay that annotates discoverable elements with their effort classification; and (4) per-level Marks earning ranges that scale with effort, demonstrating that higher cooperative effort yields proportionally higher cooperative returns.

## #2124 — Printable Cue Card Business Cards with Red Carpet QR
**Category**: Outreach / Commerce
**Crown Jewel**: No
**Prior Art**: QR code business cards exist (Vistaprint, Canva). Referral cards exist. But a member-generated, print-ready business card that encodes a Red Carpet URL specific to the target business, includes the member's Brand/logo, and earns the member Marks when the business scans and onboards — integrated with a cooperative verification system — no prior art for this specific combination.

### Description
Extension of the Cue Card system (Innovation #1546) to produce printable business-card-sized layouts (3.5" × 2") containing:
- Member's Brand/logo (or default LB branding)
- Target business name
- QR code encoding `lianabanyan.com/red-carpet?for={business-slug}&from={member-slug}`
- Tagline and brief value proposition
- Member's contact info (optional)

Members generate these from the Cue Card Generator tool, download as PDF, and either print at home or order through an integrated print service. When the business scans the QR code, they enter the Red Carpet verification flow. Successful onboarding earns the member Seeder Marks (Elbow Grease Level 6).

### Formal Claim
A cooperative member outreach system comprising a printable business-card generator that produces download-ready layouts containing a QR code encoding a verification URL specific to both the generating member and the target business, the member's brand identity, and a value proposition, wherein scanning the QR code initiates a domain-verified onboarding flow for the target business, and successful business onboarding triggers cooperative currency rewards for the generating member.
