### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform wherein members ("Pearl Divers") earn cooperative currency (Marks) for discovering and logging local deal intelligence — store discount schedules, stacking discount combinations, clearance timing, hidden sources, price comparisons, and seasonal alerts — to a cooperative Resource Board, with an OPTIONAL influencer track that allows Pearl Divers to post their discoveries to personal social media for additional Marks and follower attribution rewards, all using the same zero-storage metadata architecture as the Bounty Photography Network (#2100).

### The Two-Track Architecture

```
PEARL DIVER (any member who shops)
    │
    ├── TRACK 1: QUIET PEARL (Resource Board only)
    │   ├── Log deal to LB Resource Board
    │   ├── Members verify ("I confirmed this deal")
    │   ├── Verified tips earn Marks
    │   ├── Tips feed into Let's Get Groceries routing
    │   └── No social media required. No audience. Just tips.
    │
    └── TRACK 2: PEARL INFLUENCER (Resource Board + Social Media)
        ├── Everything from Track 1
        ├── Post hauls/finds to social media (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube)
        ├── Platform stores metadata reference (~1 KB, same as #2100)
        ├── Additional Marks for social posts
        ├── Follower signups earn attribution Marks (ONE LEVEL)
        └── Career path from casual to full-time deal influencer
```

### Why This Is Valuable

**The knowledge Diana Jones carries in her head is worth thousands of dollars per year to people who don't have it.**

When she knows that Goodwill on Bandera has 50% off color-tag Tuesdays, AND that veteran's 20% stacks on top (= 60% total discount), AND that the best inventory arrives Monday afternoon — that's $50-100 saved on a single trip. Multiply by 50 trips/year = $2,500-$5,000 in savings.

One Pearl Diver in a city helps hundreds of members save that kind of money. Fifty Pearl Divers in a city create a LIVING MAP of every deal, every discount, every stacking opportunity — updated daily.

No existing platform does this. Couponing apps (Honey, RetailMeNot, Rakuten) track ONLINE codes. They don't track:
- Which physical store has 50% off on which day
- Whether discounts stack (veteran + sale + clearance)
- Best times to arrive for fresh inventory
- Hidden sources (this Dollar Tree carries Tide; that one doesn't)
- Seasonal timing ("back-to-school clearance starts Monday at THIS store")

This is HUMAN intelligence. It requires feet on the ground, eyes in the aisles, and the social knowledge of which cashier applies the veteran discount without asking.

### The Let's Get Groceries Integration

Pearl Diver tips flow directly into cooperative purchasing:

```
Pearl Diver logs: "HEB has 25lb flour bags at $8.99 this week (normally $14.99)"
    │
    └── Let's Get Groceries system:
        ├── Alerts all members within 10 miles
        ├── Offers cooperative bulk buy: "12 families want flour — carpool to HEB?"
        ├── Calculates: 12 × $8.99 = $107.88 total (vs. $179.88 normal = $72 saved)
        ├── Pearl Diver earns: 2 (tip) + 5 (verified) + 15 (fed bulk buy) = 22 Marks
        └── All 12 families save money. Platform takes nothing extra.
```

**Influencer Marks (in addition to Resource Board Marks):**
- Social media post with deal content: +2-5 Marks per post (zero-storage, same as #2100)
- Follower signs up via attribution link: +5 Marks (ONE LEVEL)
- Follower completes a purchase using a logged deal: +2 Marks (ONE LEVEL)

**Career potential at scale:**
- 1,000 followers: 500-1,500 Marks/month
- 10,000 followers: 1,500-5,000 Marks/month
- 50,000 followers: 5,000-15,000+ Marks/month (legitimate full-time income)

**BUT:** The quiet track ALWAYS works. No audience required. No content creation pressure. Just log deals, earn Marks, help neighbors save money. The influencer track is a BONUS, not a requirement.

### Patent Relevance: HIGH

A cooperative commerce platform with a dual-track resource intelligence system wherein:

(a) Members ("Pearl Divers") earn cooperative currency for logging local deal intelligence (discount schedules, stacking combinations, clearance timing, hidden sources, price comparisons) to a cooperative Resource Board verified by community confirmation (Track 1 — no social media required), AND

(b) The same members may optionally post discoveries to personal social media for additional cooperative currency rewards and follower attribution (Track 2 — influencer), with zero incremental storage cost (metadata pointers only),

with verified deal tips automatically feeding cooperative purchasing systems (Let's Get Groceries), bulk buying coordination, and cross-city intelligence sharing through Guild networks —

represents a novel community-sourced deal intelligence marketplace for cooperative commerce.

**No existing platform** combines:
1. Community-verified local deal intelligence logging
2. Dual-track: quiet resource finder OR public influencer (same system)
3. Zero-storage metadata architecture
4. Direct integration into cooperative purchasing (Let's Get Groceries)
5. Stacking discount intelligence (veteran + sale + clearance combinations)
6. Physical store schedule mapping (not just online coupon codes)
7. Guild formation for cross-city deal intelligence networks
8. Attribution chain with ONE LEVEL Marks rewards

Existing coupon/deal platforms (Honey, RetailMeNot, Rakuten, Ibotta) focus on ONLINE codes and cashback. They do NOT map physical store discount schedules, stacking combinations, inventory timing, or hidden sources. This is the physical-world deal intelligence layer that doesn't exist yet.
