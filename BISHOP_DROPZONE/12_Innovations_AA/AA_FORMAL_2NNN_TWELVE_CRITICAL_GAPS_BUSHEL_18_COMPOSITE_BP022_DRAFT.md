## Section 1 — Field of Invention

This invention relates to cooperative-platform structural-architecture audit-and-closure cascades. More specifically, it relates to a composite-class disclosure of 12 critical-gap closure events surfaced during a Bushel-15-class architectural audit, addressed through a Bushel-18-class closeout cascade, using the KNOW-WHAT-WE-ARE framing pattern that surfaces gaps between the platform's *self-description* and its *operational reality* and closes the gaps through structural primitive-reconciliation.

The invention addresses a structural tension long present in evolving cooperative platforms: as new primitives ratify and new substrate canonical evolves, gaps appear between the self-description (*"what we say we are"*) and the operational reality (*"what runs in production"*). Without periodic audit-and-closure cascades, these gaps compound into synchronization debt that erodes member trust + canonical coherence + filing-eligible novelty.

## Section 2 — Background / Prior Art

Prior art in this field comprises:

- **Software-engineering tech-debt audits** — typically per-codebase / per-team; not coupled to canonical-substrate self-description; not surfacing as filing-eligible structural-primitive class
- **Compliance-audit closeout cascades** (regulatory class) — typically per-rule / per-control; opaque to the audited party until report; not coupled to live-substrate provenance
- **Living documentation systems** — typically markdown-class drift between docs and code; addressed via per-document edits; not addressed as composite-class structural-audit events
- **LB Frame Bushel-class composing receipts** (LB-CODEX series) — composing-receipt-class events stamping multi-primitive ratifications to canonical substrate; the present invention applies the Bushel-class composing-receipt pattern to audit-and-closure events specifically (Bushel 15 audit; Bushel 18 closeout cascade)

The novel combination is a **composite audit-and-closeout cascade** — Bushel-15-class audit surfaces 12 critical gaps; Bushel-18-class closeout cascade closes 10 gaps + re-classifies 2; both events stamp to canonical substrate as composing receipts; all under the KNOW-WHAT-WE-ARE framing — operating under cooperative-license + member-cooperative-ownership.

## Section 3 — Summary of the Invention

A method and system for cooperative-platform composite audit-and-closure cascade where:

(i) a **Bushel-class audit event** (Bushel 15 in this disclosure) surfaces a set of N critical gaps between the platform's canonical self-description and its operational reality (N=12 in this disclosure) under the **KNOW-WHAT-WE-ARE** framing pattern;
(ii) gaps fall into structurally-distinct classes — synchronization debt (canon vs. implementation), provenance drift (claim-graph vs. ledger), naming collision (Codex / Bushel / primitive name overlaps), false-positive gating (Augur-class), stale labels (pending-ratification on operational primitives), missing receipts (claimed-but-unreproduced empirical anchors), incomplete cohort coverage (Federation vs. Lone Wolf parity), unbound primitives (orphan canon Eblets without filing target), incomplete trademark coverage (pending Cluster K marks), incomplete pollination (pending Wrasse-trigger updates), incomplete documentation (memory pointer drift), and incomplete UX (pending Helm / Cephas surface productization);
(iii) a **Bushel-class closeout cascade event** (Bushel 18 in this disclosure) addresses the gaps via structural primitive-reconciliation — 10 of 12 gaps closed via direct-closure-action (canon edit / receipt-binding / rename / registry update / etc.) and 2 of 12 gaps re-classified (initially-surfaced-as-gap → re-classified as not-actually-gap-or-different-class);
(iv) both audit-event and closeout-cascade-event stamp to the Year of Jubilee append-only ledger (#2308) at their respective Chronos timestamps under BC / AD calendar convention;
(v) the closeout cascade uses the **Maintenance Scribe** (BP021 Crown-Jewel-class new Scribe class) as the operational primitive performing synchronization-debt reconciliation;
(vi) per-gap closure events are themselves stamped as Pheromone-Anchored Decisions (KN050) with full criteria/decision trace;
(vii) the composite audit-and-closure pattern composes recursively — subsequent Bushel-class audits will surface new gaps, which subsequent Bushel-class closeouts will close;
(viii) **BC / AD calendar convention** governs the time-axis canonical labeling.

## Section 4 — Detailed Description

### 4.1 The KNOW-WHAT-WE-ARE framing (Bushel 15 audit lineage)

The KNOW-WHAT-WE-ARE framing was canonized in Bushel 15 (BP021 audit cohort). It is a structural-audit pattern: surface every place where the platform's self-description (canonical Eblets, MEMORY.md, paper claims, A&A formal scaffolds) diverges from the platform's operational reality (production code, deployed routes, actual receipt provenance, runtime substrate state).

The framing is recursive — KNOW-WHAT-WE-ARE applies to the audit framing itself. If the audit's own description diverges from its own findings, that divergence is itself a gap.

**Claim 1 — Composite Audit-and-Closeout Cascade Method**
A computer-implemented method for cooperative-platform composite audit-and-closure cascade comprising: (i) executing a Bushel-class audit event surfacing N critical gaps between the platform's canonical self-description and its operational reality under a KNOW-WHAT-WE-ARE framing pattern, where N = 12 in the disclosed embodiment; (ii) classifying each gap into a structurally-distinct gap class; (iii) executing a Bushel-class closeout cascade event addressing each gap via structural primitive-reconciliation, where M of N gaps close via direct closure-action and the remainder re-classify, with M = 10 of 12 in the disclosed embodiment; (iv) stamping both audit-event and closeout-cascade-event to a Year of Jubilee append-only ledger at their respective Chronos timestamps under BC / AD calendar convention.

**Claim 2 — KNOW-WHAT-WE-ARE Framing Pattern**
The method of Claim 1 wherein the audit framing is the KNOW-WHAT-WE-ARE pattern: surfacing every divergence between the platform's self-description (canonical Eblets, memory pointers, paper claims, A&A formal scaffolds) and the platform's operational reality (production code, deployed routes, actual receipt provenance, runtime substrate state); the framing is recursive — KNOW-WHAT-WE-ARE applies to the framing itself.

**Claim 3 — Gap Class Taxonomy**
The method of Claim 1 wherein gaps fall into at least the following structurally-distinct classes: (a) synchronization debt (canon vs. implementation); (b) provenance drift (claim-graph vs. ledger); (c) naming collision (Codex / Bushel / primitive name overlaps); (d) false-positive gating (Augur-class); (e) stale labels (pending-ratification on operational primitives); (f) missing receipts (claimed-but-unreproduced empirical anchors); (g) cohort coverage gap (Federation vs. Lone Wolf parity); (h) unbound primitives (orphan canon Eblets without filing target); (i) incomplete trademark coverage (pending Cluster K marks); (j) incomplete pollination (pending Wrasse-trigger updates); (k) memory pointer drift (thin-pointer vs canonical content drift); (l) incomplete UX (pending Helm / Cephas surface productization).

**Claim 4 — Maintenance Scribe Composition**
The method of Claim 1 wherein the closeout cascade uses a Maintenance Scribe (BP021 Crown-Jewel-class new Scribe class — synchronization-debt reconciler) as the operational primitive performing per-gap reconciliation; the Maintenance Scribe detects canon-vs-implementation drift, ledger-vs-reality mismatches, and stale labels on operational primitives; the closeout cascade is the first composite-class application of the Maintenance Scribe at productization scale.

**Claim 5 — Pheromone-Anchored Decision Stamping**
The method of Claim 1 wherein per-gap closure events are stamped as Pheromone-Anchored Decisions (KN050) with full criteria/decision trace; closure events log the gap class, the divergence-surface description, the closure mechanism applied, and the post-closure verification receipt; re-classification events similarly log the original class assignment, the re-classified class, and the rationale.

**Claim 6 — Recursive Audit-and-Closeout Property**
The method of Claim 1 wherein the audit-and-closure cascade pattern composes recursively: subsequent Bushel-class audits surface new gaps (including any divergence between the prior closeout-cascade's description and its own outcomes); subsequent Bushel-class closeouts close the new gaps; the recursive property ensures the platform's self-description and operational reality converge over time toward asymptotic alignment.

**Claim 7 — Composite vs. Per-Gap Filing Posture Disclosure**
The method of Claim 1 disclosed in two filing postures: (a) **composite-class formal** — one A&A covers all N sub-claims under a single umbrella; (b) **separate-per-gap formals** — N individual A&A formals branch from an umbrella reference doc; both postures are claimed as alternatives; per-gap claim drafting is preserved across both postures.
