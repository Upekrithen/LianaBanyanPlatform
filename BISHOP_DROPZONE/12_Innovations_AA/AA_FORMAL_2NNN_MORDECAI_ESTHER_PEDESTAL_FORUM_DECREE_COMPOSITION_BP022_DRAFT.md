## Section 1 — Field of Invention

This invention relates to cooperative-platform paper-class artifact collaboration architectures. More specifically, it relates to a decree-composition mechanism enabling member additions to immutable published artifacts where additions are themselves immutable, co-equal in authority to the original, and emerge collectively as a discussion forum without editorial-class moderation authority concentration.

The invention addresses a structural tension long present in publication architectures: once a work is published canonically, it cannot be safely edited (lossy revision destroys provenance), yet collaboration-class participation is essential for member sovereignty + cooperative-platform civic-class engagement. The invention resolves this tension by structural form rather than by policy convention.

---

## Section 2 — Background / Prior Art

Prior art in this field comprises:

- **Wikipedia-class collaborative editing** — content is mutable; edits overwrite or layer over previous content; editorial-class moderation (admin / steward / arbcom) decides which contributions persist. Solves collaboration but sacrifices immutability + concentrates editorial authority.
- **Comment-thread architectures** (Disqus / Substack comments / academic-paper margin notes / GitHub discussions) — original is immutable; additions are second-class citizenship (comment-class, threaded under the primary text). Solves immutability but does not give comments co-equal authority; readers and citation-graphs treat the primary as canonical and comments as ephemeral.
- **Append-only ledgers** (blockchain / Git / event-sourcing) — originals + additions are all immutable + co-equal; but the ledger does not surface additions as Pedestals with visibility + ownership + agency in the reader-experience layer.
- **Biblical/historical precedent — the Law of the Medes and Persians** (Esther 8 + Daniel 6, ~5th century BC) — establishes the doctrinal mechanism of decree-composition: a king's decree once signed cannot be revoked (Esther 8:8: *"for the writing which is written in the king's name, and sealed with the king's ring, may no man reverse"*); but a SECOND decree of co-equal authority may be issued that contradicts or extends the first (Esther 8:11-13: Mordecai and Queen Esther's response to Haman's death-decree against the Jews). Both decrees coexist; the population chooses which to follow; the new decree effectively neutralizes the old without revising it. Civilization-class precedent — *"as old as Persia."*
- **Cooperative-platform Pedestal allocation** (LB 10% IP-allocation framework, `project_ip_load_balancing_v2.md`) — sister-class allocation primitive: 10% of innovation-IP allocates to individual Pedestals giving member-contributors visibility + ownership + agency. The present invention specializes this Pedestal pattern for paper-class artifact collaboration.

The novel combination is a **decree-composition mechanism** mapping the biblical Mordecai-Esther pattern onto a cooperative-platform append-only-ledger substrate, surfacing additions as member-Pedestals with co-equal authority, and emerging collectively as a discussion forum without editorial-class moderation — operating under cooperative-license + #2260 Cooperative Defensive Patent Pledge umbrella for IP-class additions.

## Section 3 — Summary of the Invention

A method and system for cooperative-platform paper-class artifact collaboration where:

(i) the original published artifact (paper, canon, member-content) is **immutable** per append-only-ledger substrate-architecture (substrate-as-immutable-backup);
(ii) member-contributors may author **additions** that are CONTRADICTORY-OR-EXTENDING-OR-BOTH relative to the original;
(iii) additions stamp into the same append-only ledger with **co-equal authority** to the original — neither addition nor original may be retroactively revised;
(iv) each addition receives a **Pedestal** — a member-visible surface conveying visibility + ownership + agency to the addition's author;
(v) the composed-original-plus-additions emerges as the **discussion forum** without editorial-class moderation authority concentration;
(vi) the mechanism follows the biblical-historical **Mordecai-Esther Decree-Composition pattern** from Esther 8 (Law of the Medes and Persians);
(vii) composes with the Cooperative Defensive Patent Pledge (#2260) for IP-class additions — additions can include patent-class extensions; the same coexistence pattern holds — additions don't void the original; they compose;
(viii) Year of Jubilee append-only ledger semantics (#2308) ensure every addition is forever-stamped and reconciled at canonical Year-of-Jubilee reset cycles;
(ix) **BC / AD calendar convention** governs the time-axis canonical labeling for additions stamped to the ledger.

## Section 4 — Detailed Description

### 4.1 The biblical-historical anchor (canonical reference)

**Esther 8 + Daniel 6 — The Law of the Medes and Persians**:

A king's decree, once signed and sealed with the king's ring, cannot be revoked. The mechanism is structural, not procedural — even the king cannot un-sign it. Mordecai + Queen Esther, faced with Haman's prior decree authorizing the destruction of the Jewish people, do not attempt revocation. They issue a SECOND decree, of co-equal legal force, authorizing the Jews to defend themselves and gather. Both decrees stand. The population chose which to follow. The new decree, by structural form, neutralized the old without revising it.

Per Esther 8:8: *"for the writing which is written in the king's name, and sealed with the king's ring, may no man reverse."*
Per Esther 8:11-13: the new decree of co-equal authority specifying defense rights.

The pattern: legally-valid additions that don't revise the original but compose alongside.

### 4.2 Mapping to cooperative-platform substrate

| Biblical element | Platform mapping |
|---|---|
| **King's seal** | Append-only ledger substrate (substrate-as-immutable-backup) |
| **Original decree** | The published paper / canon / member-artifact |
| **Mordecai-Esther's contradictory decree** | Member's Pedestal Forum addition |
| **Co-equal authority** | Both stamp into the ledger at the same authority class |
| **Population chooses which to follow** | Readers cite which Pedestal-additions they find load-bearing |
| **Population gathered to defend** | Cooperative-platform members compose alongside published artifacts |

The pattern is structurally identical. The platform substrate IS the king's seal; the append-only ledger IS the record-of-decrees; the Pedestal IS the surface where the addition becomes visible + ownable + agentic.

### 4.3 The Pedestal mechanism

A Pedestal is a member-visible surface where an addition sits, conveying three properties:

| Property | Function |
|---|---|
| **Visibility** | Surface placement in the artifact's discussion forum — equal-class to other Pedestals (no editorial promotion / demotion) |
| **Ownership** | Pedestal-author retains authorship-credit + IP-ledger credit-where-due stamp + share-back-pay structural form for IP-class additions |
| **Agency** | Pedestal-author may extend their own Pedestal with sub-additions, may issue subsequent decree-compositions, may engage other Pedestal-authors via cross-Pedestal references |

Pedestals are sister-class to the existing 10% IP-allocation Pedestal framework (`project_ip_load_balancing_v2.md`); the present invention specializes the Pedestal pattern for paper-class artifact collaboration.

### 4.4 The addition class — CONTRADICTORY-OR-EXTENDING-OR-BOTH

Additions occupy three logical classes:

| Class | Function | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **CONTRADICTORY** | Challenges a claim, methodology, or conclusion of the original | A Pedestal Forum addition to Paper 8 (Engineering Conducted AI) arguing that the Architecture-Beats-More empirical receipt does not generalize to model-tier-N |
| **EXTENDING** | Builds on the original; adds new claims, embodiments, applications, empirical receipts | A Pedestal Forum addition to Paper 4 (Abolishing World Hunger) adding a per-region food-sovereignty pattern not covered in the original |
| **BOTH** | Simultaneously contradicts one element while extending another | A Pedestal Forum addition to Paper 2 (DNA-Engineered AI) contradicting the recursive-self-building generalization claim while extending the substrate-as-genetic-code metaphor to a new domain |

All three classes are legally-valid + co-equal in authority. No editorial-class authority decides which class is acceptable.

### 4.5 The discussion forum emergence property

The composed-paper-plus-additions IS the discussion forum. There is no separate "comment section." There is no "editor's choice." There is no "moderator-promoted thread." Every Pedestal sits at the same surface-class as every other Pedestal, including the original. Readers navigate the forum by:

- Reading the original first (canonical-source-of-record)
- Surveying the Pedestal Forum for additions
- Choosing which Pedestal-additions to read in depth
- Citing whichever Pedestals they find load-bearing in their own subsequent additions or external citations

The discussion forum is **emergent** — it is constituted by the act of composition, not by moderator-class intervention. Per Founder direct phrasing (BP021 turn 91): *"Then it becomes a discussion forum."*

### 4.6 Composition with #2308 Year of Jubilee Ledger

Each addition stamps to the Year of Jubilee append-only ledger (#2308) at its authoring-time Chronos timestamp (BC / AD calendar convention applies). Year-of-Jubilee reconciliation cycles operate on the composed forum without removing additions: Jubilee reset reconciles claim-graph + provenance-graph + IP-allocation-distribution but NEVER deletes a Pedestal addition. Additions are forever.

Per BP020 substrate-as-immutable-backup canon: *"your work isn't gone when the tab closes — it was never in the tab."* Extended by this primitive: *"your contribution isn't blocked by published canon — it composes alongside as co-equal."*

**Claim 1 — Decree-Composition Collaboration Mechanism**
A computer-implemented method for cooperative-platform paper-class artifact collaboration comprising: (i) publishing an original artifact to an append-only-ledger substrate where the artifact is structurally immutable; (ii) receiving from a cooperative-platform member-contributor an addition to the artifact, the addition being one or more of CONTRADICTORY relative to the original, EXTENDING the original, or BOTH; (iii) stamping the addition to the same append-only ledger with co-equal authority class to the original such that neither original nor addition is subsequently revisable; (iv) presenting the addition to readers via a Pedestal surface conveying visibility, ownership, and agency to the contributor; and (v) emerging the composed original-plus-additions as a discussion forum without editorial-class moderation authority concentration — wherein the mechanism follows the Mordecai-Esther decree-composition pattern from Esther 8 (Law of the Medes and Persians).

**Claim 2 — Pedestal Property Triple**
The method of Claim 1 wherein each Pedestal conveys three structural properties to its contributor: (a) **visibility** — surface placement in the artifact's discussion forum at equal class to all other Pedestals, including the original; (b) **ownership** — authorship-credit, IP-ledger credit-where-due stamp, and share-back-pay structural form for IP-class additions; (c) **agency** — capacity to extend own Pedestal with sub-additions, issue subsequent decree-compositions, and engage other Pedestal-authors via cross-Pedestal references.

**Claim 3 — Append-Only Ledger Composition (Year of Jubilee #2308)**
The method of Claim 1 further comprising stamping each addition to a Year of Jubilee append-only ledger at the addition's authoring-time Chronos timestamp under BC / AD calendar convention; running canonical Year-of-Jubilee reconciliation cycles on the composed forum without removing additions; and reconciling claim-graph, provenance-graph, and IP-allocation-distribution at each Jubilee reset such that additions remain permanent and forever-stamped.

**Claim 4 — Cooperative Defensive Patent Pledge Composition (#2260)**
The method of Claim 1 further comprising: where an addition includes a patent-class extension claim, stamping the extension to an IP-Ledger under the #2260 Cooperative Defensive Patent Pledge umbrella; preserving the original artifact's claim coverage unchanged; composing the addition's claim coverage alongside such that extension claims supplement and contradicting claims trigger interference-resolution at a cooperative-counsel layer; and crediting the addition's author with Pedestal-credit plus 10% IP-allocation share for any registered claim originating in the addition.

**Claim 5 — Discussion Forum Emergence Property**
The method of Claim 1 wherein the discussion forum is an emergent property of the composition — constituted by the act of stamping additions to the ledger, NOT by moderator-class intervention — such that no editorial authority decides which Pedestal-additions are surface-promoted; readers navigate by reading original-first, surveying Pedestal Forum, and choosing which additions to read in depth; citation-graphs and IP-ledger credit-where-due automatically track which Pedestals readers find load-bearing.
