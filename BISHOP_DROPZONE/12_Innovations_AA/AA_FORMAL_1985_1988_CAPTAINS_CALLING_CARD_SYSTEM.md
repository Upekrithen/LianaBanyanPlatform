## Innovation #1985 — Captain's Calling Card: QR-Bearing Physical/Digital Card That Opens a Personalized Red Carpet for the Recipient

### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform for creating and distributing personalized onboarding cards ("Captain's Calling Cards"), wherein:

(a) A Captain (community business development leader) generates a physical or digital card through the platform that contains: the Captain's name and medallion (their unique identifier and reputation badge), a QR code, and optionally a brief tagline or one-line pitch. (b) The QR code on each card routes to a **personalized Red Carpet onboarding experience** tailored specifically for the intended recipient — not a generic landing page, but a curated walkthrough that includes everything the Captain would have said in person: the tiered commitment chart, the specific benefits for their business type, example revenue projections, the Family Table listing opportunity, and a one-click "I'm In" acceptance flow. (c) The Captain can hand this card to a restaurant owner, a barber, a mechanic, a potential member, or anyone — and walk away. The card does the pitching. If the recipient has no time now, they scan it later. The Red Carpet experience is waiting for them 24/7. (d) Because the card is tied to the Captain's medallion, the Captain gets full credit for any onboarding that results — whether the recipient scans the physical card, clicks the digital version, or is referred through any link tied to that card's unique identifier.

### The Hand-It-and-Walk-Away Flow

```
CAPTAIN walks into La Capital del Sabor at a busy time.

Owner: "Sorry, really busy right now."
Captain: "No problem." [Hands over a card]
         "When you get a minute, scan this. It's made for you."
         [Walks out]

LATER THAT NIGHT — Owner scans the QR code:

┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Welcome to Liana Banyan                                      │
│  🏆 Captain Jonathan Jones prepared this for you.              │
│                                                               │
│  La Capital del Sabor — Here's What We Can Do Together        │
│                                                               │
│  📊 [X] families in your area plan meals every week            │
│  💰 Your options: (tiered commitment chart)                    │
│  🍽️ Your menu is already in our Cookbook (if listed)            │
│  📱 How it works (30-second video or animation)                │
│  ✅ Ready? [Accept — Choose Your Tier]                         │
│     Not yet? [Save for Later] [Ask Jonathan a Question]       │
│                                                               │
│  "What you do in little, you do in much."                     │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### The Captain Gets Credit — Always

The card carries the Captain's medallion (unique tracking ID). Whether the recipient:
- Scans the physical card's QR code
- Clicks a digital link sent via SMS/email
- Types in a URL from the card
- Uses a secret phrase the Captain told them

...the system attributes the onboarding to that Captain. The medallion is the key.

### Physical + Digital — Captain Chooses

The Captain can generate:
- **Physical card only** — printed, handed out in person
- **Digital only** — sent via SMS, email, WhatsApp, or shared as a link
- **Both** — physical card AND a digital follow-up

All three point to the same personalized Red Carpet experience.

### Patent Relevance: CROWN JEWEL CANDIDATE

A system for generating personalized, QR-coded onboarding cards (physical and/or digital) tied to a specific community leader's unique identifier (medallion), where each card opens a customized Red Carpet onboarding experience tailored to the specific recipient's business type and local market data, with automatic attribution of all resulting onboardings to the originating leader — represents a novel decentralized, asynchronous merchant acquisition mechanism for cooperative commerce platforms.

## Innovation #1986 — Sponsored LB Cards: Pre-Funded Payment Cards as Onboarding Incentive with Sponsor Attribution

### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform for enabling any member to sponsor other people's onboarding by providing them with pre-funded LB Cards (physical or digital), wherein:

(a) A sponsoring member loads real money (from their own bank account or LB Card balance) onto a NEW LB Card designated for a specific recipient or for general distribution. (b) The pre-funded card requires the recipient to SIGN UP for the platform before they can access the funds. The signup flow is integrated: scan the card → create account → card activates → funds available. (c) The sponsor receives **Sponsorship Credit** (in Marks) when the recipient activates the card AND when they make their first purchase. The Marks reward scales with how much the sponsored member uses the platform. (d) The sponsored card carries the sponsor's medallion, so all downstream activity (purchases, referrals, onboarding) is attributed to the original sponsor.

### Use Cases

**Business Onboarding:** Captain hands restaurant owner a $25 LB Card: "Here's $25 on the house. Sign up, try buying from another LB partner, see how it works. Then let's talk about YOUR menu being on here."

**Homeless Outreach:** Member hands a person experiencing homelessness a $10 LB Card: "Sign up and this $10 is yours to use at any LB partner — food, services, whatever you need." The person gets immediate tangible value. The platform gets a new member. The sponsor gets Marks.

**Employee Onboarding:** Business Captain gives each employee a $5 LB Card: "Welcome to the team. Here's $5 to try the platform. Your lunch discount starts now."

**Community Events:** Distribute 50 pre-funded cards at a neighborhood fair: "$5 on each card. Sign up, grab lunch at La Capital del Sabor. Welcome to the cooperative."

### The Incentive Math

```
SPONSOR loads $10 onto a new LB Card:

$10 leaves sponsor's bank account → loaded onto new card
Recipient signs up → card activates → $10 available
Recipient spends $10 at LB partner → transaction completes

SPONSOR receives:
├── Sponsorship Marks for the signup (e.g., 10 Marks)
├── Referral Marks when recipient spends (e.g., 5% of first $100)
└── Ongoing attribution: anything that recipient does on LB
    traces back to the sponsor's medallion

RECIPIENT receives:
├── $10 in actual spendable money
├── LB membership (free tier activated at signup)
├── Exposure to the platform, discounts, Family Table, etc.
└── Their own medallion when they start onboarding others
```

### Physical + Digital — Sponsor Chooses

- **Physical pre-funded cards:** Ordered in batches. Each has a unique QR code. Sponsor can hand them out like business cards.
- **Digital pre-funded cards:** Sent via SMS/email. Recipient clicks link → signup → virtual card in Apple/Google Wallet.
- **Both:** Physical card mailed + digital link sent.

### Patent Relevance: CROWN JEWEL CANDIDATE

A system for creating sponsor-funded, pre-loaded payment cards (physical and digital) that require recipient account creation before fund access, with automatic sponsor attribution via medallion tracking for all downstream platform activity, enabling decentralized, incentivized community onboarding at scale — represents a novel pay-it-forward onboarding mechanism for cooperative commerce platforms.

## Innovation #1987 — Personalized QR Routing via Secret Phrases, Email Codes, and Durin's Door Conditional Access

### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform for creating dynamically routed QR codes on Captain's Calling Cards, wherein:

(a) Each QR code on a card does NOT point to a static URL. Instead, it routes to a **conditional access gateway** (Durin's Door) that determines what the recipient sees based on: a secret phrase the Captain told them in person, their email address (if the Captain pre-registered it), a public code word, a time/date window, or any combination. (b) The Captain specifies the routing rules when creating the card: "If they enter 'borrego' → show the La Capital del Sabor pitch. If they enter their email → show the personalized version I set up for them. If they just scan without a code → show the generic Captain's page." (c) This allows a single physical card design to serve MULTIPLE purposes. The Captain hands out the same card to everyone, but each person's experience is different based on what the Captain told them: "Scan this and type 'borrego' — I made something special for you."

### The Durin's Door Integration

The platform's existing Durin's Door system (conditional access based on code words/phrases) is the engine:

```
QR Code → lianabanyan.com/welcome/[captain-medallion-id]

DEFAULT (no code):
→ Generic Captain's welcome page with general info

WITH SECRET PHRASE "borrego":
→ La Capital del Sabor personalized Red Carpet
→ Tiered commitment chart pre-filled for restaurants
→ Revenue projections based on their area

WITH EMAIL "owner@lacapital.com":
→ Ultra-personalized version with their name
→ "Enrique — Jonathan prepared this for you"
→ Their specific menu items and pricing shown

WITH PHRASE "neighbor":
→ Member onboarding (not business)
→ Family Table tour
→ $5 trial card offer

WITH PHRASE "delivery":
→ Crew Call driver onboarding
→ 83.3% payout comparison
→ Sign-up-and-drive flow
```

### The Cue Card Creation Flow

The Captain builds all of this through the Cue Card system:

```
CAPTAIN opens Cue Card Generator:

1. Pick template: [Business Pitch] [Member Invite] [Driver Recruit] [Custom]
2. Customize:
   - Recipient name (optional): "Enrique"
   - Business name (optional): "La Capital del Sabor"
   - Secret phrase: "borrego"
   - Tier recommendation: C+90 (entry)
   - Personal message: "I've been eating here for years..."
3. Pick transmission:
   - [ ] Physical card (order printed cards)
   - [x] Digital QR code (generate shareable link)
   - [x] SMS via MoneyPenny
   - [ ] Email
   - [ ] All of the above
4. Schedule:
   - Send now / Schedule for [date/time]
   - Expires: [never / 30 days / 90 days]
5. [Generate] → Card created, QR code assigned, routing configured
```

### Patent Relevance: CROWN JEWEL

A system for creating dynamically routed QR codes on physical/digital onboarding cards, wherein: a single card design supports multiple personalized experiences determined by recipient-entered secret phrases, pre-registered email addresses, or conditional access rules (Durin's Door), with each routing path configured by the originating community leader through a template-based card creation tool (Cue Card system) that supports multiple transmission methods and scheduled delivery — represents a novel adaptive, multi-path onboarding mechanism for cooperative commerce.

## Innovation #1988 — Three-Layer Captain Pitch System: Printable Plan + Card + Red Carpet as Unified Asynchronous Onboarding

### Description

A system and method within a cooperative commerce platform for creating a three-layer asynchronous onboarding package that a Captain can deliver in person, by mail, or digitally, comprising:

**Layer 1 — Printable Mini Business Plan:** A customized one-page document showing the recipient what the platform offers for their specific situation. For a restaurant: tiered commitment chart, revenue projections, comparison to delivery apps. For an individual: Family Table savings, LB Card benefits, community perks. The Captain chooses: fully customized (with recipient's name and business details) or generic (category-appropriate template). Generated from the Cue Card system.

**Layer 2 — Physical or Digital Card:** A Captain's Calling Card (physical, digital, or both) with the Captain's medallion and a QR code. Optionally pre-funded with real money (Sponsored LB Card). The Captain chooses the form factor based on the situation.

**Layer 3 — Personalized Red Carpet Experience:** The destination behind the QR code. A full, curated onboarding walkthrough tailored to the recipient. Shows them everything they need to know, answers common questions, and provides a one-click path to acceptance. Available 24/7 — the recipient can scan at midnight and get the same complete experience.

### The Captain's Choice Matrix

| Situation | Layer 1 | Layer 2 | Layer 3 |
|-----------|---------|---------|---------|
| Restaurant pitch (busy owner) | ✅ Customized plan | ✅ Physical card | ✅ Personalized Red Carpet |
| Community event (50 people) | Generic flyer | ✅ Pre-funded $5 cards | Generic member onboarding |
| Homeless outreach | Skip | ✅ Pre-funded $10 card | Simplified signup + services |

The Captain picks what they need. The system generates all chosen layers as a unified package.

### Patent Relevance: HIGH

A three-layer asynchronous onboarding system for cooperative commerce, comprising: (1) a customizable printable business plan document, (2) a physical/digital card with QR routing and optional pre-funded value, and (3) a personalized Red Carpet digital onboarding experience — all generated from a single Captain-initiated workflow and tied to the Captain's unique medallion for attribution — represents a novel multi-modal, asynchronous merchant and member acquisition toolkit.
