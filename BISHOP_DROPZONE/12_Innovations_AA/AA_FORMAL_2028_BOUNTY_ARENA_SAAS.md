## Innovation #2028 — X-Ray Bounty Arena as a Service (BaaS)
**Category:** Product / Revenue / Platform Economics
**Impact:** HIGH
**Crown Jewel Candidate:** YES

### Description

The entire X-Ray Bounty Game cluster (#2023-#2027) — coin tracker, three-tier error marketplace, design auction, half-life decay, self-generating bounties — packaged as a **licensable SaaS product** that other websites and platforms can embed.

**Pricing Model:**
- **FREE** for any site that uses the LB system as a member (cooperative membership benefit)
- **Licensable SaaS** for non-LB sites (paid subscription, priced per-site or per-active-user)

This creates a **triple flywheel:**
1. **Non-LB sites** pay for the Bounty Arena → revenue for the cooperative
2. **Non-LB sites** see the LB ecosystem benefits → conversion funnel to membership
3. **LB member sites** get it free → membership retention + competitive advantage over non-members

### Architecture

The Bounty Arena operates as an **embeddable widget** (JavaScript SDK or Web Component):
- Site owner adds a `<script>` tag or imports a component
- Bounty Arena overlay activates on their site
- Error reports, documentation, and fix proposals flow into a dashboard
- Marks earned can be LB Marks (for LB members) or site-specific points (for non-LB sites)
- Non-LB sites can connect to LB later to convert points → real Marks

### Revenue Streams
1. **SaaS license fees** from non-member sites
2. **Conversion revenue** when non-member sites join the cooperative
3. **Quality improvement** across all participating sites (network effect)
4. **Data insights** — anonymized error patterns across the network inform platform-wide improvements

### Why Free for LB Members?
This follows the cooperative principle: **collective investment benefits all members.** The Bounty Arena was built by the cooperative (funded by member activity and innovation). Members get the full value. Non-members pay because they're benefiting from cooperative infrastructure without contributing to it.

The $5/year membership that unlocks real Marks (#2026) ALSO unlocks free Bounty Arena for your site. One membership, two massive benefits. This is the Guild Benefit Cascade (#2018) in action.

### What Makes It Novel
No QA gamification platform offers a "free if you're a member of our cooperative, paid otherwise" model. This inverts the typical SaaS pricing: instead of charging your best customers more, you charge outsiders and reward insiders. It's the cooperative's answer to the proprietary SaaS model.
