## Innovation #2030 — Canister System as Bounty-Funded Separate Project
**Category:** Manufacturing / Project Structure / Crowdfunding
**Impact:** HIGH
**Crown Jewel Candidate:** YES

### Description

The Canister System (#2022) becomes a SEPARATE PROJECT from HexIsle — with its own Pedestal page, its own Project Bridge, its own bounty board, and its own funding target. It launches SIMULTANEOUSLY with the SlottedTop Kickstarter (Campaign 1 of 13) but lives independently on the LB platform.

**Why separate:**
- The Canister System serves ALL makers, not just HexIsle users
- It has its own patent path (Provisional #11)
- It needs its own vertically integrated production pipeline
- Different audience: HexIsle backers are gamers, Canister backers are MAKERS

**The Bounty-Funded Manufacturing Pipeline:**

Instead of hiring a team to build the Canister System, the project posts bounties for every stage of production:

| Stage | Bounty Category | Example Tasks | Marks Range |
|-------|----------------|---------------|-------------|
| **Design** | CAD / Engineering | 3D model the S sleeve, design the screw press assembly, thermal analysis of heated barrel | 50-200 Marks/task |
| **Prototyping** | 3D Printing | Print test sleeves, test twist-lock tolerances, print sprue plugs in high-temp resin | 20-75 Marks/task |
| **Validation** | QA / Testing | Pressure test at 5,207 PSI, material flow test, cycle time measurement | 30-100 Marks/task |
| **Tooling** | Manufacturing Engineering | Design production molds for sleeve components, source ACME screws, BOM optimization | 75-300 Marks/task |
| **Production** | Assembly / Fulfillment | Assemble kits, quality check components, pack and ship | 10-50 Marks/task |
| **Documentation** | Technical Writing | Assembly instructions, material guides, safety documentation | 25-75 Marks/task |

This is the #2029 Bounty-to-Partnership Pipeline applied specifically to Canister System production. Bounty hunters who accumulate enough Marks become Contractors, then Partners — earning revenue share on every Canister System sold.

**Production targets:**
- **5,000 Canister Systems** (mix of Gravity Starter, Thermoplastic, and Combined kits)
- **15,000 SlottedTops** (HexIsle universal hex terrain adapter, Campaign 1 product)

Both ship from distributed Factory Nodes as the network grows.

### Funding Model

The project is funded through:
1. **Kickstarter co-launch** — SlottedTop campaign includes Canister System add-on tiers
2. **LB platform pledges** — Members can pledge Credits toward the Canister project on its Pedestal page
3. **Pre-orders** — Direct pre-orders on the LB marketplace
4. **The 1/3 model** — 1/3 customer pre-orders, 1/3 LB treasury, 1/3 production partner investment

### What Makes It Novel
No hardware product has been built entirely through a gamified bounty system where contributors earn their way from task worker to revenue-sharing partner. Traditional hardware startups hire employees or contractors. This builds the manufacturing team through meritocratic contribution — the same people who test the prototypes become the people who manufacture the product.

## Innovation #2031 — "DO THE WORK = GET THE STATUS" Cue Card System
**Category:** Marketing / Recruitment / Onboarding
**Impact:** HIGH

### Description

A marketing Cue Card built around the core message:

> **"Marks thresholds replace applications, interviews, and negotiations. DO THE WORK = GET THE STATUS. A teenager printing test pieces can reach Partner faster than an engineer who fills out a form and stops."**

This becomes the recruitment Cue Card for the Canister System project AND the broader LB manufacturing network. The Cue Card includes:

**Front:** "DO THE WORK = GET THE STATUS" in bold. QR code linking to the Canister System bounty board.

**Back:** The escalation ladder:
- 0 Marks → Bounty Hunter (pick up tasks)
- 500 Marks → Contractor (recurring work, 1.5x multiplier)
- 2,000 Marks → Partner (revenue share, Factory Node seed)
- "No resume required. No interview. No application. Just contribute."

**Distribution:**
- Included in every SlottedTop Kickstarter shipment
- Available at /tools/card-generator with Canister System template
- Shared by Captains during maker outreach
- Posted in r/3Dprinting, r/TerrainBuilding, r/InjectionMolding, maker spaces, FabLabs

### What Makes It Novel
The marketing copy IS the mechanism. The Cue Card doesn't just describe the opportunity — it contains the QR code that starts the bounty hunting. Every card is a recruitment tool that feeds directly into the #2029 pipeline.
