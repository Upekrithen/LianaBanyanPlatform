## Section 1 — Innovation Summary

### What this paper discloses

*"A Considered Approach to Universal Abundant Low Cost Energy"* is the energy + water sovereignty paper — Paper 3 of the 12-paper Save-the-World Series. Liana Banyan Corporation as *"The Hot Water Company"* productizes this architecture.

**Founder direct (preserve verbatim)**: *"anywhere in the world that has water, gravity, dirt, and the sun"* — the universal inputs.

The paper discloses a complete household-scale energy system from components that have existed for 200+ years (Stirling cycle, 17th-century hydraulics, 19th-century electrolysis), composed by architecture that doesn't exist yet as a cooperative-platform infrastructure:

1. **Stirling-cycle heart**: Sun heats above-ground surface to ~100-150°F; earth 5 feet below stays at ~50-60°F year-round; the ΔT of 50-100°F drives a Stirling engine — mechanically simple, repairable in the field, no exotic materials
2. **Tereno Hydraulic push-assist**: The HexIsle Water Table hydraulic architecture (Inverse Hydraulic Coupling + Sawtooth60 Directional Current + Hydraulic-to-Pneumatic Plant System) provides push-assist to amplify the Stirling's "feeble power" (Founder direct) to industrial-useful pressures
3. **Electrolysis bridge**: Pushed output drives water electrolysis → hydrogen + oxygen; hydrogen is the storage mechanism (solves intermittency)
4. **Bernzomatic-pattern canisters**: Small, refillable, plug-in hydrogen holders — universal connector; refill at the household Stirling-electrolysis station; plug into stoves, heaters, engines, generators
5. **End-use cluster**: Hot water + electricity + cooking heat + space heating + motive power from one hydrogen-canister architecture
6. **Cooperative-platform layer**: Liana Banyan productizes at Cost+20%; community-installable; member-cooperative maintenance

### Why the combination is novel

The Stirling cycle, hydraulics, and electrolysis are each 100+ years of prior art individually. The novel combination:

1. **The specific ΔT path**: not geothermal deep (expensive drilling), not solar thermal concentrating (expensive optics) — specifically the 5-foot underground stable-cool differential driven by surface sun, requiring only a shovel
2. **Tereno Hydraulic push-assist composing with Stirling**: the hydraulic architecture from HexIsle Water Table (already patented under 33 mechanical innovations) provides the pressure amplification that converts "feeble power" to electrolysis-useful current
3. **Hydrogen-canister productization**: the Bernzomatic-pattern portable-refillable format turns hydrogen from centralized utility to household consumable
4. **Universal-inputs constraint**: the complete system operates on sun + gravity + water + dirt — no rare earths, no grid connection, no special geography
5. **Cooperative-platform layer at Cost+20%**: productization as member-owned infrastructure rather than extractive energy utility
