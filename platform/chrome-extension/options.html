Windows Copilot Workaround
Windows Copilot cannot directly connect to localhost apps (no local plugin API exists as of 2026). Use the "Copy for Copilot" button in the extension popup to copy a formatted context block, then paste it into Copilot's chat window. This gives Copilot your local Mnemosyne context in one action.

The button queries your local memory, formats the result as a prompt prefix, and copies it to the clipboard. In Copilot: just paste (Ctrl+V) and ask your follow-up question.
