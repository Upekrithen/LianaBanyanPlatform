## Q5: Node Operator Revenue Share (2-Day Personal Use)

**Context**: 
- ✅ LB gets 3 days/week priority
- ✅ Node Operator gets 2 days/week for own projects
- ✅ Operator MUST use LB portals for tracking/cuts

**DECISION REQUIRED**: How does LB monetize Operator's 2 days?

### Options:
- [ ] **Flat % of Revenue** (e.g., 10% of all sales on those 2 days)
- [ ] **Per-Transaction Fee** (e.g., $5 per order processed)
- [ ] **Supply Markup Only** (Operator buys supplies at cost+%, no revenue cut)
- [ ] **Tiered Model** (Revenue share decreases as volume increases)
- [x] **Combination**: % Revenue + Supply Markup
- [ ] **Custom Model**: _________________________________

**Your Decision**:
Combination model with differential pricing for LB member projects vs. outside orders:

1. **Revenue Share**: Cost + 20% on ALL projects (LB member and outside orders)
2. **Supply Discount Structure**:
   - **LB Member Overage Preorders**: Aggregate cost discount applied (no markup) - for supplies/equipment used to fulfill prior LB project products
   - **Outside Orders**: NO supply discount applied, charged at standard rates
3. **Pricing Differential**:
   - **LB Member Projects**: Standard pricing
   - **Outside Orders**: Minimum 2x price per unit

**Specific Rate/Fee**:
- 20% revenue share on all production
- Aggregate cost pricing for LB member supply overage only
- 2x minimum unit price multiplier for non-LB orders
