# How Credits Work - System Explainer Script

**Duration**: 1.5-2 minutes  
**Style**: Clean motion graphics with icons and animations  
**Voice**: Clear, educational, friendly  
**Music**: Upbeat, modern, tech-forward

---

## SCENE 1: THE PROBLEM (0:00-0:15)
**Visual**: Split screen showing traditional work vs. gig economy. Frustrated faces, complicated payment systems.

**Narration**:
> "Getting paid for your work shouldn't be complicated. But in today's economy, payment delays, unclear compensation, and middleman fees are the norm."

**AI Prompts / Motion Graphics**:
- Animated icons: clock ticking (delays), percentage symbols (fees), confused person icon
- Color: Grays and reds (representing frustration)
- Text overlays: "Payment Delays", "Hidden Fees", "Complex Systems"

---

## SCENE 2: INTRODUCING CREDITS (0:15-0:35)
**Visual**: Clean transition to Liana Banyan interface. Bright, simple credit icon appears.

**Narration**:
> "Liana Banyan Credits are different. They're your universal currency within our ecosystem—representing both your contributions and your earning potential. Think of them as points that actually matter."

**Motion Graphics Elements**:
- Animated credit coin rotating in 3D
- Simple UI mockup showing credit balance
- Icons flowing: work → credits → rewards
- Color: Transition to brand colors (greens, golds)

---

## SCENE 3: EARNING CREDITS (0:35-0:55)
**Visual**: Three animated cards flipping to show different ways to earn.

**Narration**:
> "Earn credits in three ways: Complete projects and tasks. Purchase credit bundles with bonus multipliers. Or receive Expression of Interest credits as early investment in promising ventures."

**Motion Graphics**:
- **Card 1**: Person working → project complete checkmark → credits added
- **Card 2**: Shopping cart → purchase → bonus credits sparkle effect
- **Card 3**: Handshake → EOI badge → future equity icon
- Each card has distinct icon and animation style

## SCENE 4: CREDIT VALUE (0:55-1:15)
**Visual**: Animated chart showing credit-to-dollar conversion. Scale balancing.

**Narration**:
> "One credit equals one dollar in real purchasing power. No confusing conversion rates. No hidden deductions. What you earn is what you get—minus only the platform maintenance fee."

**Motion Graphics**:
- 1 credit coin = $1 dollar bill (animated equation)
- Transparent fee breakdown pie chart
- Scale balancing perfectly
- Text: "1 Credit = $1 USD" prominently displayed

## SCENE 5: USING CREDITS (1:15-1:35)
**Visual**: Branching animation showing multiple use cases.

**Narration**:
> "Use your credits to access premium features, purchase products from our marketplace, pay membership dues, or convert them to real cash through our withdrawal system."

**Motion Graphics**:
- Central credit icon branches out to four paths:
  - Premium features icon (star)
  - Shopping cart (marketplace)
  - Membership badge
  - Bank transfer icon
- Each path animates sequentially with smooth transitions

## SCENE 6: EOI CONVERSION (1:35-1:50)
**Visual**: Timeline animation showing EOI credits maturing into equity.

**Narration**:
> "Expression of Interest credits are special. Hold them, and after 12 months, they can convert to actual equity in Liana Banyan projects—giving you ownership in what you helped build."

**Motion Graphics**:
- Timeline: Day 1 → 12 months → Conversion event
- EOI badge morphs into equity certificate
- Percentage ownership visualization
- Growth chart trending upward

## SCENE 7: THE BIG PICTURE (1:50-2:10)
**Visual**: Network visualization. Individual credits connecting into larger ecosystem.

**Narration**:

**Motion Graphics**:
- Individual user nodes lighting up across network
- Credits flowing between nodes
- Ecosystem growing and expanding
- Color palette: Warm, optimistic colors

**Visual**: Simple signup interface with credit counter starting at zero, then showing bonus.
