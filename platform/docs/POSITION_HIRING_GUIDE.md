Shows detailed position specifications for LB's own needs:

#### Critical Priority:
- **Certified Public Accountant (CPA)**
  - Category: `accounting_services`
  - Compensation: $75-150/hr or 0.5-2% participation
  - Responsibilities: Books, tax filings, compliance, tax strategy
  - Required: Active CPA license, multi-entity experience, tax expertise

- **General Counsel**
  - Category: `legal_services`
  - Compensation: $150-300/hr or 1-4% participation
  - Responsibilities: Contracts, IP, securities compliance, governance
  - Required: Licensed attorney, business law, membership rights knowledge

#### High Priority:
- **Chief Financial Officer (CFO)**
  - Category: `accounting_services`
  - Compensation: $100-200/hr or 2-5% participation
  - Responsibilities: Financial strategy, forecasting, capital management
  - Required: CFO experience, financial modeling, capital markets

- **Director of People Operations**
  - Category: `hr_staffing`
  - Compensation: $75-125/hr or 1-3% participation
  - Responsibilities: HR strategy, contract management, participation allocation
  - Required: HR leadership, contract management, participation comp expertise

- **Executive Steward (Board Member)**
  - Category: `steward_owner`
  - Compensation: Board stipend + 0.5-2% participation
  - Responsibilities: Strategic direction, oversight, governance
  - Required: Board/executive experience, governance expertise, 4.5+ reputation

## How the Hiring Process Works

### 1. Create Position Template
**Route:** `/manage-positions`  
**Navigation:** Business Portal → Manage Positions

1. Click "Create Position"
2. Select category (e.g., `accounting_services` for CPA)
3. Fill in:
   - Position title
   - Description
   - Compensation type (participation, cash, or hybrid)
   - Time commitment options (30-365 days)
   - Requirements (skills, reputation minimum, guild membership)
   - Project lifecycle stage requirement (optional)

### 2. Members Apply
**Route:** `/positions` (for applicants)

- Qualified members see open positions
- They apply with:
  - Resume/portfolio
  - Cover letter
  - Preferred time commitment (affects participation/cash ratio)
  - Application questionnaire responses

### 3. Review Applications
**Route:** `/manage-positions` → Select position → View applications

You see:
- All applications with applicant profiles
- Reputation scores (visual blocks/stars/suns)
- Past work history on LB
- Guild memberships
- Category scores (Quality, Timeliness, Professionalism, etc.)
- Skills and credentials
- Preferred compensation structure

### 4. Conduct Interviews

All pertinent information is automatically available:
- ✅ Complete work history with verified outcomes
- ✅ Reputation scores from real projects (not self-reported)
- ✅ Guild affiliations (peer validated)
- ✅ Skill verification from multiple sources
- ✅ Compensation preferences clearly stated
- ✅ Availability and timeline

**Focus your interview on:**
- Culture fit
- Specific scenario questions
- Strategic alignment
- Communication style

### 5. Assign Contracts
**Route:** `/manage-positions` → Assign applicant

1. Select approved applicant
2. Adjust compensation if needed:
   - Original participation % → Adjusted participation %
   - Original cash amount → Adjusted cash amount
   - Original credits → Adjusted credits
3. Set duty percentage (full or partial)
4. Add assignment notes
5. Set start/end dates

### 6. Track Performance

During the contract:
- Reputation automatically updates based on interactions
- Other team members can rate them
- Work history builds for future positions

After completion:
- Contract completion recorded
- Participation/credits awarded
- Medallion updated (if applicable)
- Available for rehire with proven track record

## Information Ready for CPA/CFO Interviews

When you interview candidates for CPA or CFO positions, their profile will show:

### Financial Expertise Validated By:
- Past projects where they held financial roles
- Ratings from project owners they worked with
- Guild membership (Accounting Professionals, Tax Strategy collectives)
- Specific skill endorsements from peers

### Reputation Breakdown:
- **Quality Score:** How well they perform (4.0-5.0 is excellent)
- **Timeliness Score:** Do they meet deadlines?
- **Professionalism Score:** Communication and conduct
- **Collaboration Score:** Team player rating
- **Standards Compliance Score:** Following best practices

### Quantifiable History:
- Number of projects completed
- Total value of budgets managed (from project data)
- Success rate of engagements
- Peer recommendations from guild members

### No Manual Resume Review Needed:
Everything is pre-compiled, verified, and cross-referenced. You can focus on:
- Tax strategy discussions (for CPA)
- Financial modeling approaches (for CFO)
- Industry-specific experience
- Culture and communication fit

## Tax Strategy & Equipment Questions

The crucial decision questions likely cover:
- Equipment purchase vs. lease decisions
- Depreciation strategies (Section 179, bonus depreciation)
- Entity structure optimization
- Multi-state tax considerations
- R&D tax credits
- Inventory accounting methods

**Your CPA will need access to:**
- Project cost structures (in Project Cost Dashboard)
- Equipment lists and values
- Entity formation documents
- Multi-entity relationship diagrams
- Revenue projections

All of this data is tracked in LB's system and can be exported for CPA review.
