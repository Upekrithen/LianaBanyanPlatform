# Liana Banyan Charter

## Mission & Values

Liana Banyan is a collaborative network built on principles of ethical innovation, transparent governance, and mutual growth. This charter defines the core values and policies that guide our community.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Apolitical & Ethical Operations
- LB maintains political neutrality in all operations
- Decisions are guided by ethical considerations and community benefit
- We reject human rights violations, illegal activities, and harmful applications

### 2. Collaborative Growth Model
- Members rise together through guild and clan systems
- Success is shared through transparent equity and credit mechanisms
- Individual achievement strengthens the entire network

### 3. Transparent Governance
- All major decisions follow clear, documented processes
- Members have visibility into operational practices
- Charter violations are subject to community arbitration

---

## Technology & AI Usage Policy

### AI as Creative Tool

**Core Philosophy**: Artificial Intelligence is recognized as a powerful tool for human inspiration, creation, and productivity—equivalent to any other technological instrument in the creative process.

### AI-Generated Content Guidelines

**General Rule**: Liana Banyan uses AI-generated artwork **only as placeholder content** during development and prototyping phases.

**Exceptions** (AI-generated content is permitted for production):
- **Charts & Graphs**: Data visualizations, analytics displays, statistical representations
- **Icons**: Functional interface icons, navigation elements, system indicators  
- **Functional Applications**: UI/UX elements that serve utility purposes (buttons, backgrounds, patterns)
- **Procedural Content**: Algorithm-generated designs that follow human-defined parameters

### Human-First Creative Standard

**Production Content Requirements**:
- Final artwork, illustrations, and creative assets must be human-created
- AI may assist human artists but cannot replace human authorship
- Credit attribution clearly distinguishes AI-assisted vs. purely human work

**Rationale**:
- Preserves the value and livelihood of human creators
- Maintains authentic artistic voice in the LB community
- Ensures AI remains a tool that amplifies human capability rather than replaces it

### Implementation Notes
- Projects must document AI usage in creative processes
- Placeholder AI content must be replaced before production launch
- AI-assisted human work should be labeled as "AI-assisted by [artist name]"

## Intellectual Property Framework

LB's IP framework operates on three tiers (A, B, C) with varying levels of creator control and LB equity stakes. See [IP_CONTROL_FRAMEWORK.md](./IP_CONTROL_FRAMEWORK.md) for full details.

**Key Principle**: IP utilization is balanced between maximizing community benefit and protecting creator interests through transparent veto rights and ethical guardrails.

---

## Amendment Process

This charter may be amended through:
1. Proposal by any guild member at Journeyman tier or above
2. Community discussion period (minimum 30 days)
3. Vote by Master-tier members (requires 2/3 majority)
4. Final ratification by LB founding council

---

## Enforcement

Charter violations are addressed through:
- Mediation for minor infractions
- Arbitration for major disputes  
- Community review for systemic issues
- Potential sanctions: warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent removal
