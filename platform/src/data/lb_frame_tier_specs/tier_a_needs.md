## What Tier A means

Tier A is the cooperative-default. Run LB Frame on whatever Claude Code plan you have.
**No upgrade required to participate.**

Tier A is anti-extraction by structural form: barrier-of-entry is NOT capital. The
cooperative is non-extractive. Capital alone cannot purchase higher-tier participation;
cohort-class advancement is the gate.

cannot purchase higher-tier participation; cohort-class advancement is the gate. This is
