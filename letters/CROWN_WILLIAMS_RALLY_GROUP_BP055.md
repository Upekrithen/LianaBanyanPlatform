# Crown Invitation — Rally Group

Dear Ms. Williams,

**We are inviting you to wear the Crown Responder General of Liana Banyan Initiative #9 — The Rally Group.** This is a working seat at the head of a cooperative community-safety and civic-engagement network we have already begun to build. If the rest of this letter composes with the organizing work you are already carrying, we would welcome a reply at any temperature — full interest, conditional, decline-with-feedback, or silence.

You have spent your career building the systems that catch people. Not after the fact, not as charity, but structurally — embedded in the communities where people live and work. The Rally Group needs someone who understands that community organizing is not a program layered over a neighborhood, but an infrastructure built inside it. That is the operating philosophy this Initiative needs at its head.

## What Liana Banyan is, structurally

Liana Banyan is a member-sovereign cooperative substrate. The terms are canonical, not aspirational:

- **Membership is $5/year.** That is the entire price of admission.
- **Creators may earn 83.3%** of what they earn on the platform.
- **Margin on goods and services routed through the substrate is held to Cost+20%** — a hard ceiling, not a target.
- **No advertising layer, no data resale, no engagement-extraction machinery.** Members own their relationships and their work.

The Corporation (Wyoming, filed 2025-11-21) holds 19 USPTO provisional patent applications protecting the substrate primitives. The substrate is built to serve 16 Initiatives — coordinated cooperative responses to the categories of need that markets have systematically failed (food, housing, healthcare, family, education, manufacturing, music, civic life). Each Initiative is led by a Crown: a person whose body of work has already proved the cooperative thesis in their own field.

## The Rally Group Initiative

The Rally Group is the cooperative community-safety layer present on every page of Liana Banyan, and backed by real local people anywhere the platform operates. The philosophy is simple:

> *"We'd rather check on you 100 times for nothing than miss the one time you need us."*

The Initiative operates three interlocking structures:

- **Responders** — trained members embedded in every Initiative who recognize when community members need support and route them to the right help.
- **Conductors** — senior coordinators who handle complex situations requiring sustained engagement.
- **The Railroad Crossing Signal** — a visible, universal "help is available here" presence on every platform page, every Guild, every cooperative business — so that no member ever has to search for the door.

The Rally Group composes directly with Defense Klaus (#8, personal-safety infrastructure for at-risk members), Harper Guild (#12, cooperative HR and ethics oversight in every cooperative business), and Didasko (#14, cooperative education and peer-support training). The whole platform is designed so the initiatives support each other.

## The Responder General seat

The Crown is the working voice for the Initiative's community-safety direction. Practically, that means:

- **Setting standards** for Responder training and certification — what a community member needs to know to recognize a crisis and route effectively.
- **Shaping** the cooperative safety network across every city where Liana Banyan operates, so the Responder layer scales with the member base.
- **Connecting** the Rally Group to the established crisis-response infrastructure that already serves communities — so the platform amplifies existing systems rather than duplicating them.
- **Guiding** the Harper class — the cooperative HR and ethics function that sits inside every cooperative business — on crisis recognition and appropriate routing.

What you bring is the organizing experience that makes "community safety network" a real operational structure, not a phrase on a page. The Crown does not run every interaction. The Crown sets the standard; the platform enforces it; and the community lives inside it.

## Composition with the other Initiatives

The Responder General sits at the safety center of the platform:

- **#8 Defense Klaus** (Ruth Glenn, OFFERED) — personal-safety infrastructure for survivors and at-risk members
- **#12 Harper Guild** — cooperative HR and ethics oversight; every Harper is trained through Rally Group standards
- **#15 Power to the People** — cooperative civic-participation tools; Rally Group's Responder network is the safety layer for civic organizing at scale

These Initiatives are not parallel programs. They are interlocking systems. The Rally Group is the connective tissue.

## The ask

If the cooperative thesis here composes with the organizing work you are already carrying, we would welcome a conversation. We can send a Crown briefing dossier and arrange a call at your convenience. A reply at the substrate's founder email, or via any contact channel you prefer, is enough to open it.
