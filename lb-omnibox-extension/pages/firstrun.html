How it works

1
Search as usual. Type in Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, Perplexity, Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini — exactly as you always do.
2
Cathedral Effect runs silently. If your Helm daemon is running, the query is substrate-enriched before it leaves your browser. You get better answers automatically.
3
You choose durability. After results load, a small card appears: "Make a book of this search?" Click Save → it goes to your Personal-Permanent library. Click Not now → ephemeral, no record kept.
4
Your library, your rules. View, search, and delete any entry at any time. "Forget Everything" wipes the library in one click.
Privacy-by-default: Incognito / private-browsing sessions are always ephemeral — the curation prompt never appears. Sensitive searches (medical, financial, legal, political) also default to ephemeral unless you explicitly opt in per category in Settings. This is not a privacy feature bolted on. It is the structural model.
