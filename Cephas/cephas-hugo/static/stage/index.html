Architecture

How the Substrate Works

Three layers. Each does one job. Each built to mirror how memory actually works - uncertain at first, verified over time, permanent when proven. Click Technical Detail to flip any card.

📚 Layer 1 · The Reader
The Reader finds the right memory and hands it to your AI.
A little more -

When you ask a question, the Reader scans the substrate for relevant stored knowledge and injects it as context before your model sees your prompt. No retraining required.

⚖️ Layer 2 · Verifier
Three independent checks. Two must agree before anything gets stored.
A little more -

Wrong answers never make it in. Three Shadow E-Giant models vote independently. Fewer than two agree? The claim dies. This is why the substrate stays accurate as it grows.

Technical Detail · Verifier
PropertyDetail
Method3-way Shadow E-Giant concordance vote
Threshold2-of-3 agreement required for write to Eblet store
On disagreementClaim downgraded to Pheromone stage with decay timer
Bias mitigationEach Shadow E-Giant runs on independent context window
Constitutional AI (Anthropic 2022) Self-Consistency (Wang 2022) LLM-as-Judge (Zheng 2023)
💾 Layer 3 · Accumulator
Stores verified knowledge permanently, append-only.
A little more -

Eblets are SHA256-stamped knowledge entries. Append-only means nothing gets overwritten. Once verified and promoted to a Stone Tablet, the knowledge is permanent. The substrate gets cleaner over time, not noisier.
