The Marks System#

Marks are not money. They are a record of work performed.

When you contribute to MnemosyneC or the Liana Banyan cooperative and there is no cash to pay you yet, you earn Marks. Marks are:

  • Contingent accounts payable — a forward obligation from the cooperative to you
  • Not equity — Marks do not represent ownership or investment shares
  • Not transferable — they track your personal contribution record
  • Clearable through participation — as the cooperative earns revenue, Marks are cleared in order

How Marks are earned:

ActivityMark value
macOS build + maintenanceSignificant (negotiated)
Linux test reportStandard
Quality YouTube tutorialStandard
Quality written tutorialStandard
Bug report with reproduction stepsSmall
Community Coffee session hostedSmall

Marks are tracked in the cooperative substrate — append-only, SHA256-stamped, auditable.

Pedestal voting: Members with standing (earned through Marks and participation) have weighted votes in cooperative decisions. Standing is earned, not purchased.
