Section 5 - Bilateral Non-Exclusivity (BP094 Canon)

LB Never Enters Exclusive Partnerships

LB stays free to work with whichever AI companies work well with LB. AI companies compete for innovator pipeline access. No time constraints - only popularity constraints.

"No time constraints, just popularity constraints." Founder verbatim - BP094 canon (canon_universal_innovation_pipeline_marketplace_popularity_constrained_layered_on_tier_1_or_2_bp094)
Non-Exclusivity

LB never signs an exclusive arrangement with any single AI counterparty. All eight companies on this page remain in the innovation pipeline simultaneously. The cooperative's independence is its structural protection.

Popularity Contest

Companies that work well with LB get more innovator pipeline referrals. The pipeline is merit-weighted, not time-exclusivity-weighted. A better product gets more cooperative members. That is the constraint.

Universal Innovation Pipeline

The Universal Innovation Pipeline layers on top of either Tier 1 or Tier 2. Pipeline access is not contingent on tier choice - both tiers receive full pipeline participation rights. Canon: canon_universal_innovation_pipeline_marketplace_popularity_constrained_layered_on_tier_1_or_2_bp094
