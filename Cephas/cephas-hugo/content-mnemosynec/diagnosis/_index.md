## What Is The Diagnosis?

The Diagnosis is the **Human Salt layer** of MnemosyneC — the third tier of the Just Add Salt architecture.

When Substrate Salt (your local verified eblets) and Federation Salt (Constellation peers) cannot resolve a question to 3-voter concordance, The Diagnosis opens the question to **human cooperative peers** — real people who have experienced or solved the same problem.

Think of it as the cooperative-class implementation of the NYT "Diagnosis" column model: a complex, ambiguous question gets surfaced to a community of peers who collectively know the answer — and the best answer rises through votes, concordance, and community endorsement.

## Persistence Tiers: Pinch · Seasoning · Preserved-in-Salt

Every answer in MnemosyneC exists at one of three persistence tiers:

### 🧂 Pinch — Candidate Signal
- A pheromone-class entry: something might be true
- Has a half-life — if nothing refreshes it, it fades
- Not yet verified through concordance
- *Status: transient · can die quietly*

### 🌿 Seasoning — Verified, In Use
- Has achieved 3-voter concordance (Socceri Triad™ confirmation)
- Actively buoyed by community use and federation concordance
- Persists as long as the community keeps it alive through use
- *Status: living · sustained by network activity*

### 🫙 Preserved-in-Salt — Stone Tablet
- Verified + Used + Buoyed by enough living connection over enough time
- Promoted to Stone Tablet™ — immutable, append-only, permanent
- Does not decay. The truth the network has earned together.
- *Status: permanent · load-bearing canon*

**The substrate gets cleaner over time, not noisier.** Failed claims die. Verified+used claims persist. Used-and-verified claims become permanent.

## The Glow Mechanic — Bounty Surfacing

When a question enters The Diagnosis, it can accumulate **Glow** — a cooperative bounty signal that draws the attention of knowledgeable peers.

| Glow Level | Marks | Visual | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| **No Glow** | 0 | ⬜ Dim | Visible to local community |
| **Warm Glow** | 1–50 | 🟡 Amber | Boosted in federation feed |
| **Bright Glow** | 51–1000 | 🔴 Red-Gold | Surfaced to expert network tier |

Glow is funded via **Substitution Rails** — the cooperative payment layer that uses Fiat · Marks · Barter (never direct fiat conversion). A member can add Marks to a question's Glow to draw more cooperative attention to difficult or time-sensitive Diagnoses.

**Glow is not payment for an answer — it is signal of urgency for the cooperative network.** Responders are not compensated per-answer; they earn through the cooperative work-and-pay layer (83.3% to workers).

## The Diagnosis in Action — Domain-Universal Examples

### 🏥 Medical Mystery
*"My father has been experiencing fatigue, joint pain, and a recurring rash for 6 months. Three doctors have given three different diagnoses. What are we missing?"*

- Layer 1 (Substrate): Checks local eblets for concordant symptom clusters
- Layer 2 (Federation): Queries Constellation peers who have seen similar cases
- Layer 3 (Human Salt): Surfaces to The Diagnosis — cooperative peers who have navigated similar diagnostic odysseys share their experience
- **Disclaimer applies**: informational peer experience only — not medical advice

### 🔧 Mechanical Fix
*"My 2009 F-150 5.4L has a ticking noise from cylinder 3 that doesn't go away after warmup. Already replaced the cam phasers."*

- Layer 1 + 2: Substrate and Federation likely have many eblets on F-150 cam issues
- Layer 3: Mechanics in the cooperative who have seen this exact symptom pattern
- **Result**: Peer experience from people who solved the same problem

### 🌿 Practical How-To
*"I want to start a backyard orchard in Zone 6b with heavy clay soil. What rootstocks and varieties have actually survived?"*

- Highly local, highly practical — perfect for Human Salt
- Cooperative members in similar growing zones share lived experience
- Stone Tablets emerge for well-verified regional knowledge

### 📍 Local Find
*"Best independent auto mechanic in the Portland, ME area who won't upsell?"*

- Pure local knowledge — cannot be resolved by Substrate or Federation alone
- Human Salt is the only correct tier for this question
- Glow mechanic helps surface recent, verified local recommendations

## How to Post a Diagnosis

*(Available in MnemosyneC v0.4.x — The Diagnosis in-app surface)*

1. **Start a Plow** on your question
2. If the pipeline reaches **Federated Andon Cord Tier 2** (Federation cannot resolve), you receive an option: "Submit to The Diagnosis"
3. Set a **Glow budget** (0 = no boost, up to 1000 Marks for maximum surfacing)
4. Your question joins the cooperative peer network
5. Answers are **voted up** by peers (Reddit-style top-voted model)
6. **3-voter concordance** at the human response layer promotes answers to Seasoning tier
7. The best answer(s) are written back to your substrate as human-verified eblets

## The Diagnosis & The Federated Andon Cord

The Diagnosis is **Tier 3 of the Federated Andon Cord** escalation:

| Tier | Trigger | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1** | Local 3-voter concordance fails | Retry locally (up to 3 attempts) |
| **Tier 2** | Local retries exhausted | Escalate to Constellation peers (Federation) |
| **Tier 3** | Federation cannot resolve | Escalate to The Diagnosis (Human Salt) |

The Andon Cord **never silently fails**. It escalates through the salt layers until a cooperative answer is found — or it honestly reports "no resolution available at this time" and quarantines rather than writing a wrong answer.
