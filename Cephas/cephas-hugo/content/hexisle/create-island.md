## 3-Access-Key Gating (FLAG_MARKER FM-001)

When you create your island, you configure how other members visit it:

### Deck Card (Durable)
A signed membership credential. Non-consumable — members present once and it's valid across sessions. LB membership is the foundation; your island's cohort-class eligibility criteria layer on top.

### Guide (Mediated)
A co-member vouches for a visitor. Routes through the Pheromone trust-anchor graph — the endorsement creates a visible, permanent social trail. Guide access is the trust-network in action: relationships matter.

### Babylon Candle (Consumable Transient)
Single-use Marks expenditure. The visitor burns a Candle. The Marks flow: **83.3% to you as creator** (platform Cost+20%). Every Babylon Candle burn is an IP Ledger credit to your account. The Candle is transient — it burns once. The credit is permanent.

## Mordecai-Esther Decree-Composition

Once your island is submitted, its **original spec is immutable** — the Law of the Medes and Persians (Esther 8). No one, not even you, modifies it.

But other members can **append co-equal-authority decrees** to your Pedestal Forum:

- `extending` — builds on your original
- `contradicting` — challenges your original
- `composing` — does both simultaneously

All three have the same standing as your original. Contradictions are legally valid alongside the original. The island grows through co-authorship without anyone touching the foundation.

**MIC-003** (see [Standing Bounties](/bounties/)) specifically rewards members who design their island to attract high decree-composition density.
