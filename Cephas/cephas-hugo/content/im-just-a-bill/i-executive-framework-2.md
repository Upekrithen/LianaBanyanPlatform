## I. EXECUTIVE FRAMEWORK

This analysis examines twelve critical legislative domains in the 119th Congress through the lens of five governance innovation principles: Power-to-the-People Council structures, Federal-Congressional Body Cam Doctrine (transparency accountability), Listen Before Talk (deliberative process), Ambassador Position (representation integrity), and By Their Fruits (outcomes-based evaluation). These frameworks assess how civic infrastructure and voting policies either strengthen or erode democratic legitimacy, governmental accountability, and citizen participation.
