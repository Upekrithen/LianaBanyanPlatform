# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Jobs Guarantee Pilot
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal Jobs Guarantee pilot legislation in the 119th Congress. Funds limited-scope pilot programs in selected jurisdictions to test federal-jobs-guarantee policy mechanics.

## What does it actually do?
- Pilot funding for ~5-10 jurisdictions
- Wage floor at minimum-wage-plus living-wage benchmark
- Public-good work focus (infrastructure, care, environmental)
- Cooperative-class enterprise eligibility for jobs-guarantee employer designation
- Evaluation framework for measuring labor-market effects

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Cooperative-class enterprises (Step 4 Nodes, Freezer Hosts, Concierge providers) could be designated jobs-guarantee employers. Cooperative members in pilot jurisdictions could enter the cooperative-class economy via jobs-guarantee employment. Composes with **#16 Brass Tacks** + **#1/#2/#3** marketplace cluster.
