# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Indian Health Service Modernization
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Indian Health Service (IHS) modernization in the 119th Congress addressing chronic IHS underfunding (~50% of healthcare-need-based funding levels per documented HHS reports).

## What does it actually do?
- Advance appropriations for IHS (multi-year funding stability)
- Tribal Self-Governance expansion (more 638-contracted services)
- Workforce recruitment + retention programs
- Facilities modernization funding
- Mental and behavioral health expansion

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#6 Tatiana Schlossberg Health Accords** Tribal-sovereignty composing layer, **#7 MSA** for cooperative-class supplemental capital pooling, and the [Native American Self-Determination Act Amendments](../native-american-self-determination/) Bounty Poster.
