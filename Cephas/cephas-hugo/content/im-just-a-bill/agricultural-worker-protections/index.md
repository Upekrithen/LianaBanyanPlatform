# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Agricultural Worker Protections
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Agricultural worker protections legislation in the 119th Congress addressing heat-illness, pesticide-exposure, housing standards, and wage-payment infrastructure for the ~2.4M U.S. farmworkers.

## What does it actually do?
- OSHA heat-illness rulemaking codification
- Pesticide-exposure protections
- Farmworker housing standards
- Wage-payment Substitution-routing infrastructure (some versions)
- H-2A reform components

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#2 Lets Get Groceries** (local agricultural supply chain), **Save-the-World Paper 4** (Abolishing World Hunger), Farm Bill Reauthorization Bounty Poster.
