# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Automatic Voter Registration
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal automatic voter registration (AVR) legislation in the 119th Congress. Builds on AVR systems operating in 24+ states. Federal version creates uniform AVR framework for federal-election registration via state DMV, social-services, and Tribal-government interactions.

## What does it actually do?
- AVR via DMV (driver's license / ID issuance)
- AVR via Medicaid / SNAP / other social-services interactions
- AVR via Tribal-government interactions
- Opt-out provisions (privacy/preference)
- Citizenship-verification provisions composing with [SAVE Act](../save-act/) framework

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
The cooperative does not endorse positions on voting-policy bills. The Council exists to provide accurate plain-language explanation. Concrete impact: cooperative-class members in states without AVR currently face higher registration friction; AVR composes with the cooperative's broader civic-information infrastructure (Initiative #15).
