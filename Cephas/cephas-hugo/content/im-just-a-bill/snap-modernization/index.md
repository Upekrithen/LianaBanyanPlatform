# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — SNAP Modernization Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Modernization of the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP / food stamps) in the 119th Congress — addressing benefit adequacy, eligibility administration, and program structure separate from the broader Farm Bill reauthorization.

## What does it actually do?
- **Benefit adequacy:** Updates the Thrifty Food Plan calculation methodology
- **Administrative streamlining:** Reduces categorical eligibility friction across state programs
- **Work requirements:** Modifies (expands or contracts depending on version) work-requirement provisions
- **Hot foods purchase:** Some versions allow SNAP at prepared-foods retailers
- **Online purchasing:** Expands SNAP online-purchase access

## Who votes when?
- House Agriculture + Senate Agriculture active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
SNAP composes directly with **#1 Lets Make Dinner** Freezer Host network and **#2 Lets Get Groceries** city-chapter cooperatives. Hot foods + online purchasing provisions could allow SNAP usage at cooperative-class Freezer Hosts and cooperative grocery chapters.
