# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Water Infrastructure Investment Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Water infrastructure legislation in the 119th Congress addressing lead-pipe replacement, PFAS contamination remediation, and rural water-system viability.

## What does it actually do?
- **Lead service line replacement** — full federal funding for the documented ~9 million U.S. lead service lines
- **PFAS remediation** — funds municipal water-system PFAS removal
- **Rural water systems** — operating-grant support for small water systems
- **Drought response** — funds drinking-water resilience in arid regions
- **Tribal water access** — separate funding stream for Tribal water infrastructure

## Who votes when?
- House Transportation & Infrastructure + Senate Environment & Public Works active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **Save-the-World Paper 5** (Universal Earth Stewardship) and **Save-the-World Paper 3** (Universal Cooperative Shelter — water infrastructure is structurally part of housing). Lead-pipe replacement directly affects cooperative-class member housing health.
