# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Universal School Meals
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Universal School Meals legislation in the 119th Congress, building on the pandemic-era universal school meals waiver (which ended in 2022) and several states' subsequent universal-school-meals laws.

## What does it actually do?
- Federal universal-eligibility school breakfast and lunch
- Eliminates application/eligibility-verification process
- Expands Community Eligibility Provision (CEP) thresholds
- Funds summer-meals continuity programs
- Tribal Schools coverage provisions

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#1 Lets Make Dinner** (cooperative-class food infrastructure for school-age kids) and **Save-the-World Paper 12** (Universal Childhood). Universal school meals is the federal floor; cooperative-class composing layer fills the gaps (weekend, summer, and household-level food security).
