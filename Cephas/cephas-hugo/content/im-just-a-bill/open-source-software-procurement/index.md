## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?

## What does it actually do?

- Cooperative-class licensee eligibility
- AGPL and similar copyleft license recognition
- Federal-agency contributor-back-to-upstream provisions
