# 👀 Rural Grocery Tax Credit

## What
Federal rural grocery tax credit in the 119th Congress addressing food-desert formation in rural communities (~30M Americans live more than 10 miles from a grocery store).

## What it does
- Tax credit for grocery operators in designated rural food deserts · cooperative-grocery model preference · USDA technical-assistance integration.

## Composes
**#2 Lets Get Groceries** (Stacy Mitchell ILSR model directly) · **Farm Bill Reauthorization**.
