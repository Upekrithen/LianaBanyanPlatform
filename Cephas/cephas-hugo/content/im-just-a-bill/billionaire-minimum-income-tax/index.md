# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Billionaire Minimum Income Tax
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Tax-fairness legislation in the 119th Congress establishing a minimum income tax on households with net worth above $100 million, including unrealized capital gains in the tax base.

## What does it actually do?
- **Mark-to-market** — taxes unrealized capital gains for affected households on tradable assets
- **Non-tradable assets** — deferred-payment structure with interest
- **Threshold** — applies to households with $100M+ net worth (or $1B+ in some versions)
- **Minimum rate** — establishes minimum effective rate (e.g., 20-25%) including unrealized gains
- **Liquidity provisions** — allows phased payment for asset-rich/cash-poor situations

## Who votes when?
- House Ways & Means + Senate Finance active consideration; constitutional questions in active debate

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Direct impact only on ~700-900 households nationwide. Indirect impact: federal revenue generation that funds programs affecting cooperative-class members.

The cooperative does not endorse positions on this. The Council exists to provide accurate plain-language explanation.
