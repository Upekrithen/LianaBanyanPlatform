# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Lower Costs, More Transparency Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Healthcare-cost transparency and Pharmacy Benefit Manager (PBM) reform legislation in the 119th Congress. Focuses on hospital-price transparency, PBM transparency, and direct-to-consumer drug pricing.

## What does it actually do?
- **Hospital price transparency:** Strengthens existing CMS price-transparency rules; adds enforcement.
- **PBM transparency:** Requires PBMs to disclose rebate structures and pass-through pricing arrangements.
- **Drug coupons:** Modifies Medicare/Medicaid treatment of manufacturer drug coupons.
- **Site-neutral payments:** Adjusts Medicare payment differentials between hospital-outpatient and physician-office settings for the same procedures.
- **PBM reform:** Specific provisions limiting PBM "spread pricing" and clawback practices.

## Who votes when?
- House Energy & Commerce + Ways & Means + Senate Finance + HELP active consideration.

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes directly with three cooperative-healthcare initiatives:
- **#6 Tatiana Schlossberg Health Accords** — drug-pricing transparency is structurally embedded in the Galveston Legal Framework
- **#7 MSA Medical Savings Accounts** — Substitution-routing at participating providers depends on knowing actual prices
- **Affordable Insulin Now Act** — companion legislation in this same domain (separate Bounty Poster)
