# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Firefighter Cancer Presumption
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal firefighter cancer presumption legislation in the 119th Congress, codifying federal-employee firefighter cancer presumption (analogous to many state-level frameworks).

## What does it actually do?
- Federal-employee firefighter cancer presumption for workers' compensation
- Specified covered cancer types
- Presumption rebuttability standards
- VA equivalent provisions for veteran firefighters

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#7 MSA** (cooperative-class healthcare-supplemental composing with workers' comp), **#5 The Family Table** (firefighter family Trust Graph), and Social Security Fairness Act precedent (public-sector worker recognition).
