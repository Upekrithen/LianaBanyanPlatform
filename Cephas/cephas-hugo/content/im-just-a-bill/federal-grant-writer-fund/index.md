# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Grant Writer Fund
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal Grant Writer Fund in the 119th Congress addressing the documented pattern where rural and small-jurisdiction governments cannot access federal-grant funding because they lack grant-writer capacity.

## What does it actually do?
- HHS/USDA technical-assistance grant-writer pool
- Small-municipality and Tribal-government priority access
- Federal-grant simplification provisions
- Cooperative-class entity eligibility provisions

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with cooperative-class member-organized grant access (Initiative #9 Rally Group + cooperative-class licensee federation). Cooperative-class jurisdictions and entities benefit from grant-writer access parity with well-resourced jurisdictions.
