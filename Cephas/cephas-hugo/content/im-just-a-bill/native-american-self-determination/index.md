# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Native American Self-Determination Act Amendments
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Amendments to the Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act (ISDEAA) in the 119th Congress, expanding Tribal authority to administer federal programs and addressing the documented Tribal-sovereignty gaps in federal funding administration.

## What does it actually do?
- **638 contracts expansion** — broadens federal programs eligible for direct Tribal administration
- **Indirect cost coverage** — full federal-coverage of indirect costs for 638-contracted programs
- **Fee-to-trust process** — streamlines land-into-trust applications
- **Tribal climate adaptation** — funding stream for Tribal climate-adaptation projects
- **Public Law 280 states** — addresses jurisdictional ambiguity in PL280 states

## Who votes when?
- House Natural Resources + Senate Indian Affairs active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **Save-the-World Paper 5** (Universal Earth Stewardship — Tribal sovereignty as cooperative-class partnership), **Save-the-World Paper 7** (Health Care — Indian Health Service modernization composing), and **Save-the-World Paper 9** (Universal Education — Tribal college and university funding).
