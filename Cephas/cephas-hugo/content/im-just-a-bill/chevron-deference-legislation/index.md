# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Post-Loper-Bright Agency Authority
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Legislation in the 119th Congress responding to the 2024 Supreme Court Loper Bright decision overturning Chevron deference. Various proposals reaffirm agency interpretive authority by statute on a case-by-case basis.

## What does it actually do?
- Statutory delegation of interpretive authority for specific agencies/topics
- Sector-specific (environmental, healthcare, financial-services, food-safety) variations
- Court-of-jurisdiction provisions for agency-action review
- Rulemaking-process modifications

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Many cooperative-class composing federal-policy floors operate via agency rulemaking. Post-Chevron uncertainty affects nearly every Bounty Poster on this list. The cooperative does not endorse positions on this constitutional/administrative-law question; the Council exists to provide accurate plain-language explanation.
