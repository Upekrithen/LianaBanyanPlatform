# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Disability Benefits Modernization
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Modernization of Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) and Supplemental Security Income (SSI) in the 119th Congress, addressing backlog, asset limits, and earned-income disincentives.

## What does it actually do?
- **SSI asset limit reform** — raises the long-frozen $2,000 individual / $3,000 couple asset limit
- **Application backlog** — funds SSA capacity to clear documented multi-year application backlog
- **Earnings disincentives** — modifies the cliff at which earned income terminates eligibility
- **Marriage penalty** — addresses SSI marriage penalty
- **Plan to Achieve Self-Support** — expands PASS work-incentive program

## Who votes when?
- House Ways & Means + Senate Finance active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
SSDI/SSI affect approximately 8.5 million American adults and 1 million children. Composes with **#7 MSA** member capital-pooling (current SSI asset limit prevents disabled members from participating in MSA at meaningful levels), **#5 The Family Table** disability-care coordination, and **#8 Defense Klaus** disability-survivor crisis services.
