# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Workforce Housing Tax Credit
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Workforce Housing Tax Credit in the 119th Congress addressing the "missing middle" housing affordability gap (households earning 80-120% of area median income who don't qualify for LIHTC but cannot afford market-rate housing).

## What does it actually do?
- Federal tax credit for missing-middle housing development
- Set-asides for cooperative-housing trust projects
- Composes with LIHTC framework
- State allocation provisions

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **Save-the-World Paper 3** (Universal Cooperative Shelter), **Affordable Housing Tax Credit Act** Bounty Poster (LIHTC), and the cooperative-housing trust architecture.
