# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Universal Savings Accounts
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Universal Savings Accounts (USA) legislation in the 119th Congress proposing a tax-advantaged general-purpose savings vehicle (similar to Roth IRA but without retirement-purpose restriction).

## What does it actually do?
- Tax-advantaged general-purpose savings (no withdrawal-purpose restriction)
- Annual contribution limits
- Income-eligibility provisions
- Substitution-routing compatibility (some versions allow USA → cooperative-class participation)

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **Cooperative Finance Trio** (#7 + #10 + #11). USA vehicle could compose with MSA member capital pooling for tax-advantaged cooperative-class participation if Substitution-routing provisions are included.
