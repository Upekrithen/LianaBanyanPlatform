# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Opioid Settlement Funds Protection
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal protection legislation in the 119th Congress for the ~$50B in opioid-settlement funds flowing to state and local governments. Addresses the documented diversion of opioid-settlement funds to general-revenue purposes rather than substance-use-disorder treatment and recovery.

## What does it actually do?
- Federal accountability framework for state opioid-settlement spending
- Treatment-and-recovery-services minimum allocation requirements
- Transparency reporting requirements for state opioid funds
- Federal grant matching for jurisdictions exceeding minimum allocation thresholds
- Indigenous-community-impact provisions

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#5 The Family Table** (recovery and care intersection), **#7 MSA** (Substitution-routing for participating recovery providers), and **#8 Defense Klaus** (overlap with survivor-class infrastructure when SUD intersects domestic violence). Composes with [Rural Mental Health Access Act](../rural-mental-health/) Bounty Poster.
