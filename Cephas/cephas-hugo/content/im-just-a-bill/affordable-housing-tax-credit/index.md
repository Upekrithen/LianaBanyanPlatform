# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Affordable Housing Tax Credit Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Expansions to the federal Low-Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) program in the 119th Congress, addressing the documented shortage of affordable housing units in nearly every metropolitan area.

## What does it actually do?
- **LIHTC allocation:** Increases per-state allocation of 9% credits.
- **4% credit floor:** Sets minimum 4% credit rate for bond-financed projects.
- **Income averaging:** Allows tax-credit-funded units to serve a wider income mix.
- **Cooperative housing:** Provisions specifically supporting cooperative-housing models.
- **Tribal housing:** Set-asides for Tribal area housing.

## Who votes when?
- **House Ways & Means + Senate Finance:** Active consideration
- **Bipartisan support:** Has historically had cross-aisle support
- **President:** Signature expected if both chambers pass

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **Save-the-World Paper 3 — Universal Cooperative Shelter**. Federal LIHTC expansion is the financing-mechanism floor; cooperative-housing trust architecture is the structural complement that ensures wealth stays with members rather than extracted by absentee landlords.
