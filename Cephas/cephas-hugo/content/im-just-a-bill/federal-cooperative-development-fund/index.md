# 👀 Federal Cooperative Development Fund

## What
Federal Cooperative Development Fund expansion in the 119th Congress (builds on existing USDA Rural Cooperative Development Grants).

## What it does
- Cross-sector cooperative-development grants · technical assistance for worker-cooperative conversions · cooperative-class infrastructure financing · Tribal cooperative provisions.

## Composes
**Worker-Cooperative Conversion Tax Deferral** + **Rural Electrification 2.0** + **Federal Charter Credit Unions**.

🟡 Provisional. *Help Each Other Help Ourselves.*
