## I. EXECUTIVE SYNTHESIS

This artifact examines eleven critical education policy domains under consideration by the 119th Congress, analyzing legislative mechanisms, stakeholder impacts, implementation architectures, and systemic transformation potential. The analysis employs multi-framework synthesis to evaluate policy proposals through lenses of equity, efficacy, fiscal sustainability, and democratic participation in educational governance.

**Core Policy Clusters:**
1. **Access Infrastructure** (FAFSA, PSLF, college affordability, textbook affordability)
2. **Developmental Foundations** (Universal Pre-K, childcare affordability, school meals)
3. **Human Capital Systems** (teacher pipeline, school counselor funding)
4. **Emerging Equity Domains** (college athlete compensation, prison education)
