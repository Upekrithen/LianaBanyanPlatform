# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — American Innovation and Choice Online Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
AICOA would prohibit dominant online platforms (Amazon, Google, Apple, Meta) from self-preferencing their own products and services over competitors on the platforms they operate.

## What does it actually do?
- **Self-preferencing prohibition:** Dominant platforms can't preference their own products/services in search results, app stores, or marketplace algorithms.
- **Tying restrictions:** Can't require business users to buy other services as a condition of platform access.
- **Discrimination prohibition:** Can't treat business users worse than the platform's own offerings.
- **Enforcement:** FTC + DOJ Antitrust + state AGs.
