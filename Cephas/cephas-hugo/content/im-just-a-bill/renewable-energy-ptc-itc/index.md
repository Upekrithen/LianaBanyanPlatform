# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Renewable Energy PTC/ITC Continuation
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Continuation of the federal Production Tax Credit (PTC) and Investment Tax Credit (ITC) for renewable energy in the 119th Congress. PTC/ITC were extended by the 2022 IRA; current legislation addresses ongoing structure and qualifying technology definitions.

## What does it actually do?
- **PTC continuation** — extends per-kWh production credit for wind, solar, geothermal, hydro
- **ITC continuation** — extends investment tax credit for solar, storage, fuel cells
- **Domestic content** — bonus credit for projects meeting domestic-content thresholds
- **Energy communities** — bonus credit for projects in fossil-fuel-transition communities
- **Community-owned generation** — provisions specifically enabling cooperative-class member-owned generation

## Who votes when?
- House Ways & Means + Senate Finance active consideration; closely watched by the energy sector

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Direct composing with **Save-the-World Paper 2** (Universal Abundant Low-Cost Energy). Community-owned generation provisions enable cooperative-class member-pooled solar/storage installations that share the federal tax-credit incentive structure.
