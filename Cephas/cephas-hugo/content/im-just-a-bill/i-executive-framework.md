## I. EXECUTIVE FRAMEWORK

This artifact surveys federal legislative and regulatory initiatives within the 119th Congressional session relevant to cooperative financial infrastructure, examining eleven distinct policy domains through the analytical lens of three cooperative finance frameworks: Money Service Agreement (#7 MSA), Value Sequencing Language (#10 VSL), and Let's Make Bread (#11 LMB). The analysis incorporates structural constraints including the NO-FIAT-CONVERSION Blood Rule, SEC regulatory engagement protocols, and substitution-only routing architectures.
