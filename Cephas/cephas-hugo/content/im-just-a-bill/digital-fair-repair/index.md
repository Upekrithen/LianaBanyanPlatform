# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Digital Fair Repair Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
The Digital Fair Repair Act in the 119th Congress (companion / extension of [H.R.906 Right to Repair](../right-to-repair/)). Focuses specifically on digital products — software-locked devices, DRM circumvention for repair purposes, and "right to install" provisions.

## What does it actually do?
- **DRM exemption** — codifies right-to-repair-related DMCA Section 1201 exemptions
- **Software locks** — prohibits manufacturer use of software locks to prevent third-party repair
- **Parts availability** — manufacturers must make parts available for ~7 years post-sale
- **Diagnostic tools** — independent repair shops can access manufacturer diagnostic tools
- **Documentation** — repair documentation publicly available

## Who votes when?
- House Energy & Commerce + Judiciary + Senate Judiciary active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#16 Brass Tacks** (maker-economy foundational principle), **#3 Lets Go Shopping** (cooperative marketplace where repair-friendly goods are categorically supported), and the cooperative's broader anti-extraction structural commitment.
