# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Immigration Court Reform
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Immigration court reform in the 119th Congress addressing the documented immigration-court backlog (~3.7M+ pending cases) and structural concerns about immigration-court independence (currently within DOJ rather than independent).

## What does it actually do?
- Article I court structure proposal (immigration courts independent of DOJ)
- Backlog-reduction funding
- Counsel-availability provisions for unaccompanied minors
- Procedural-fairness reforms

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
The cooperative does not endorse positions on immigration-policy bills. The Council exists to provide accurate plain-language explanation. Concrete impact: many cooperative-class members have family members in immigration proceedings; court-backlog and procedural-fairness affect daily life for those families.
