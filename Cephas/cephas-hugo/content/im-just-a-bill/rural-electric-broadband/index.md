# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Rural Electric Cooperative Broadband Authority
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal authorization in the 119th Congress for rural electric cooperatives to expand into broadband service delivery, leveraging existing fiber-and-pole infrastructure.

## What does it actually do?
- USDA Rural Utilities Service loan eligibility for cooperative broadband
- BEAD program coordination provisions
- Cooperative-cross-subsidy clarity
- Federal-pole-attachment authority for cooperative broadband providers

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **BEAD Broadband Expansion** + **Universal Broadband Floor** + **Federal Broadband Affordability** Bounty Posters. Direct kin-cooperative federation potential — rural electric cooperatives expanding into broadband are natural cooperative-class peers.
