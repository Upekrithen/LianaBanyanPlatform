# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Antitrust Merger Review Reform
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Antitrust reform legislation in the 119th Congress modernizing the Hart-Scott-Rodino (HSR) Act merger-review process and updating the agencies' authority to challenge anti-competitive mergers.

## What does it actually do?
- **HSR thresholds** — adjusts pre-merger notification thresholds
- **Healthcare-PE rollups** — specific provisions addressing private-equity rollups in healthcare (composing with [Stop Wall Street Looting Act](../stop-wall-street-looting/))
- **Labor markets** — strengthens FTC/DOJ authority to challenge mergers reducing wage competition
- **Vertical mergers** — clarifies authority to challenge anti-competitive vertical integration
- **Enforcement budgets** — funds expanded FTC + DOJ Antitrust Division capacity

## Who votes when?
- House Judiciary + Senate Judiciary active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#2 Lets Get Groceries** (anti-monopoly architecture is Initiative #2's explicit thesis), **#3 Lets Go Shopping** (cooperative marketplace as structural alternative to consolidated platform-capitalism), and the cooperative compact's broader structural-anti-extraction commitment.
