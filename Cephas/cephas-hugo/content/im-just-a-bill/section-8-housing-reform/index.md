# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Section 8 Housing Voucher Reform
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Reform of the federal Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher program in the 119th Congress, addressing voucher-acceptance discrimination, payment-standard adequacy, and administrative friction.

## What does it actually do?
- **Source of income protection** — federal prohibition on landlord discrimination against voucher holders
- **Payment standard** — modifies HUD Fair Market Rent calculation
- **Mobility** — easier inter-jurisdictional voucher transfer
- **Landlord recruitment** — incentives for landlords accepting vouchers in low-poverty areas
- **Waiting list reform** — addresses the documented multi-year voucher waiting lists

## Who votes when?
- House Financial Services + Senate Banking active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **Save-the-World Paper 3** (Universal Cooperative Shelter). Voucher reform composes with cooperative-housing trust architecture: voucher recipients can be cooperative members; cooperative-owned housing can accept vouchers; the cooperative composes with the federal floor.
