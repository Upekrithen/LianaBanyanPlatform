# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Election Worker Protection Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal protection legislation for state and local election workers in the 119th Congress, addressing documented increases in threats against poll workers, election administrators, and their families.

## What does it actually do?
- **Federal criminal penalties** — for threats against election workers
- **Doxing protection** — federal prohibition on publishing personal information of election workers with intent to intimidate
- **Election infrastructure** — funds physical security upgrades at polling places and election-administration facilities
- **Training and recruitment** — federal grants for state election-worker training and recruitment
- **Family protection** — extends protections to immediate family of election workers
