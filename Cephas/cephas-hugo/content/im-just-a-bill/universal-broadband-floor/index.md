# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Universal Broadband Floor
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Universal Broadband Floor in the 119th Congress establishing minimum broadband-speed standards as a federal-policy floor for federally-funded deployment.

## What does it actually do?
- Federal minimum broadband speed standards (typically 100/20 or 100/100 Mbps)
- Universal Service Fund (USF) reform
- Affordability provisions composing with ACP successor
- Tribal broadband floor

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Cooperative-class member participation in nearly every Initiative depends on functional broadband. Universal Broadband Floor is structural infrastructure for the cooperative-class economy.
