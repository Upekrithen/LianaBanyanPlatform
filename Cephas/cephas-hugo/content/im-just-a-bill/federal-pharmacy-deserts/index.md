# 👀 Federal Pharmacy Desert Mitigation

## What
Federal pharmacy desert mitigation in the 119th Congress addressing the documented closure of ~9,000 retail pharmacies since 2020.

## What it does
- 340B Drug Pricing Program access expansion · independent pharmacy reimbursement reform · PBM mandatory contracting standards · cooperative-pharmacy eligibility provisions.

## Composes
**#6 Tatiana Schlossberg Health Accords** + **#7 MSA** + **Lower Costs Transparency** + multiple drug-pricing Bounty Posters.
