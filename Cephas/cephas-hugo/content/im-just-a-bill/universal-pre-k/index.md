# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Universal Pre-K Funding
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal Universal Pre-Kindergarten funding legislation in the 119th Congress. Builds on existing Head Start framework with broader pre-K access provisions for 3- and 4-year-olds regardless of family income.

## What does it actually do?
- **Federal grants** — funds state-administered universal pre-K programs
- **Provider standards** — quality and curricular standards
- **Teacher pay** — addresses pre-K teacher compensation parity with K-12
- **Mixed-delivery system** — funds both public-school-based and community-based pre-K
- **Indian Country provisions** — Tribal pre-K funding stream
