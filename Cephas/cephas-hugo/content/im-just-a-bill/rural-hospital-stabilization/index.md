# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Rural Hospital Stabilization
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Rural hospital stabilization legislation in the 119th Congress addressing the documented closure rate of rural hospitals (~150+ closed since 2010, with hundreds more financially at-risk).

## What does it actually do?
- Enhanced Medicare reimbursement for designated rural hospitals
- Critical Access Hospital program modernization
- Rural Emergency Hospital designation expansion
- Telehealth permanence for rural-hospital service-line continuity
- Workforce provisions composing with rural mental health legislation

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#6 Tatiana Schlossberg Health Accords** + **#7 MSA**. Rural members face the documented care-desert created by hospital closures. The cooperative composes with whatever federal floor exists.
