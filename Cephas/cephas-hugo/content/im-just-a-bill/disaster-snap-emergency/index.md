# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Disaster SNAP Emergency Authorization
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Disaster-SNAP (D-SNAP) program expansion in the 119th Congress. D-SNAP provides temporary food assistance to households affected by federally-declared disasters; current proposals expand eligibility, streamline application, and increase per-household amounts.

## What does it actually do?
- **Streamlined eligibility** — automatic D-SNAP enrollment for current SNAP recipients in disaster areas
- **Application simplification** — single-form application; mobile-accessible
- **Benefit duration** — extends D-SNAP duration for prolonged-impact disasters
- **Hot foods** — explicitly allows D-SNAP for hot-prepared foods in disaster areas (often the only food available)
- **Coordination with disaster relief** — composes with FEMA Individual Assistance

## Who votes when?
- House Agriculture + Senate Agriculture active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes directly with **#1 Lets Make Dinner** Freezer Host network (cooperative-class food infrastructure for members in disaster-affected areas) and **#2 Lets Get Groceries** local supply chain. Disaster-SNAP modernization makes the federal floor work better for cooperative-class members in crisis.
