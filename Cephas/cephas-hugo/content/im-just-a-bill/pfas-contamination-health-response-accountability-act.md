## Bill Bounty Poster #6: PFAS Contamination Health Response & Accountability Act

### Legislative Priority Classification
**Urgency Level**: EMERGENCY
**Feasibility Score**: 6/10
**Coalition Potential**: Bipartisan with regional champions

### Policy Problem Statement
PFAS ("forever chemicals") detected in 45% of US tap water; 200+ million individuals exposed. Linked to cancers, thyroid disease, pregnancy complications, immune suppression, childhood developmental delays. Military bases, firefighting training sites, industrial facilities created contamination hotspots. No comprehensive federal health response framework exists; affected communities lack screening, treatment, monitoring infrastructure.

### Core Legislative Components

#### Title I: Public Health Surveillance & Screening
- **National PFAS Biomonitoring**: CDC/ATSDR conducts blood serum testing in high-exposure communities (military bases, contaminated watersheds, industrial zones)
- **Expanded NHANES Integration**: Add PFAS panel to National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey for population-level tracking
- **Cancer Registry Enhancement**: Mandate PFAS exposure history in state cancer registries for epidemiological analysis
- **Reproductive Health Monitoring**: Track pregnancy outcomes, birth defects
