# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Medicaid Expansion Incentive
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal incentive legislation in the 119th Congress to encourage the ~10 remaining non-expansion states to expand Medicaid under the Affordable Care Act framework.

## What does it actually do?
- **Federal match enhancement** — temporary higher federal match for newly-expanding states
- **Direct federal-marketplace option** — for states that decline expansion, enables federal-marketplace Medicaid-equivalent coverage
- **Coverage-gap closure** — addresses the ~2-4 million Americans who fall in the coverage gap (too much income for state Medicaid; too little for ACA subsidies)
- **Provider rate provisions** — supports rural-hospital sustainability in expansion contexts

## Who votes when?
- House Energy and Commerce + Senate Finance active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#6 Tatiana Schlossberg Health Accords** + **#7 MSA**. Cooperative-class members in non-expansion states currently fall in the coverage gap most acutely; this legislation directly addresses that gap.
