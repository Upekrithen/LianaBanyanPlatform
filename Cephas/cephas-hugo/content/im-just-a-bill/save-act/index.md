# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — SAVE Act (H.R.22)
## Safeguard American Voter Eligibility Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

---

## What is this bill?

The SAVE Act would require documentary proof of U.S. citizenship for federal voter registration. It was introduced in the 119th Congress and has been a recurring priority for one wing of Congress over multiple sessions.

---

## What does it actually do?

In plain language:

- **Documentary proof of citizenship required** to register to vote in federal elections (passport, certified birth certificate, or specified other documents).
- **State officials must verify** documentation at registration; states would update systems to comply.
- **Applies to** new voter registrations and changes; existing registrations not directly purged but subject to state implementation choices.
- **Penalties** added for officials who register voters without verifying citizenship documentation.

---

## Who votes when?

- **House:** Has passed in prior Congresses; 119th Congress action ongoing
- **Senate:** Faces 60-vote threshold; passage unclear
- **President:** Signature dependent on Senate outcome
