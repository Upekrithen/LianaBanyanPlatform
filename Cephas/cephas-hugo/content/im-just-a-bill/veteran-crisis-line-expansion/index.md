# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Veteran Crisis Line Expansion Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Expansion of the Veterans Crisis Line program in the 119th Congress, addressing capacity, response time, and integration with the broader 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline.

## What does it actually do?
- **Staffing:** Increases counselor staffing for the Veterans Crisis Line (Press 1 from 988).
- **Wait time:** Sets statutory maximum wait time for veteran callers.
- **Follow-up:** Funds structured follow-up protocols for veterans who reach crisis services.
- **Peer-support:** Expands peer-support specialist availability (veterans helping veterans).
- **Family services:** Adds family-member-of-veteran callers to qualifying-caller categories.

## Who votes when?
- House and Senate Veterans' Affairs Committees active consideration.

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with the cooperative's veteran-Crown anchors on Initiative #15 (Mark Wahlberg, John Cena, Gary Sinise — the Lt. Dan Band founder, Sam Elliott, Tom Hanks). Crisis Line capacity directly affects veteran members of the cooperative.

Composes with **#5 The Family Table** family-member-of-veteran caller expansion and **#8 Defense Klaus** survivor-class crisis-response architecture.
