# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Dental Medicare Coverage
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Medicare dental-coverage expansion legislation in the 119th Congress. Standard Medicare currently excludes most dental care, leaving ~24M older adults without coverage.

## What does it actually do?
- Medicare Part B dental-coverage addition (preventive + restorative)
- Phased implementation over several years
- Coverage standards for dentures, root canals, periodontal care
- Provider-network expansion provisions

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#5 The Family Table** (older-adult care) and **#7 MSA** (dental Substitution-routing where federal coverage falls short).
