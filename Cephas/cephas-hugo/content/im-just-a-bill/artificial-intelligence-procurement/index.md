# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal AI Procurement Standards
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal AI procurement standards legislation in the 119th Congress addressing how federal agencies acquire and deploy AI systems, including transparency, bias testing, and open-source preference.

## What does it actually do?
- Federal-agency AI acquisition standards (NIST AI Risk Management Framework based)
- Open-source preference for federal AI deployments
- Bias testing requirements for high-risk applications
- Transparency provisions for algorithmic decision-making
- Cooperative-class AI enterprise eligibility for federal contract competition

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **Save-the-World Paper 8** (CAI Substrate Orchestra). The cooperative's SSPL-licensed Conducted AI Substrate is structurally compatible with federal open-source-preference procurement.
