# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Child Tax Credit Expansion
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Expansion of the federal Child Tax Credit (CTC) in the 119th Congress, addressing the documented child-poverty reduction the 2021 expanded CTC achieved before it expired.

## What does it actually do?
- **Increases** maximum CTC amount per child
- **Full refundability** for lowest-income households (current structure phases in)
- **Monthly payment option** restored (versus annual lump sum)
- **Age expansion** for qualifying children
- **Phase-out** adjustments for higher-income households

## Who votes when?
- House Ways & Means + Senate Finance active consideration; historically bipartisan if smaller

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
The 2021 expanded CTC cut child poverty by ~half during its operative period. Reinstatement directly affects cooperative-class member family budgets, MSA contribution capacity, and Family Table Trust Graph stability.
