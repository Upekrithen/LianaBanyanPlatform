# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Financial Data Portability
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Codification of the CFPB's Section 1033 open-banking rule in the 119th Congress (rule subject to ongoing litigation).

## What does it actually do?
- Consumer right-to-port financial data
- Standardized API requirements
- Cooperative-finance entity eligibility
- Data-broker prohibition

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes directly with **Cooperative Finance Trio** (#7 + #10 + #11). Members can port their financial data into cooperative-class platforms without bank-class friction.
