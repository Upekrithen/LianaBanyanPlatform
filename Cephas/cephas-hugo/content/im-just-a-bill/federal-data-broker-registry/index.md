# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Data Broker Registry
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What
Federal data broker registry in the 119th Congress requiring data brokers to register and disclose data categories collected.

## What it does
- Federal registry with public-search interface · annual disclosures · opt-out mechanism · penalties for noncompliance · cooperative-class entity exemption.

## Composes
**#9 Rally Group** (surveillance-capitalism prevention) · **American Privacy Rights Act** Bounty Poster.
