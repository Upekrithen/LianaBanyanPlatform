# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Overdraft Fee Cap
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal overdraft-fee cap legislation in the 119th Congress. Current bank overdraft fees average $30-35; documented extraction pattern from low-income account holders.

## What does it actually do?
- **Fee cap** — limits per-overdraft fee (typically proposed $3-$10)
- **Per-day cap** — limits aggregate daily overdraft fees
- **Reasonable-and-proportional standard** — requires fees be tied to actual costs
- **Disclosure** — strengthens overdraft-protection disclosure requirements
- **Credit-union exemption** — most proposals carve out small credit unions

## Who votes when?
- House Financial Services + Senate Banking active consideration; CFPB rulemaking parallel track

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#7 MSA** (Cathie Mahon, Inclusiv credit-union model) and **#10 VSL** peer-to-peer cooperative finance. Cooperative-class members who currently absorb overdraft fees would benefit; the cooperative-finance trio is the structural alternative to bank-class fee-extraction.
