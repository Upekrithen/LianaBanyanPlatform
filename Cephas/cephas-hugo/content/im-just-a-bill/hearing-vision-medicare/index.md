# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Hearing and Vision Medicare
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Medicare hearing and vision coverage expansion in the 119th Congress. Standard Medicare excludes hearing aids, hearing-aid exams, eyeglasses, and routine vision care.

## What does it actually do?
- Medicare hearing-aid coverage (typically every 5 years)
- Hearing-exam coverage
- Eyeglass coverage (one pair every 2 years)
- Routine vision-care coverage
- Provider-supply provisions to address current OTC-hearing-aid market

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#5 The Family Table** (older-adult sensory-care impacts caregiver coordination) and **#7 MSA** (hearing/vision Substitution at participating providers).
