# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Inflation Reduction Act Amendments
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
A series of 119th Congress proposals to amend, extend, or roll back specific provisions of the 2022 Inflation Reduction Act (IRA), particularly its clean-energy tax credits and manufacturing incentives.

## What does it actually do?
- **Clean energy tax credits:** Modifications to wind/solar/battery production tax credits (PTC) and investment tax credits (ITC).
- **Manufacturing incentives:** Domestic-content requirements for clean-energy components.
- **Methane fee:** Implementation modifications for oil-and-gas methane emissions fees.
- **EV credits:** Eligibility rules for the $7,500 new-EV credit and used-EV credit.
- **Drug pricing:** Implementation of Medicare drug-price-negotiation provisions.

## Who votes when?
- **Various committees, 119th Congress:** Active markup
- **Multiple vehicles:** May be bundled into reconciliation or appropriations
- **President:** Signature dependent on which provisions reach desk

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
- **Energy costs:** IRA clean-energy tax credits affect home solar payback periods and EV affordability for middle-class members.
- **Manufacturing tax credits:** Composes with **#16 Brass Tacks** reshoring infrastructure and the cooperative's Step 4 Node manufacturing network.
- **Drug pricing:** Composes with **#7 MSA** member healthcare-cost transparency.
