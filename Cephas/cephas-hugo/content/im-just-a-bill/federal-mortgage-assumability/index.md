# 👀 Federal Mortgage Assumability

## What
Federal mortgage assumability restoration in the 119th Congress permitting buyers to take over existing low-rate mortgages.

## What it does
- Restores assumability for conventional Fannie/Freddie loans · qualification standards · seller-protection provisions · cooperative-housing-trust assumability.

## Composes
**Save-the-World Paper 3** (Cooperative Shelter) · **Workforce Housing Tax Credit** + **Affordable Housing Tax Credit**.
