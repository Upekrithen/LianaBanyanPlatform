# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Whistleblower Protection Expansion
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Expansion of federal whistleblower protections in the 119th Congress, addressing gaps in current law for federal employees, contractors, and private-sector whistleblowers reporting fraud, waste, or abuse.

## What does it actually do?
- **Federal employee protections** — strengthens Merit Systems Protection Board enforcement
- **Contractor protections** — covers federal contractor employees disclosing fraud
- **Anti-retaliation** — increases damages and remedies for retaliation
- **Reporting channels** — expands acceptable disclosure channels
- **Intelligence-community** — limited expansion of IC whistleblower channels

## Who votes when?
- House Oversight + Senate Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with the Federal-Congressional Body Cam transparency doctrine and the cooperative's broader structural commitment to "accountability is dignity, not vulnerability." Whistleblower protections are infrastructure for the transparency the cooperative argues for.
