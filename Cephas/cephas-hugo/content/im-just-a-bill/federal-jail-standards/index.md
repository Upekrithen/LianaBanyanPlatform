# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Jail and Pretrial Detention Standards
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal jail and pretrial detention standards legislation in the 119th Congress addressing documented variations in jail-conditions, medical care, and pretrial-release decision quality across U.S. jurisdictions.

## What does it actually do?
- Federal minimum standards for medical care in detention
- Pretrial-release decision-quality standards
- Mental health crisis-response in detention contexts
- Family-visitation provisions
- Tribal-jurisdiction provisions
