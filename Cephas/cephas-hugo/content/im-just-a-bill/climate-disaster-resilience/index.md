# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Climate Disaster Resilience Funding
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Climate disaster preparation and resilience funding in the 119th Congress addressing the documented increasing frequency and severity of climate-driven disasters (hurricanes, wildfires, floods, heat events).

## What does it actually do?
- **FEMA pre-disaster mitigation** — increased funding for hardening against future disasters
- **National Flood Insurance Program** — modifications to actuarial-soundness and affordability balance
- **Wildfire risk reduction** — funding for fuels-management on federal land + community-protection grants
- **Heat resilience** — funding for cooling centers, energy assistance, urban tree canopy
- **Tribal climate adaptation** — separate funding stream for Tribal climate-adaptation projects

## Who votes when?
- House Transportation & Infrastructure + Senate Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Climate disasters disproportionately affect cooperative-class members (manufactured housing, rental housing, lower-income communities). Composes with **Save-the-World Paper 3** (Universal Cooperative Shelter), **Save-the-World Paper 5** (Earth Stewardship), and **#8 Defense Klaus** (crisis-response architecture).
