# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Childcare Affordability
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal childcare affordability legislation in the 119th Congress. Documented childcare costs now exceed in-state college tuition in 28+ states; documented "child-care cliff" where pandemic-era childcare-stabilization funding exhausted in 2023.

## What does it actually do?
- **CCDBG expansion** — Child Care and Development Block Grant funding expansion
- **Provider compensation** — addresses the structural underpayment of childcare workers (~$12-15/hour median nationally)
- **Family co-payment cap** — caps family contribution at percentage of income
- **Mixed-delivery system** — funds home-based, center-based, and family-friend-and-neighbor care
- **Tribal childcare** — separate funding stream

## Who votes when?
- House Education and Workforce + Senate HELP active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes directly with **#4 Household Concierge** (cooperative childcare connection), **#5 The Family Table** (intergenerational care composing), **Save-the-World Paper 12** (Universal Childhood), and **[Universal Pre-K Funding](../universal-pre-k/)** Bounty Poster.

The cooperative does not duplicate federal childcare-affordability infrastructure; the cooperative composes with the federal floor.
