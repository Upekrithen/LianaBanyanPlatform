# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Cybersecurity Infrastructure Investment
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal cybersecurity investment in the 119th Congress strengthening CISA (Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency) capacity, mandating reporting for critical-infrastructure breaches, and expanding state/local cybersecurity grants.

## What does it actually do?
- **CISA expansion** — funds enhanced CISA capacity for threat-hunting, incident-response, and critical-infrastructure defense
- **Breach reporting** — codifies critical-infrastructure breach reporting requirements (72-hour windows)
- **State/local grants** — funds municipal cybersecurity (water systems, schools, hospitals)
- **K-12 cyber** — specific provisions for K-12 ransomware response
- **Healthcare cyber** — provisions for hospital ransomware resilience

## Who votes when?
- House Homeland Security + Senate Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs
