# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — PRESS Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
The Protect Reporters from Exploitative State Spying (PRESS) Act in the 119th Congress establishing a federal shield law for journalists protecting source confidentiality, with specified exceptions.

## What does it actually do?
- **Source protection** — federal protection for journalist-source confidentiality
- **Exceptions** — clear exceptions for terrorism, kidnapping, imminent harm
- **Definition of "journalist"** — broad enough to cover independent and cooperative-class journalists, narrow enough to exclude bad-faith actors
- **Tech-company protection** — limits federal subpoenas to tech companies seeking journalist communications
