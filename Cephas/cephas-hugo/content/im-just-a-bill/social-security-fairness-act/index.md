# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Social Security Fairness Act
## Public Law 118-333 — Signed January 5, 2025
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

---

## What is this bill?

The Social Security Fairness Act eliminated two provisions that had reduced Social Security benefits for millions of public-sector workers since the 1980s: the **Windfall Elimination Provision (WEP)** and the **Government Pension Offset (GPO)**.

It was signed into law in January 2025 with significant bipartisan support.

---

## What does it actually do?

In plain language:

- **WEP repealed.** Public-sector workers who also worked Social-Security-covered jobs no longer have their Social Security benefits reduced by a special formula.
- **GPO repealed.** Spouses, widows, and widowers of public-sector workers no longer have their Social Security spousal/survivor benefits offset by their government pension.
- **Effective date:** Provisions apply to benefits payable for months after December 2023 (with retroactive payments where applicable).
- **Affected groups:** Approximately 2.5 million public-sector retirees including teachers, firefighters, police officers, and federal employees in non-Social-Security-covered positions.
