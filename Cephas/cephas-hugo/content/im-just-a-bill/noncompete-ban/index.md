# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Noncompete Ban
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal noncompete ban legislation in the 119th Congress codifying the FTC's 2024 Noncompete Rule (currently subject to ongoing litigation).

## What does it actually do?
- Prohibits most noncompete clauses in employment contracts
- Senior-executive carveouts
- Trade-secret protection preserved through other mechanisms (NDA enforcement)
- State-law-floor preservation

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#16 Brass Tacks** (worker mobility into Step 4 Node cooperative manufacturing), **#3 Lets Go Shopping** (cooperative-marketplace seller mobility), and worker-cooperative-conversion legislation.
