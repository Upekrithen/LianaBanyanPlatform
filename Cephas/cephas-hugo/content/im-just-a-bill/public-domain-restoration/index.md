# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Public Domain Restoration
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Copyright reform in the 119th Congress addressing the documented public-domain shortage created by repeated copyright-term extensions (Sonny Bono Act, Mickey Mouse Protection Act variants).

## What does it actually do?
- Tightens orphan-works treatment
- Renewal-registration option for post-1978 works (formerly required)
- Library/archive fair-use clarification
- Statutory-damages cap modifications
- Anti-circumvention DMCA Section 1201 review

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#13 JukeBox** (cooperative music licensing depends on understanding what's in the public domain), **Save Local Journalism Act** (fair-use for archival journalism), and **#12 Harper Guild + #14 Didasko** (cooperative-education materials benefit from larger public domain).
