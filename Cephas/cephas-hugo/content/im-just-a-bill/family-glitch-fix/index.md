# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — ACA Family Glitch Fix
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
ACA Family Glitch fix legislation in the 119th Congress. The IRS issued a regulatory fix in 2022 that addressed the long-documented "family glitch" (employees with affordable self-only employer coverage but unaffordable family coverage previously couldn't access ACA marketplace subsidies for their families). Current legislation codifies the regulatory fix.

## What does it actually do?
- Codifies 2022 IRS regulatory fix into statute
- Strengthens family-coverage affordability test
- Clarifies enrollment-period transitions for family-glitch-affected households
- Addresses auto-enrollment provisions

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#6 Tatiana Schlossberg Health Accords** + **#7 MSA**. Cooperative-class member families with employer self-only coverage frequently fell into the family glitch. Statutory fix provides durable protection.
