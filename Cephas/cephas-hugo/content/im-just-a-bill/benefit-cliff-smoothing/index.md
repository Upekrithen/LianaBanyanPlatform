# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Benefit Cliff Smoothing
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal benefit-cliff smoothing legislation in the 119th Congress addressing the documented "cliff effects" where modest income increases cause disproportionate benefit losses (SNAP, Medicaid, ACA subsidies, childcare assistance).

## What does it actually do?
- Phase-out smoothing for SNAP, Medicaid, ACA subsidies
- Earned-income disregards expansion
- Cross-program coordination to prevent stacked-cliff effects
- State-flexibility grants to design smoothing systems

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes broadly across the cooperative-class member experience. Working cooperative-class members frequently hit benefit cliffs that punish modest income gains. Cliff smoothing aligns with cooperative-class wage-progression sustainability.
