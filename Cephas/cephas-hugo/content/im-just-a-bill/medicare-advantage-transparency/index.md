# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Medicare Advantage Transparency Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Medicare Advantage (MA) transparency legislation in the 119th Congress, addressing documented patterns of denial-of-care, prior-authorization friction, and provider-network adequacy gaps in MA plans.

## What does it actually do?
- **Prior authorization:** Caps prior-auth response times; requires denial reasons in plain language.
- **Network adequacy:** Strengthens enforcement of provider-network requirements.
- **Marketing transparency:** Limits misleading marketing practices for MA plans.
- **Star ratings:** Modifies the CMS Star Ratings methodology to weight beneficiary-experience metrics more heavily.
- **Risk adjustment:** Addresses upcoding documented in OIG investigations.

## Who votes when?
- Senate Finance + House Ways & Means + Energy & Commerce active consideration.

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
The cooperative does not endorse positions on Medicare structural reform. The Council exists to provide accurate plain-language explanation of what bills do.
