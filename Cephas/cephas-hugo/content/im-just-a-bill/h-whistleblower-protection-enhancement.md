### H. Whistleblower Protection Enhancement

**Policy Substance:**
Strengthens federal whistleblower protections through: expanded coverage (including contractors, grantees), anti-retaliation enforcement, reward programs, legal fee coverage, and protections for disclosures to Congress/inspectors general/media. Addresses implementation gaps in existing frameworks.

**Power-to-the-People Council Analysis:**
- **Empowerment Dimension:** Enables individual citizens (employees/contractors) to activate accountability mechanisms against institutional power
- **Council Integration:** Whistleblowers often reveal failures in organizational governance councils; protections enable internal correction mechanisms
- **Participation Impact:** Creates "participatory accountability" where institutional insiders can safely contribute to public oversight

**Federal-Congressional Body Cam Doctrine:**
- **Transparency Requirements:** Whistleblowers serve as human "body cameras" on institutional misconduct; protections determine whether footage sees light
- **Accountability Mechanism:** Complementary to formal oversight; provides non-hierarchical accountability channel
- **Implementation Visibility:** Metrics on whistleblower complaints filed, retaliation investigations, disclosure outcomes, organizational corrections implemented

**Listen Before Talk Application:**
- **Consultation Strength:** Builds on decades of whistleblower case law, inspector general recommendations, Government Accountability Project expertise
- **Stakeholder Engagement:** Input from whistleblowers who experienced retaliation informs protection gap identification
