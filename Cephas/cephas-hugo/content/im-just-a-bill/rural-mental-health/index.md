# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Rural Mental Health Access Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Mental health access legislation in the 119th Congress targeting rural and frontier communities where the documented mental-health-provider shortage is most severe.

## What does it actually do?
- **Telehealth expansion** — extends rural telehealth reimbursement parity
- **Provider recruitment** — National Health Service Corps loan-repayment expansion for rural mental-health providers
- **Crisis services** — funds rural 988 + Veterans Crisis Line capacity
- **Community Health Workers** — funding for non-clinical peer-support roles in rural communities
- **Loan repayment** — student-loan forgiveness for mental-health providers serving rural designations

## Who votes when?
- Senate HELP + House Energy and Commerce active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#5 The Family Table** rural family-care infrastructure, **#6 Tatiana Schlossberg Health Accords** (rural Galveston-pattern composition), and **#8 Defense Klaus** rural survivor-class crisis-response.
