# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Digital Services Tax
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal Digital Services Tax legislation in the 119th Congress, addressing tax-base erosion from large platform companies through revenue-based digital services tax similar to several European jurisdictions.

## What does it actually do?
- Revenue-based DST on large digital-services revenue
- Threshold provisions excluding small-and-medium platforms
- Cooperative-class platform exemption
- Coordination with OECD Pillar 1/Pillar 2 international tax framework

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
The cooperative-class platform exemption is structurally important.
