# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Save Local Journalism Act
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Local journalism support legislation in the 119th Congress addressing the documented collapse of local newsrooms (~1/3 of U.S. newspapers closed since 2005; ~200 million Americans live in news deserts).

## What does it actually do?
- **Local journalism tax credit** — for publishers employing local journalists
- **Subscriber credit** — for individual subscribers to local outlets
- **Antitrust safe harbor** — limited collective-bargaining authority for news publishers vs platform aggregators
- **Public-interest journalism grants** — direct funding for investigative and civic journalism
- **Library/educational use** — strengthens fair-use provisions for educational distribution

## Who votes when?
- House Ways & Means + Senate Finance + House/Senate Judiciary active consideration

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#15 Power-to-the-People Council** "I'm Just a Bill" pilot (Bounty Posters are themselves a form of cooperative-class civic journalism), **#13 JukeBox** (cooperative-class media infrastructure), and **PRESS Act** Bounty Poster.
