# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Veterans Homelessness Prevention
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Veterans homelessness prevention legislation in the 119th Congress, building on HUD-VASH (HUD Veterans Affairs Supportive Housing) and SSVF (Supportive Services for Veteran Families) programs.

## What does it actually do?
- HUD-VASH voucher expansion
- SSVF funding stabilization
- Veteran-specific rapid-rehousing capacity
- Prevention-side intervention for at-risk veterans
- Family-of-veteran inclusive provisions

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#1 Lets Make Dinner Homeless Help Solution**, **Veteran Crisis Line Expansion** Bounty Poster, **Veterans Auto-Education Improvement** Bounty Poster, and the cooperative's veteran-Crown Council anchors (Wahlberg, Cena, Sinise, Sam Elliott, Hanks, The Rock).
