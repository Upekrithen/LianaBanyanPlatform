# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Food Is Medicine
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Food Is Medicine legislation in the 119th Congress establishing federal Medicare/Medicaid coverage for medically-tailored meals, produce prescriptions, and nutrition counseling for diet-related chronic conditions.

## What does it actually do?
- Medicare coverage for medically-tailored meals (diabetes, heart failure, cancer treatment recovery)
- Produce-prescription program federal funding
- Medical nutrition therapy expansion
- Provider-credentialing standards
- Cooperative-class food-provider eligibility provisions

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Direct composing with **#1 Lets Make Dinner Freezer Host network** (medically-tailored meal capacity), **#6 Tatiana Schlossberg Health Accords**, and **#7 MSA** (Substitution-routing for medically-tailored meal access).
