# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Credit Union Modernization
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal Credit Union Act modernization in the 119th Congress addressing field-of-membership rules, capital requirements for cooperative-finance entities, and CDFI fund expansion.

## What does it actually do?
- Field-of-membership rule modernization
- Cooperative-finance entity capital requirements
- CDFI fund expansion (composes with Inclusiv)
- Member Business Lending caps adjustment
- Cooperative-class technology infrastructure provisions

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Direct composing with **#7 MSA** (Crown Cathie Mahon — Inclusiv President & CEO). Strengthens the credit-union infrastructure that MSA architecture depends on.
