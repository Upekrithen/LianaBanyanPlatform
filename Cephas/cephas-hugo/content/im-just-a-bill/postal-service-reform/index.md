# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Postal Service Reform Extensions
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Continued reform of the U.S. Postal Service in the 119th Congress, building on the 2022 Postal Service Reform Act. Provisions address service quality, financial sustainability, and rural-area delivery integrity.

## What does it actually do?
- **Service standards:** Codifies minimum delivery standards, especially in rural and last-mile areas.
- **Financial reform:** Continues prefunding-relief; adjusts pension and benefits accounting.
- **Postal Banking pilot:** Expands USPS pilot program for basic financial services in postal facilities.
- **Vehicle electrification:** Funds USPS fleet transition timeline.

## Who votes when?
- House Oversight + Senate HSGAC active consideration.

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
USPS rural delivery is critical infrastructure for **#1 Lets Make Dinner** Freezer Host distribution and **#2 Lets Get Groceries** local-supply-chain logistics. Postal Banking pilot composes with **#7 MSA** member financial-services architecture for the unbanked.
