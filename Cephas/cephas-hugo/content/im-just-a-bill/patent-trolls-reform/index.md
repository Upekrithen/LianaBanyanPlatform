# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Patent Troll Reform
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Patent troll (NPE) reform legislation in the 119th Congress addressing abusive patent-litigation patterns from non-practicing entities.

## What does it actually do?
- Heightened pleading standards for patent infringement claims
- Fee-shifting in frivolous-claim outcomes
- Customer-stay provisions
- IPR (Inter Partes Review) procedural reform

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **The 300** patent-pledge cascade (cooperative's defensive-only patent posture). Patent troll reform composes with the cooperative's structural commitment to never use patents against people building something good.
