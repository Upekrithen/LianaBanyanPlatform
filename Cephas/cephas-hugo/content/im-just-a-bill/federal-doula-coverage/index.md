# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Medicaid Doula Coverage
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal Medicaid coverage for doula services in the 119th Congress addressing documented maternal-mortality disparities, particularly among Black and Indigenous birthing people.

## What does it actually do?
- Federal Medicaid doula-coverage standards
- Provider-credentialing standards (cooperative-class eligible)
- State-implementation grants
- Tribal-doula provisions
- Composes with rural maternal health legislation

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#5 The Family Table** (perinatal-care Trust Graph), **#6 Tatiana Schlossberg Health Accords**, **#7 MSA**, and **Pregnant Workers Fairness Act** + **Universal Pre-K** Bounty Posters.
