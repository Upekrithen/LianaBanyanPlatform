# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — FAMILY Act (Paid Family and Medical Leave)
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
The FAMILY Act would establish a national paid family and medical leave insurance program — comparable to programs in California, New York, New Jersey, and most peer countries.

## What does it actually do?
- **12 weeks** of paid leave for new parents, serious illness (own or family member), or military caregiver needs.
- **Funded by** small payroll contributions (~0.2% from employees + 0.2% from employers).
- **Administered by** SSA's Office of Paid Family and Medical Leave.
- **Wage replacement:** Up to 66% of usual wages, capped.
- **Coverage:** All workers regardless of employer size.

## Who votes when?
- **Senate Finance + House Ways & Means:** Active consideration
- **Bipartisan negotiations:** Funding mechanism is the major obstacle
- **President:** Signature dependent on chamber action

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes directly with **#5 The Family Table** ($11T unpaid-care-work adversary framing) and **#1 Let's Make Dinner** (parental leave allows time for cooperative-food-network participation). Federal paid leave is the safety-net floor; cooperative mutual-aid is the structural complement.
