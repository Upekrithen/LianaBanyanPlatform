# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Federal Mileage Rate Reform
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Federal mileage rate reform legislation in the 119th Congress addressing the documented gap between IRS standard mileage rate and actual vehicle operating costs, particularly for charitable/medical/cooperative-class travel.

## What does it actually do?
- Aligns charitable mileage rate (currently $0.14/mi) with business rate
- Updates medical mileage rate for healthcare access travel
- Provisions for cooperative-class member travel (Freezer Host pickup, MSA participating-provider travel)
- Adjusts methodology for ongoing rate updates

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#1 Lets Make Dinner** (Freezer Host travel for cooperative-class meal access), **#7 MSA** (member travel to participating providers), and **#8 Defense Klaus** (survivor-class travel to safety services). The cooperative's mutual-aid pools currently absorb travel costs the cooperative could route through tax-advantaged member arrangements if rate-fix landed.
