# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Medicare Physician Payment Reform
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Medicare Physician Fee Schedule reform in the 119th Congress addressing the documented year-over-year physician payment reductions and primary-care valuation gaps.

## What does it actually do?
- Eliminates statutory physician fee-schedule reductions
- Inflation-adjustment for physician payments
- Primary-care valuation reform
- Rural and underserved area bonuses
- Composes with rural hospital legislation

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **#6 Tatiana Schlossberg Health Accords** Galveston Legal Framework + **#7 MSA** (cooperative-class primary care availability depends on physician-payment economics).
