# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — Rural Attorney Recruitment
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Rural Attorney Recruitment in the 119th Congress addressing documented "legal deserts" — rural counties with declining attorney populations and limited access to legal services.

## What does it actually do?
- Loan-forgiveness for attorneys serving designated rural counties
- Federal grants for state-bar rural-attorney programs
- Tribal Attorney recruitment provisions
- Rural-court technology infrastructure
- Composes with [State Bar Open Letter](../../glass-door/state-bar/) framework

## How does this affect cooperative-class families?
Composes with **LBCCL Healthcare** Galveston Legal Framework first-deployment + future LBCCL variant deployments in rural jurisdictions where attorney shortages limit cooperative-class legal-services accessibility.
