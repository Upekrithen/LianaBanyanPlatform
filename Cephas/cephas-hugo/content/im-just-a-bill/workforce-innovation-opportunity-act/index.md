# 👀 I'm Just a Bill — WIOA Reauthorization
## A Liana Banyan "I'm Just a Bill" Bounty Poster

## What is this bill?
Reauthorization of the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA) in the 119th Congress. WIOA is the major federal workforce-development law funding apprenticeships, job training, and dislocated-worker services through state and local workforce boards.

## What does it actually do?
- **Apprenticeship expansion** — funds Registered Apprenticeships and Apprenticeship Hubs
- **Workforce board governance** — modifies state/local workforce-board structure
- **Dislocated workers** — extends services for trade-affected and displaced workers
- **Youth services** — programs for 16-24 year-olds entering the workforce
- **Adult education** — basic-skills and English-language programs

## Who votes when?
- House Education and Workforce + Senate HELP active consideration; historically bipartisan
