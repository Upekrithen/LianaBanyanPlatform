# Blue Team — Defenders

The Blue Team are the defensive hardening arm of the LB Elves Guild. Their mission: take every verified Red Team find and author a canonical defense — closing the vulnerability and extending the Slow Blade defense-canonical-list.

Every verified Blue Team harden:
- Generates a permanent `ip_ledger_stamp` (stamp_class: `blue_team_harden`)
- Pays Marks per the win-class multiplier matrix (Tier 4–5, 2.5×–3.0×)
- Composes with the Slow Blade defense stack — Blue Team hardens populate Slow Blade's defense-canonical-list
- Can be initiated as a cross-team challenge on any verified Red Team find

See the full scoreboard at `/helm/red-blue-leaderboard`.
