# Member Competition Record

Each LB Elves Guild member has a permanent, publicly-viewable competition record:

- **Wins**: verified finds (Red Team) or verified hardens (Blue Team)
- **Marks**: cumulative Marks earned via per-win-class multiplier matrix
- **IP Ledger stamps**: permanent attribution records in the Public IP Ledger, including first-finder / first-hardener markers
- **Event history**: full timeline of submitted, claimed, verified, IP-stamped, and marks-paid events

Member records are publicly visible — zero hidden information, per the cross-team transparency canon.

See the live platform surface at `/helm/red-blue-leaderboard/member/:member_id`.
