# Red Team — Finders

The Red Team are the adversarial arm of the LB Elves Guild. Their mission: find bypass paths, vulnerabilities, and exploits in the platform's cryptographic primitives, substrate layer, and AI-agent canon.

Every verified Red Team find:
- Generates a permanent `ip_ledger_stamp` (stamp_class: `red_team_find`)
- Pays Marks per the win-class multiplier matrix (Tier 4–5, 2.5×–3.0×)
- Becomes a cross-team challenge opportunity for Blue Team to claim a corresponding harden
