# Contributor License Agreement

The Liana Banyan Platform is built by and for Workers, Builders, and Creators.

To accept contributions while protecting all parties, we use a standard Contributor License Agreement (CLA).

## Sign the CLA

[Sign at cla-assistant.io →](https://cla-assistant.io/LianaBanyanCorporation/LianaBanyanPlatform)

## Why a CLA?

The CLA ensures that:
- You retain copyright in your work
- The cooperative platform can legally use your contribution
- Cooperative members receive attribution rights and economic participation

## Cooperative Member Protections

Workers, Builders, and Creators who are platform members receive additional protections under the Cooperative Membership Agreement: attribution rights, economic participation (83.3% goes to you — because We Are Those Workers / Builders / Creators), and IP transparency.
