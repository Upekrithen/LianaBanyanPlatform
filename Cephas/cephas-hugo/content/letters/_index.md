---

## The Shield Protocol

Some letters below are covered by the Shield. A shielded letter has been received by the system but not yet publicly released by the Founder. When the Founder ratifies a shielded letter, the Shield lifts and the full text becomes visible. Shielded letters do not appear in this listing.

If you access a shielded letter directly (by URL), you will see a "Letter Pending Ratification" notice in place of the content.

## Voter-Also-Receives

When you vote for a letter on the Pedestal, you become a recipient -- you join the network this letter reaches. Your vote carries it one step further, and you are in the chain of people it touches. This is the six-degrees engine: every vote extends the letter's reach by one real person.

*(Voting is a platform-side feature. The Pedestal Vote section on each letter page is a UI placeholder -- join to participate.)*
