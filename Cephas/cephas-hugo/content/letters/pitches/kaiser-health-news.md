**The Economic Lock:**

Unlike discount cards or PBM negotiations, the platform margin is fixed in the operating agreement. It can't be changed without dissolving the corporation. This prevents the drift toward extraction that's plagued other healthcare intermediaries.

**The Model Comparison:**

| Model | Who Captures Value |
