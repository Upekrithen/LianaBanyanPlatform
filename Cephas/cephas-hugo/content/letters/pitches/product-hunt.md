**Product Name:** Liana Banyan

**Tagline:** The platform that can't enshittify. Creators keep 83.3%. Economics locked.

---

**Description:**

Liana Banyan is a cooperative commerce platform where the economics are locked in the operating agreement—not changeable by executives, not erodable by investor pressure.

**Why we're different:**

**Margin locked at Cost + 20%** — It's in the operating agreement. Changing it would require dissolving the company.

**Creators keep 83.3%** — On a $500 transaction, creators get $416.67.

**$5/year membership** — Low barrier, real ownership.

**Fractional ownership** — Earn medallions through participation, not speculation.

**What you can do:**

- **Sell products** with 83.3% retention
- **Share meals** through Let's Make Dinner
- **Access prescriptions** through LifeLine Medications
- **Get microloans** through Village Savings & Loans
- **License music** fairly through The JukeBox
- And 9 more initiatives

**The story:**

15 years in development. 150+ innovations. 470+ patent claims. Built by a 52-year-old Army veteran who's invested $5K/year from a $30K income because he couldn't find a platform that didn't extract.

The math is published. Scrutiny is invited.
