Dear OpenWarHex Team,

You chose to make your work free. That's a statement.

Your open-source ethos + our patent umbrella = protection without restriction.

Here's the deal: Your designs. Our manufacturing network. Shared revenue. You keep your GitHub. You keep your community. You get access to infrastructure that can actually pay creators fairly.
