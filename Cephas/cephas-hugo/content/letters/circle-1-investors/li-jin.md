**The Architecture**

The platform margin is locked at **Cost + 20%**.

Creators keep **83.3%** of every transaction. This cannot change through:
- Acquisition
- Leadership change
- Investor pressure (no VC funding)
- Market power growth

The operating agreement makes extraction structurally impossible. If someone buys Liana Banyan, they buy the operating agreement too. The margin is architecture, not policy.
