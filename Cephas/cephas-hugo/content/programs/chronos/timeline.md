## Rope of History — Knots at Events

The platform's history is best visualized as a **rope** along which **knots** are tied at each substrate event: every Pheromone fired, every Eblet minted, every canon ratified, every innovation moment captured. Knot spacing reflects innovation density — sparse for steady incremental work, **tight clusters** for high-density sessions where a single prompt thought cascades into many components. See the [Rope of History page](/programs/chronos/rope-of-history/) for the BP053→BP058 arc knot cluster.

Members who discover historical artifacts — documents, dates, decisions — not yet in the substrate may submit them for Chronos ingest.

1. Canonical reference (or create one)

4. Two Member witnesses (or one CANON document reference)

Accepted contributions earn Marks and a substrate receipt.
