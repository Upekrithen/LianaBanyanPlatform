# Join Bruck'lyn

## Find Your Node

Search for an existing Bruck'lyn node in your neighborhood. If one exists, joining is simple: your Liana Banyan Membership ($5/year) gives you automatic standing in your local node.

## Start a Node

If no node exists in your neighborhood, you can start one.

**Requirements:**
- You must be a current Liana Banyan Member
- You need at least 4 other Members to join you (5 total to activate)
- You need a neighborhood anchor (a geographic identifier)

**What you get:**
- A local cooperative node registered to your neighborhood
- Access to the full Bruck'lyn stack (commerce, mutual aid, governance)
- Your neighborhood's data stays with your neighborhood
- Node Founder status (logged to substrate, canonical)

## The First 90 Days

Bruck'lyn nodes that complete the 90-day activation sprint receive:
- **Founding Node recognition** — canonical record in the Liana Banyan substrate
- **Bonus Marks** — cooperative currency distributed to all founding Members
- **Pattern publication** — your node's innovations documented and shared

## What You Commit To

Starting a Bruck'lyn node means:
- Holding your first local Pnyx within 30 days
- Logging at least 3 cooperative transactions in the first 90 days
- Recruiting at least 2 new Members in the first 90 days

These are minimums. Every node that hits them survives. Most do more.
