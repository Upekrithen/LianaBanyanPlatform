# Join Pnyx

## Who Can Participate

Any current Liana Banyan Member may participate in the Pnyx. Membership is $5/year — the most affordable governance stake in any cooperative on earth.

## Your First Deliberation

When you join the Pnyx, you receive:

1. **Standing** — your voice is recognized in all deliberations
2. **Vote credential** — one vote per active Membership year
3. **Proposal right** — you may submit one Proposal per 30-day period
4. **Receipt access** — you may request canonical receipts for any decision

## How to Engage

### Read First

Browse open Proposals and active deliberations. Read the canonical record of past decisions. Understand the platform's governance history before you vote.

### Respond

Add your voice to deliberations that matter to you. Responses are logged with your Member credential (anonymous to other Members, visible to you in your receipt).

### Propose

When you see a gap, a problem, or an opportunity the platform hasn't addressed — submit a Proposal. Good Proposals are specific, practical, and cross-referenced to existing initiatives.

### Vote

Every active deliberation has a voting window. Cast your vote. Request your receipt. Know your voice was counted.

## The Cooperative Compact

By participating in the Pnyx, you affirm:

- You are a current Liana Banyan Member
- You will engage in good faith
- You accept that decisions made by the cooperative are binding on the platform
- You understand that the Pnyx is a governance body, not a comment section
