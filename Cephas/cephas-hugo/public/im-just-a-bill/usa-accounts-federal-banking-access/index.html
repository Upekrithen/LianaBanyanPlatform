5. USA ACCOUNTS (Federal Banking Access)

Legislative Status 119th Congress

  • Postal Banking Revival Proposals: Multiple bills proposing USPS basic banking services
  • FedAccount Concepts: Federal Reserve direct consumer accounts (explored in various progressive bills)
  • Treasury Direct Deposit Infrastructure: Expansion proposals for unbanked populations

Cooperative Finance Trio Application

MSA (#7) Integration: USA Accounts as universal baseline MSA:

  • No-minimum, no-fee accounts: Eliminates exclusionary MSA terms
  • Government as MSA counterparty: Federal obligation replaces commercial discretion
  • Platform for cooperative connection: USA Accounts as rails connecting to community cooperatives

VSL (#10) Sequencing:

Government Benefit Issuance → USA Account Receipt →
(Option A) Fiat Extraction → Check Cashing/Predatory Services
(Option B) Cooperative Transfer → Local Currency Conversion →
Community Economic Circulation

VSL analysis shows USA Accounts enabling cooperative off-ramping from fiat dependence by providing secure initial deposit infrastructure.

LMB (#11) Bread-Making:

  • USA Accounts = Community grain silo: Basic storage infrastructure everyone can access
  • Without USA Accounts: Predatory storage fees (check cashing) for those unable to afford private silos (bank accounts)
  • Cooperative integration: Grain silo → cooperative mill → community bakery (USA Account → Credit Union → Community Development)

Blood Rule Compliance

Apparent Violation: USA Accounts directly distribute fiat to individuals—seemingly maximal Blood Rule violation.

Reconciliation Through Infrastructure Interpretation: USA Accounts comply if designed as public infrastructure rather than fiat proliferation:

  1. Accounts as public utilities: Like roads (carrying value) rather than gas stations (dispensing fiat)
  2. Mandatory cooperative integration: USA Accounts require linked local currency account at credit union or CDFI
  3. Automatic substitution: Federal benefits auto-split: X% retained as fiat safety reserve, Y% automatically converted to community currency upon deposit

Modified USA Account Architecture:

  • Federal deposits: Enter in fiat but immediately trigger substitution cascade
  • Member control: Choose substitution ratios (100% fiat for emergency, 0% fiat for full cooperative participation)
  • Conversion boundary: USA Account itself doesn’t circulate fiat; acts as holding/switching facility directing funds to fiat or cooperative channels

Blood Rule Interpretation: If USA Accounts reduce net fiat circulation by providing bridges to cooperative alternatives, they align with Blood Rule intent—reducing dependence on extractive fiat systems even while technically operating in fiat.
