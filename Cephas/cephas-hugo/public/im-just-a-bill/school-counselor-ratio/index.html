I'm Just a Bill: School Counselor Ratio Act

May 12, 2026

👀 I’m Just a Bill — School Counselor Ratio

A Liana Banyan “I’m Just a Bill” Bounty Poster

What is this bill?

Federal funding to lower the student-to-school-counselor ratio (currently ~400:1 average vs ASCA-recommended 250:1).

What does it actually do?

  • Title I funding stream for counselor hiring
  • Title IV Mental-Health-Services funding expansion
  • Tribal/rural priority allocations
  • Multi-tier-system-of-supports framework integration

How does this affect cooperative-class families?

Composes with Save-the-World Paper 12 (Universal Childhood), #5 The Family Table, and Rural Mental Health Access Act Bounty Poster.
