Rope of History — Knots at Events#

The platform’s history is best visualized as a rope along which knots are tied at each substrate event: every Pheromone fired, every Eblet minted, every canon ratified, every innovation moment captured. Contributing to the Timeline#

Members who discover historical artifacts — documents, dates, decisions — not yet in the substrate may submit them for Chronos ingest.

Requirements for a valid contribution:

  1. Canonical reference (or create one)
  2. Timestamp (exact or estimated, clearly labeled)
  3. Source (where the artifact comes from)
  4. Two Member witnesses (or one CANON document reference)

Accepted contributions earn Marks and a substrate receipt.
