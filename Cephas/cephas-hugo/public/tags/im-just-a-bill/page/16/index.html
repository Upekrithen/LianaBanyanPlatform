The 2025 reauthorization in the 119th Congress continues the program and modifies specific provisions. What does it actually do? In plain language: Reauthorizes federal grant programs supporting domestic-violence shelters, rape crisis centers, and survivor-services nonprofits. Maintains the Office on Violence Against Women within DOJ and its grant-making functions. Updates specific provisions including survivor housing protections, economic-empowerment supports, and Tribal court jurisdiction.
