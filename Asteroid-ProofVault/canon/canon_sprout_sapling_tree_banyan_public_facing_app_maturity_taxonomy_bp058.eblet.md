## §1 CANONICAL DEFINITION

**Sprout / Sapling / Tree / Banyan** = the 4-tier public-facing maturity taxonomy for Liana Banyan platform products, features, and initiatives.

This taxonomy communicates to the public (members, cooperators, the press, investors) where each Initiative, spinout, or platform feature stands in its journey from idea to platform-class infrastructure. It is the public-facing language of cooperative-class product maturity.

**Disambiguation — two separate but composing botanical taxonomies exist:**

| Taxonomy | What It Describes | Stages |
|---|---|---|
| **Sprout/Sapling/Tree/Banyan (THIS CANON · BP058)** | Public-facing app/feature/initiative maturity | 4 tiers — product-development stage |
| **Sprouts→Seedlings→Saplings→Lianas→Banyans (BP052)** | Youth-to-mature cooperative-class member lifecycle | 5 stages — member age/business development |

These share botanical language deliberately (both express cooperative-class growth via the parent brand's root metaphor) but are DISTINCT taxonomies with different subjects:
- BP058 taxonomy = **what the product/initiative is**
- BP052 taxonomy = **who the member is and where they are in their life-stage**

They compose: a Sprout (BP058 product tier) is typically where Sprouts and Seedlings (BP052 member stages) first access a new Initiative.

## §2 MOTIVATION / FOUNDER VOICE

The botanical metaphor carries the cooperative-class growth narrative naturally. A Sprout breaks ground: it is small, promising, needs tending. A Sapling stands on its own: it has roots, is growing but still needs nurturing. A Tree is mature: it produces, shelters, is trusted by the community. A Banyan is infrastructure: it grows new roots from its own branches, becomes the ground for the next generation of Sprouts.

The Banyan analogy is especially precise: a Banyan tree grows new aerial roots from its branches, which descend to the ground and become new trunks. A Banyan-tier feature on the Liana Banyan platform has become infrastructure that other features depend on — it literally "grows new Sprouts of its own."

This taxonomy exists so that:
1. **Members know what they are joining** — they see immediately whether an Initiative is early ("Pick this up") or mature ("Producing value now")
2. **Builders know where to focus** — Sprout-tier Initiatives need Staffing; Tree-tier Initiatives need Scale
3. **Creators know the opportunity** — Sapling-tier Initiatives have talent assembled and are ready for creative output
4. **The public sees a journey, not just a product** — the cooperative model is visible in the progression

## §3 MECHANISM

### §3.1 The 4-Tier Ladder (Canonical Specification)

#### SPROUT
- **Gate:** Idea + business plan published and open for member staffing
- **Staffing status:** Positions defined; no members assigned yet (or early experimental staffing underway)
- **Funding status:** Not yet funded by Crown or LB direct hire
- **Public message:** "Pick this up · build it · own the position"
- **Member opportunity:** Assign Marks-backed positions; claim founding positions; be the worker who picks the rotting-orchard fruit
- **What it means:** The cooperative has done the work of creating the business plan and making it available. The Sprout is real — not just an idea in someone's head. It's ground-level emergence.
- **Note:** The 16 Initiatives + 19 spinouts + Cold Start businesses are each introduced at Sprout tier. Sprout is the default for "just announced" cooperative ventures.

#### SAPLING
- **Gate:** Staffed (positions filled by Members via Marks-assignment) AND Funded (Crown or LB direct hire)
- **Staffing status:** Core positions occupied by cooperative-class members
- **Funding status:** Crown has pledged funding OR LB direct-hire funding active
- **Public message:** "Now in motion · talent assembled · resources backed"
- **Member opportunity:** Join as part of the growing team; contribute to something building real momentum
- **What it means:** The cooperative-class staffing + funding machinery worked. Real people are doing real work. The Sapling has roots and is growing.

#### TREE
- **Gate:** Operational + revenue-generating + Cost+20% pricing discipline live
- **Staffing status:** Fully operational team
- **Revenue status:** Active revenue; Cost+20% pricing applied; Workers/Builders/Creators keep 83.3% (the tri-term: We Are Those Workers/Builders/Creators)
- **Public message:** "Cooperative-class business producing value · Members keep [tri-term]"
- **Member opportunity:** Join a mature, revenue-generating cooperative-class operation; stable contribution paths; Eagle's Nest portfolio entries accumulating
- **What it means:** The tree produces fruit. It provides shelter. The cooperative model is visible in the economics (83.3% to Workers/Builders/Creators, Cost+20% margin).

#### BANYAN
- **Gate:** Network-effect scale + spawns new Sprouts of its own (recursive cooperative-class growth)
- **Platform-class criteria:** Other Initiatives or features depend on this one as infrastructure
- **Self-propagation:** This Banyan-tier component seeds new Sprouts in adjacent domains
- **Public message:** "Self-propagating · seeds the next Sprouts"
- **What it means:** A Banyan tree's roots descend from its own branches and become new trunks. A Banyan-tier platform component IS infrastructure. The Eblet substrate is Banyan-class. Mnemosyne is on the path to Banyan. When an Initiative achieves Banyan, it is no longer just a product — it is part of the cooperative-class platform's foundational layer.

### §3.2 Applies to Features and Initiatives, Not the Platform

This taxonomy applies to FEATURES and INITIATIVES individually — not to the entire Liana Banyan platform.

| Subject | Tier Assignment |
|---|---|
| The Liana Banyan platform itself | Banyan-class (the platform IS the infrastructure) |
| The Eblet substrate / Mnemosyne | Banyan-class (platform-infrastructure tier) |
| Let's Make Dinner Initiative #1 at launch | Sprout |
| Let's Make Dinner after first staffing + Crown funding | Sapling |
| Let's Make Dinner operational + revenue live | Tree |
| Let's Make Dinner spawning home-chef networks that seed new culinary Sprouts | Banyan |

### §3.3 Composability with Project SEED (Internal Development Taxonomy)

Project SEED is the internal 6-stage development maturity ladder (not public-facing). Sprout/Sapling/Tree/Banyan is the PUBLIC-FACING taxonomy. They compose but are distinct:

| Layer | Audience | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Project SEED (6-stage internal)** | Builders, Knight, Bishop, Rook, Pawn — internal cooperative team | Engineering/development readiness |
| **Sprout/Sapling/Tree/Banyan (4-tier public)** | Members, press, public, potential Crowns | Community-facing maturity signal |

A feature may be at Project SEED Stage 4 internally but still display as Sapling externally (stable for community access but not yet full revenue generation). The public taxonomy is the member-facing signal; the internal taxonomy is the builder-facing signal.

## §5 CONSTRAINTS + ANTI-PATTERNS

**Sprout/Sapling/Tree/Banyan is NOT:**
- The same as the BP052 youth-entrepreneur progression (that is member life-stage; this is product maturity)
- A linear requirement (initiatives may stay at a tier, may regress if conditions change, may skip tiers in accelerated scenarios)
- Applicable to the overall platform (the platform itself is Banyan-class by definition; this taxonomy applies to features and initiatives within it)
- A judgment of value (a Sprout is not worth less than a Tree; it is earlier in maturity — cooperative-class ethics: all stages are honored, all workers credited)

**Anti-patterns:**
- Describing the Liana Banyan platform itself as a Sprout or Sapling — the platform is Banyan; individual components within it have their own tier
- Conflating this taxonomy with the BP052 youth-entrepreneur stages — different subjects, don't merge
- Treating Banyan tier as a "win state" — Banyan means the feature IS now infrastructure; it brings new responsibilities (dependencies, stability requirements) alongside the recognition
- Advancing an Initiative to Sapling without confirmed staffing AND funding — the gate requires both; confirmed-staffing-only does not make a Sapling

## §6 EXAMPLES

**Example 1 — Let's Get Groceries at Launch (Sprout)**
At cooperative launch, Let's Get Groceries Initiative #2 is announced as Sprout-tier. Its business plan is published. Positions are open for Marks-assignment. The public message: "Pick this up · build it · own the position." Early-adopter Workers/Builders/Creators assign Marks to founding positions. The Sprout is visible, real, ready to be staffed.

**Example 2 — Rally Group reaches Sapling (Sapling)**
Rally Group Initiative #9 (Crown: Kimberly A. Williams) receives staffing: 8 cooperative members assign Marks-backed positions to core roles. Crown Williams confirms Crown funding for the first operational quarter. Rally Group advances to Sapling. Public message updates: "Now in motion · talent assembled · resources backed." The community can see real people are doing real work.

**Example 3 — JukeBox achieves Tree (Tree)**
JukeBox Initiative #13 reaches operational status: artists are earning, Cost+20% pricing is live, the first quarterly revenue pass shows Workers/Builders/Creators keeping 83.3%. Public message: "Cooperative-class business producing value · Members keep what they earn." Eagle's Nest portfolio entries are accumulating for all contributors since day 1 (No Effort Is Wasted doctrine enforced).

**Example 4 — Mnemosyne substrate achieves Banyan (Banyan)**
Mnemosyne reaches a state where 6 Initiatives depend on it as infrastructure: their member-facing features run through Mnemosyne's Eblet substrate, Helm views, and P2P distribution. Mnemosyne spawns 3 new Sprout-tier capabilities from its own branch architecture. Mnemosyne is Banyan-class. It is now the ground that other features grow from.

**Example 5 — Sprout that never reaches Sapling**
A Cold Start business enters at Sprout tier. After 90 days, no Crown funding materializes and staffing is partial. The Sprout remains a Sprout. Per "No Effort Is Wasted" doctrine: every Worker who assigned Marks and did work during the Sprout phase has permanent IP Ledger attribution. Their contribution is not erased by the Sprout's failure to advance. The business plan remains available for future staffing.
